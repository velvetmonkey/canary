# Section 57 — PDS Must Contain All Material Information

## Obligation

**Who:** Issuers of financial products making regulated offers
**What:** The issuer must prepare a Product Disclosure Statement (PDS) that contains all information required by regulations, all material information not in the PDS must appear in the register entry, and the PDS must be dated and comply with all content requirements. This is the cornerstone pre-contractual disclosure obligation for any regulated offer of financial products in New Zealand.
**Where:** In the PDS lodged with the Registrar and in the register entry for the regulated offer
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> An issuer that prepares, or is required to prepare, a PDS must ensure that, as at the date on which the PDS is lodged with the Registrar,— (a) the PDS— (i) contains all of the information that it is required to contain by the regulations.
