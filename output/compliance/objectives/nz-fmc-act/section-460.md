# Section 460 — Financial statements must be prepared within 4 months

## Obligation

**Who:** Every FMC reporting entity
**What:** FMC reporting entities must prepare financial statements complying with generally accepted accounting practice within 4 months after their balance date. Statements must be signed by two directors. Knowing failure to comply with applicable financial reporting standards is a criminal offence carrying up to 5 years imprisonment or NZD 2.5 million fine.
**Where:** Completed as formal signed documents; subsequently lodged with the Registrar
**Deadline:** Within 4 months after balance date
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Every FMC reporting entity must ensure that, within 4 months after the balance date of the entity, financial statements that comply with generally accepted accounting practice are— (a) completed in relation to the entity and that balance date; and (b) dated and signed on behalf of the entity by 2 directors of the entity or, if the entity has only 1 director, by that director.
