# Section 461D — Financial statements must be audited by qualified FMC auditor

## Obligation

**Who:** Every FMC reporting entity
**What:** All financial statements or group financial statements required to be prepared under [[Part 7]] must be audited by a qualified FMC auditor (a licensed auditor or registered audit firm). Directors, officers, and employees of the entity are disqualified from acting as auditor. Contravention gives rise to civil liability including pecuniary penalties up to NZD 5 million.
**Where:** External audit engagement; auditor's report must accompany financial statements lodged with the Registrar
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Every FMC reporting entity must ensure that the financial statements or group financial statements that are required to be prepared under this subpart are audited by a qualified FMC auditor.
