# Section 241 — Prohibition on Insider Trading in Quoted Financial Products

## Obligation

**Who:** Any information insider of a listed issuer or in relation to quoted derivatives
**What:** An information insider must not trade (acquire or dispose of) quoted financial products of the listed issuer while in possession of material information that is not generally available to the market. Criminal liability applies where the person knowingly trades with inside information.
**Where:** In any trading of quoted financial products on licensed markets
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> An information insider of a listed issuer must not trade quoted financial products of the listed issuer.
