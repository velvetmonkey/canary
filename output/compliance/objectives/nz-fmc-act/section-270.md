# Section 270 — Listed Issuers Must Comply with Continuous Disclosure Obligations

## Obligation

**Who:** Listed issuers party to a listing agreement with a licensed market operator
**What:** A listed issuer must notify material information that is not generally available to the market in accordance with the continuous disclosure provisions of the listing rules for the relevant licensed market. This is the ongoing market disclosure obligation equivalent to the EU's MAR [[Article 17]].
**Where:** Via the licensed market operator's notification system, in accordance with listing rules
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> A listed issuer must notify information in accordance with the continuous disclosure provisions of the listing rules for the licensed market if— (a) the listed issuer is a party to a listing agreement with the licensed market operator; and (b) the listed issuer has information that those continuous disclosure provisions require it to notify; and (c) the information is material information that is not generally available to the market.
