# Section 461ZG — Criminal offence for knowingly non-compliant climate statements

## Obligation

**Who:** A climate reporting entity and every director of the entity
**What:** It is a criminal offence for a climate reporting entity and its directors to knowingly lodge climate statements that fail to comply with an applicable climate standard. Individuals face up to 5 years imprisonment or a NZD 500,000 fine; entities face fines up to NZD 2.5 million. This creates strong personal liability for directors of fund managers operating as climate reporting entities.
**Where:** in lodged climate statements
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> A climate reporting entity and every director of the entity commit an offence if—
(a)
any of the following fail to comply with an applicable climate standard:
(i)
the climate statements of the entity prepared under section 461Z
