# Section 156 — Scheme Property Must Be Held by Independent Custodian

## Obligation

**Who:** Supervisor of a registered managed investment scheme; manager where there is no supervisor
**What:** The supervisor must hold scheme property or contract its holding to an independent external custodian that meets prescribed requirements. The custodian must be a body corporate not associated with the manager, ensuring segregation of assets from the manager's own assets. This is the structural investor protection equivalent to the [[AIFMD]] depositary requirement.
**Where:** In the custody and safekeeping arrangements for scheme property
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The supervisor of a registered scheme (A) must hold the scheme property or, if authorised by the governing document, contract the holding of the scheme property to another person (B) who meets the external custodianship requirements.
