# Section 143 — Manager's General Duties: Honesty, Best Interests, and Equitable Treatment

## Obligation

**Who:** Manager of a registered managed investment scheme
**What:** The manager must act honestly and in good faith, act in the best interests of scheme participants and treat them equitably, and must not use information acquired as manager to gain an improper advantage or cause detriment to participants. This is the primary fiduciary duty imposed on fund managers in New Zealand.
**Where:** In all management and administration activities of the registered scheme
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> A manager of a registered scheme must— (a) act honestly and in good faith in acting as a manager; and (b) in exercising any powers or performing any duties as a manager,— (i) act in the best interests of the scheme participants; and (ii) treat the scheme participants equitably.
