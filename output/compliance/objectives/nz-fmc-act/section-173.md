# Section 173 — General Prohibition on Related Party Transactions in Registered Schemes

## Obligation

**Who:** Manager of a registered scheme (and any investment manager, administration manager, or delegate)
**What:** The manager must not enter into any transaction that provides a related party benefit (a benefit given out of scheme property or creating exposure to loss for scheme property, given to or received by a related party) unless the supervisor consents (certifying the transaction is in participants' best interests or approved by special resolution) or a permitted exception applies. This is the conflicts-of-interest control equivalent to [[AIFMD]] [[Article 14]].
**Where:** In all investment and operational transactions involving scheme property
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> A manager of a registered scheme (and any investment manager, administration manager, or other person to whom the manager has contracted out some or all of its functions as a manager) must not enter into a transaction that provides for a related party benefit to be given.
