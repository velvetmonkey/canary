# Section 431ZC — Client Money Must Be Held in Separate Trust Account

## Obligation

**Who:** Providers of regulated client money or property services
**What:** A provider who receives client money or client property must hold it on trust for the client and must ensure client money is paid promptly into a separate trust account at a bank in New Zealand. Client money and property must be kept separate from the provider's own assets, protecting clients in the event of provider insolvency.
**Where:** In the provider's trust account operations and record-keeping
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> A provider who receives client money or client property, as part of a client money or property service,— (a) must hold the client money or client property, or ensure the client money or client property is held, on trust for the client; and (b) must ensure that the client money is paid promptly into a bank in New Zealand.
