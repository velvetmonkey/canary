# Section 265 — Prohibition on Creating False or Misleading Appearance of Trading

## Obligation

**Who:** Any person dealing in quoted financial products
**What:** A person must not act or omit to act in a way that creates, or is likely to create, a false or misleading appearance with respect to the extent of active trading in, or the supply, demand, price, or value of, quoted financial products. This is the market manipulation prohibition equivalent to MAR [[Article 12]].
**Where:** In any trading activity on licensed financial product markets
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> A person must not do, or omit to do, anything if— (a) the act or omission will have, or is likely to have, the effect of creating, or causing the creation of, a false or misleading appearance— (i) with respect to the extent of active trading in quoted financial products; or (ii) with respect to the supply of, demand for, price for trading in, or value of those financial products.
