# Part 3 – Trading Schemes (Schedule 4 – Powers to Require Information) — Trading scheme participants must provide information on request

## Obligation

**Who:** Persons subject to trading scheme information requirements (Schedule 4)
**What:** Entities subject to a trading scheme must provide information required by the scheme administrator or national authority. Failure to provide required information constitutes a criminal offence. This includes information needed to establish or operate the scheme.
**Where:** Submissions to the trading scheme administrator; regulatory filings
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> for the purposes of enabling a trading scheme to be established.
