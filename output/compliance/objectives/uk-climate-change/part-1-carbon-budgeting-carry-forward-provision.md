# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting (Carry-forward provision) — Account for carbon budget carry-forward amounts

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State; carbon budget administrators and regulated participants
**What:** When emissions in a budgetary period are below the carbon budget, the surplus may be carried forward, increasing the next period's budget by that amount. Participants and administrators must accurately track and account for these adjustments in their carbon accounting records.
**Where:** In carbon accounting records and annual statements of UK emissions
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The amount of the carbon budget for the next budgetary period is increased by the amount carried forward.
