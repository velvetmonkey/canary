# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting (Carry-forward provision) — Carbon budget carry-forward increases next budgetary period amount

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State / relevant national authority
**What:** Where an amount is carried forward from one budgetary period to the next, the carbon budget for the next period is automatically increased by that carried-forward amount. Firms and authorities must account for this adjustment in their planning and reporting.
**Where:** In carbon accounting records and budgetary period calculations
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The amount of the carbon budget for the next budgetary period is increased by the amount carried forward.
