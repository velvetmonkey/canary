# Part 3 – Trading Schemes (Schedule 2, Part 3 – Administration and Enforcement) — Trading scheme participants must comply with scheme regulations

## Obligation

**Who:** Participants in trading schemes (persons to whom the scheme applies by virtue of regulations under paragraph 4 or 15 of Schedule 2)
**What:** Entities designated as participants in a trading scheme must comply with all scheme regulations, including activity limits, allowance surrender, and record-keeping requirements. Non-compliance constitutes a criminal offence punishable by imprisonment.
**Where:** Within the trading scheme regulatory framework; enforcement by the scheme administrator
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> must provide that, where the offence is committed before that date, it is punishable with imprisonment for a term not exceeding six months.
