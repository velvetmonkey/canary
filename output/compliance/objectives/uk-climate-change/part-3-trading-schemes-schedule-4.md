# Part 3 – Trading Schemes (Schedule 4) — Trading scheme participants must provide information when required

## Obligation

**Who:** Persons subject to trading scheme information requirements ([[Schedule 4]])
**What:** Persons subject to a trading scheme may be required to provide information to the administrator. Failure to comply constitutes a criminal offence. Regulated entities must maintain records and respond to information requests.
**Where:** Information provided to the trading scheme administrator
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> commits an offence.
