# Part 1 – Annual Statement of UK Emissions (Section 16) — Annual statement of UK emissions must be published per gas per year

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State / reporting authorities
**What:** An annual statement of UK emissions must be produced, stating the total amount of each targeted greenhouse gas emitted for each year in a budgetary period. This forms the basis for determining whether carbon budgets have been met.
**Where:** Annual statement published under section 16
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> That is the total of the amounts (or adjusted amounts) stated under section 16 (annual statement of UK emissions) in respect of that gas for the years included in the period.
