# Part 2 – Committee on Climate Change (Functions) — National authorities may grant funds to the Committee on Climate Change

## Obligation

**Who:** National authorities (Secretary of State, Welsh Ministers, Scottish Ministers, Northern Ireland authorities)
**What:** National authorities are empowered — and expected — to make grants to the Committee on Climate Change in such amounts and subject to such conditions as they think fit, ensuring the Committee is adequately resourced to perform its advisory and scrutiny functions.
**Where:** In budgetary and governance arrangements for the Committee on Climate Change
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> A national authority may make grants to the Committee of such amount and subject to such conditions as the authority thinks fit.
