# Part 3 – Trading Schemes (Schedule 2) — Trading scheme participants must comply with scheme regulations

## Obligation

**Who:** Participants in trading schemes (persons to whom the scheme applies under [[Schedule 2]], paragraph 4 or 15)
**What:** Entities designated as participants in a greenhouse gas trading scheme must comply with the scheme's regulations, including activity limits, allowance surrenders, and reporting obligations set out in the scheme regulations.
**Where:** Within the trading scheme as established by regulations under [[Schedule 2]]
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> "participant", in relation to a trading scheme, means a person to whom the scheme applies by virtue of regulations under paragraph 4 or 15 of [[Schedule 2]];
