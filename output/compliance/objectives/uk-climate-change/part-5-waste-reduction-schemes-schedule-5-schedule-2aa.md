# Part 5 – Waste Reduction Schemes (Schedule 5, Schedule 2AA) — Waste reduction schemes must be revenue neutral

## Obligation

**Who:** Waste collection authorities in England operating waste reduction schemes
**What:** Any waste reduction scheme operated by a waste collection authority must comply with the requirement of revenue neutrality — charges collected must not generate a net financial surplus for the authority beyond scheme administration costs. Authorities must maintain a separate account for scheme revenues.
**Where:** Waste reduction scheme accounts and regulatory submissions to the Secretary of State
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> Requirement of revenue neutrality
