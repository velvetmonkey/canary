# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting — Secretary of State must prioritise UK domestic climate action

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State (and by extension regulated entities subject to carbon budgets)
**What:** When setting or implementing proposals and policies to meet carbon budgets, the Secretary of State must have regard to the need for UK domestic action on climate change. This shapes the policy environment within which all regulated entities must operate.
**Where:** In proposals and policies for meeting carbon budgets, published by government
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> the Secretary of State must have regard to the need for UK domestic action on climate change.
