# Part 4 – Reporting Authorities: Non-Devolved Functions — Reporting authorities must have regard to Welsh Ministers' guidance and Welsh climate report

## Obligation

**Who:** Reporting authorities exercising non-devolved functions
**What:** When preparing adaptation reports, reporting authorities must also have regard — so far as relevant — to any guidance issued by the Welsh Ministers and to the most recent report on climate change in Wales. This ensures cross-border coherence in adaptation reporting.
**Where:** In adaptation reports prepared by reporting authorities
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> it must also have regard, so far as relevant, to any guidance issued by the Welsh Ministers under section 66 and the most recent report under section 80 (report on climate change: Wales).
