# Part 4 – Reporting Authorities: Non-devolved Functions — Have regard to Welsh Ministers' guidance in adaptation reporting

## Obligation

**Who:** Reporting authorities exercising non-devolved functions in Wales
**What:** When preparing adaptation reports, reporting authorities must have regard, so far as relevant, to any guidance issued by the Welsh Ministers and the most recent Welsh climate change report. This ensures devolved climate considerations are integrated into UK-wide adaptation governance.
**Where:** Adaptation reports and programmes submitted by reporting authorities
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> it must also have regard, so far as relevant, to any guidance issued by the Welsh Ministers under section 66 and the most recent report under section 80 (report on climate change: Wales).
