# Part 3 – Trading Schemes (Schedule 2, Part 2) — Participate in schemes encouraging emissions reductions or removals

## Obligation

**Who:** Participants in trading schemes as designated by regulations under Schedule 2
**What:** Entities covered by schemes designed to encourage reductions in greenhouse gas emissions or removal of greenhouse gas from the atmosphere must comply with scheme obligations, including demonstrating qualifying activities and reporting reductions achieved.
**Where:** Within the trading scheme framework; scheme-specific compliance documentation
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Part 2 deals with schemes that operate by encouraging activities that consist of, or that cause or contribute, directly or indirectly, to reductions in greenhouse gas emissions or the removal of greenhouse gas from the atmosphere;
