# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting (Carry-back provision) — Carbon budget carry-back adjusts earlier and later period budgets

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State / relevant national authority
**What:** Where an amount is carried back from a later budgetary period to an earlier one, the later period's budget is reduced and the earlier period's budget is increased by the same amount. This must be reflected in all carbon accounting and compliance determinations.
**Where:** In carbon accounting records and budgetary period calculations
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The carbon budget for the later period is reduced, and that for the earlier period increased, by the amount carried back.
