# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting (Carry-back provision) — Account for carbon budget carry-back adjustments

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State; carbon budget administrators and regulated participants
**What:** Where a carry-back of emissions between budgetary periods is exercised, the carbon budget for the later period is reduced and that for the earlier period increased by the carried-back amount. Regulated entities must reflect these adjustments in their carbon accounting and compliance calculations.
**Where:** In carbon accounting records and periodic budget compliance assessments
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The carbon budget for the later period is reduced, and that for the earlier period increased, by the amount carried back.
