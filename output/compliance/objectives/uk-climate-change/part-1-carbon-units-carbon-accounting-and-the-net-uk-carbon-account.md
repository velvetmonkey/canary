# Part 1 – Carbon Units, Carbon Accounting and the Net UK Carbon Account — Comply with carbon accounting regulations for carbon units

## Obligation

**Who:** Regulated entities holding or trading carbon units; Secretary of State via regulations
**What:** Entities must comply with regulations governing the use of carbon units and carbon accounting as they feed into the net UK carbon account. Regulations may adapt existing schemes for these purposes, and entities must ensure their carbon unit holdings and transactions are accurately recorded and reported.
**Where:** Carbon unit registries; net UK carbon account; regulatory compliance records
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The regulations may, in particular, provide for an existing scheme to be adapted for these purposes.
