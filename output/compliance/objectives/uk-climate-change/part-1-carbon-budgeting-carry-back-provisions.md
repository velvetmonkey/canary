# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting (Carry-back provisions) — Carbon budget carry-back reduces later period and increases earlier period

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State / carbon accounting authorities
**What:** Where emissions are carried back from a later budgetary period to an earlier one, the later period's budget is reduced and the earlier period's budget is increased by the same amount. Regulated entities must account for these adjustments in their carbon reporting.
**Where:** In carbon accounting records and annual statements of UK emissions
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The carbon budget for the later period is reduced, and that for the earlier period increased, by the amount carried back.
