# Part 4 – National Reports and Programmes (Section addressing risks) — Prepare adaptation programmes addressing climate change risks

## Obligation

**Who:** Reporting authorities with non-devolved and devolved functions (public bodies, certain regulated entities)
**What:** Reporting authorities must prepare and publish adaptation programmes that address the risks identified in the most recent climate change risk assessment report under section 56. Programmes must be updated to reflect the latest risk evidence.
**Where:** National adaptation reports and programmes; published by reporting authorities
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> addressing the risks identified in the most recent report under section 56.
