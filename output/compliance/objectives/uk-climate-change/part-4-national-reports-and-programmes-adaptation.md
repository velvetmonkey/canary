# Part 4 – National Reports and Programmes (Adaptation) — Adaptation programmes must address climate change risk assessment findings

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State and national authorities responsible for adaptation programmes
**What:** National adaptation programmes must specifically address the risks identified in the most recent climate change risk assessment report (section 56). Regulated entities and reporting authorities must align their adaptation plans with the latest risk evidence.
**Where:** National adaptation programmes and reporting authority submissions
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> addressing the risks identified in the most recent report under section 56.
