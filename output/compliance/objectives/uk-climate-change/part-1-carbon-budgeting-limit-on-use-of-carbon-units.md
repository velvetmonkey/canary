# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting (Limit on use of carbon units) — Limit on use of carbon units in carbon accounting

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and entities subject to UK carbon accounting regulations
**What:** Carbon units may only be used within the limits prescribed by regulations. Entities must ensure that any carbon units used for compliance purposes fall within the permitted scope and do not exceed the statutory cap, as set out in carbon accounting regulations.
**Where:** In carbon accounting records and regulatory submissions
**Materiality:** high
