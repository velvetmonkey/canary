# Part 4 – Reporting Authorities: Devolved Welsh Functions (Section 66) — Welsh reporting authorities must have regard to Welsh Ministers' guidance

## Obligation

**Who:** Reporting authorities with devolved Welsh functions
**What:** When preparing adaptation reports covering devolved Welsh functions, reporting authorities must have regard to any guidance issued by the Welsh Ministers and to the most recent Welsh climate change report under section 80. This creates a parallel compliance track for Welsh-function entities.
**Where:** Adaptation reports covering devolved Welsh functions
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> it must also have regard, so far as relevant, to any guidance issued by the Welsh Ministers under section 66 and the most recent report under section 80 (report on climate change: Wales).
