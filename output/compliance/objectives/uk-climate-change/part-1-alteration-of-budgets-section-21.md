# Part 1 – Alteration of Budgets (Section 21) — Carbon budget amounts subject to alteration must be tracked and reported

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State / carbon accounting authorities
**What:** The carbon budget amount for any period is the amount originally set, adjusted for any carry-forward or carry-back exercises and any alteration under section 21. Entities involved in carbon accounting must ensure the correct adjusted figure is used in all compliance determinations.
**Where:** In carbon accounting records and compliance determinations
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> That is the amount originally set, subject to any exercise of the powers conferred by section 17 (powers to carry amounts from one budgetary period to another) and any alteration of the budget under section 21.
