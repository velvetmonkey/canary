# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting (Determination whether objectives met) — Annual statement of UK emissions must record gas-level totals

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State; reporting authorities and regulated entities contributing to national emissions data
**What:** The net UK carbon account for each budgetary period must be calculated as the total of the amounts stated under the annual statement of UK emissions (section 16) for each targeted greenhouse gas across all years in the period. Accurate per-gas annual reporting is therefore mandatory.
**Where:** Annual statement of UK emissions (section 16); periodic carbon budget determinations
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> That is the total of the amounts (or adjusted amounts) stated under section 16 (annual statement of UK emissions) in respect of that gas for the years included in the period.
