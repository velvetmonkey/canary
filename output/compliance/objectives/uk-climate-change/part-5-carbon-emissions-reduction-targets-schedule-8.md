# Part 5 – Carbon Emissions Reduction Targets (Schedule 8) — Energy suppliers must meet carbon emissions reduction targets

## Obligation

**Who:** Gas and electricity suppliers subject to carbon emissions reduction target orders (Gas Act 1986, Electricity Act 1989, Utilities Act 2000 as amended)
**What:** Energy suppliers designated under the amended Gas Act 1986, Electricity Act 1989, and Utilities Act 2000 must meet carbon emissions reduction targets as specified in orders. Compliance requires implementing qualifying energy efficiency measures for customers.
**Where:** Compliance demonstrated to the relevant regulator under the amended energy legislation
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> [[Schedule 8]] contains amendments to the provisions of the Gas Act 1986 (c. 44), the Electricity Act 1989 (c. 29) and the Utilities Act 2000 (c. 27) relating to carbon emissions reduction targets.
