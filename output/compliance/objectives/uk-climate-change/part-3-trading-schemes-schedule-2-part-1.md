# Part 3 – Trading Schemes (Schedule 2, Part 1) — Participate in and comply with emissions-limiting trading schemes

## Obligation

**Who:** Participants in trading schemes as designated by regulations under Schedule 2, paragraph 4 or 15
**What:** Entities designated as participants in a trading scheme that limits greenhouse gas-emitting activities must comply with the scheme's requirements for each trading period, including holding sufficient allowances, meeting caps, and adhering to administrator directions.
**Where:** Within the trading scheme framework; scheme-specific compliance documentation and registries
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Part 1 deals with schemes that operate by limiting or encouraging the limitation of activities that consist of the emission of greenhouse gas or that cause or contribute, directly or indirectly, to such emissions;
