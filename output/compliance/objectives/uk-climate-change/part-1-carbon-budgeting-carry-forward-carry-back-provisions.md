# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting (Carry-forward/carry-back provisions) — Carbon budget carry-forward increases next budgetary period amount

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State / carbon accounting authorities
**What:** If emissions in a budgetary period are below the carbon budget, the surplus may be carried forward, increasing the next period's budget by that amount. Entities involved in carbon accounting must track and report these adjustments accurately.
**Where:** In carbon accounting records and annual statements of UK emissions
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The amount of the carbon budget for the next budgetary period is increased by the amount carried forward.
