# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting (Alteration of budgets or budgetary periods) — Carbon budget carry-back reduces later period and increases earlier period

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State; entities subject to carbon budget compliance
**What:** Where permitted, amounts may be carried back from a later budgetary period to an earlier one, reducing the later period's budget and increasing the earlier period's budget by the same amount. Entities must account for such adjustments in their carbon accounting and compliance calculations.
**Where:** Carbon budget determinations and annual emissions statements
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The carbon budget for the later period is reduced, and that for the earlier period increased, by the amount carried back.
