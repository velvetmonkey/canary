# Part 4 – National Reports and Programmes (Section 56 / adaptation reporting) — Reporting authorities must address climate change risks in adaptation reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Reporting authorities (public bodies and statutory undertakers with non-devolved functions)
**What:** Reporting authorities must produce adaptation reports that address the risks identified in the most recent climate change risk assessment report under section 56. Reports must cover how the authority is managing those risks in the exercise of its functions.
**Where:** Adaptation reports submitted to the Secretary of State
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> addressing the risks identified in the most recent report under section 56.
