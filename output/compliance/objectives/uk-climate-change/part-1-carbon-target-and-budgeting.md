# Part 1 – Carbon Target and Budgeting — Comply with UK 2050 carbon target obligation

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State; by extension all regulated entities subject to carbon budgets and trading schemes
**What:** Entities operating within the UK carbon framework must align activities with the legally binding 2050 carbon target. The Secretary of State must have regard to the need for UK domestic action on climate change when setting proposals and policies for meeting carbon budgets, establishing the baseline obligation for all downstream compliance.
**Where:** Across all UK domestic operations; embedded in proposals and policies for meeting carbon budgets
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> the Secretary of State must have regard to the need for UK domestic action on climate change.
