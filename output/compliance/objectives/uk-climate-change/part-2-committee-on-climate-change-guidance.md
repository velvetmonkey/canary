# Part 2 – Committee on Climate Change (Guidance) — Secretary of State must consult devolved authorities before issuing Committee guidance

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State
**What:** Before issuing guidance to the Committee on Climate Change on the matters it must take into account, the Secretary of State must consult the other national authorities (Welsh Ministers, Scottish Ministers, Northern Ireland). Joint guidance is required where the request originates from two or more national authorities.
**Where:** In inter-governmental consultation processes prior to issuing guidance
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> Before giving guidance under any of paragraphs (a) to (f), the Secretary of State must consult the other national authorities.
