# Regulation 3(a)-(e) — National security defences for company name requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and regulated firms registering or operating under [[Companies Act 2006]] company name rules
**What:** National security-related defences and exceptions to company name requirements are now active. Firms must understand when these defences apply and ensure any disclosures or registrations involving company names are compliant with the new framework.
**Where:** In company registration, disclosure documents, and regulatory filings
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> establish national security-related defences, exemptions and exceptions to new requirements concerning company names, identity verification requirements and false statement offences.
