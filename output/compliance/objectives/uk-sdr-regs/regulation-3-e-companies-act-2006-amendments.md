# Regulation 3(e) / Companies Act 2006 amendments — National security exemptions to false statement offences

## Obligation

**Who:** Companies, directors, and financial market participants filing information under [[Companies Act 2006]]
**What:** National security-related defences and exceptions to false statement offences under the [[Companies Act 2006]] are brought into force. Firms must understand when these defences apply to avoid inadvertent liability when handling sensitive information.
**Where:** Corporate filings, regulatory disclosures, and legal compliance processes
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> [[Regulation 3(a)]] to (e) bring into force various provisions inserted into the [[Companies Act 2006]] (c. 46) by the Act which establish national security-related defences, exemptions and exceptions to new requirements concerning company names, identity verification requirements and false statement offences.
