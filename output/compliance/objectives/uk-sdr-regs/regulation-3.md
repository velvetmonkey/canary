# Regulation 3 — Commencement of multiple economic crime provisions on 15 January 2024

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and regulated businesses
**What:** A package of provisions across AML, sanctions, identity verification, information sharing, and SFO powers all come into force simultaneously on 15 January 2024. Firms must conduct a gap analysis and ensure all relevant internal policies, procedures, and controls are updated and operational by this date.
**Where:** Across AML, sanctions, KYC, and governance compliance frameworks
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The following provisions of the Act come into force on 15th January 2024.

*Regulation 3, Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023 (Commencement No. 1) Regulations 2023* [verified]
