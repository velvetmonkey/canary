# Regulation 2 — Sanctions enforcement powers operative from 15 November 2023

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and regulated firms subject to UK financial sanctions
**What:** Section 214 of the Act came into force on 15 November 2023. Firms must ensure that any internal reporting, breach escalation, and sanctions compliance monitoring processes were updated to reflect the live enforcement risk from that date.
**Where:** In sanctions compliance monitoring, breach reporting procedures, and board/senior management reporting
**Deadline:** 15 November 2023
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Section 214 (sanctions enforcement: monetary penalties) of the Act comes into force on 15th November 2023 (so far as not already in force).
