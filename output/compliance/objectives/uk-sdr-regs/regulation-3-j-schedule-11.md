# Regulation 3(j) / Schedule 11 — Recognition and application of economic crime offences schedule

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants, regulated firms, and compliance officers
**What:** [[Schedule 11]], which lists 'economic crime offences', is now in force. Firms must familiarise themselves with this list as it defines the scope of economic crime for the purposes of related provisions (e.g., information sharing, SFO powers) and update their compliance frameworks accordingly.
**Where:** In compliance policies, training materials, and economic crime risk frameworks
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> [[Regulation 3(j)]] brings into force [[Schedule 11]] to the Act, which lists 'economic crime offences'.
