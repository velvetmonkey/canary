# Regulation 3(j) / Schedule 11 — Recognition and application of economic crime offences schedule

## Obligation

**Who:** All regulated businesses and financial market participants
**What:** [[Schedule 11]] to the Act, which lists 'economic crime offences', is brought into force. Firms must familiarise themselves with this definitive list to ensure their compliance, AML, and financial crime frameworks correctly identify and address all relevant offences.
**Where:** Financial crime compliance frameworks, training programmes, and risk assessments
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> [[Regulation 3(j)]] brings into force [[Schedule 11]] to the Act, which lists 'economic crime offences'.
