# Regulation 3(e) — National security exceptions to false statement offences

## Obligation

**Who:** Companies and directors subject to [[Companies Act 2006]] false statement offences
**What:** National security-related exceptions to false statement offences under the [[Companies Act 2006]] are now in force. Firms should update their legal risk assessments and director training to reflect these exceptions.
**Where:** In director training, legal risk assessments, and Companies House filing procedures
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> [[Regulation 3(a)]] to (e) bring into force various provisions inserted into the [[Companies Act 2006]] (c. 46) by the Act which establish national security-related defences, exemptions and exceptions to new requirements concerning company names, identity verification requirements and false statement offences.
