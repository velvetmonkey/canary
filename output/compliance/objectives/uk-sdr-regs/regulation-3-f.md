# Regulation 3(f) — Money laundering exemption for partial client property dealings

## Obligation

**Who:** Regulated businesses (including asset managers) dealing with client property
**What:** Amendments to the Proceeds of Crime Act 2002 create exemptions from money laundering offences for regulated businesses dealing with only part of a client's property. Firms should review their compliance frameworks to understand where this exemption applies and document reliance on it appropriately.
**Where:** AML compliance policies, transaction monitoring, and legal/compliance sign-off processes
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Regulation 3(f) brings into force amendments to the Proceeds of Crime Act 2002 (c. 29) which create exemptions from money laundering offences for regulated businesses dealing with only part of a client's property.
