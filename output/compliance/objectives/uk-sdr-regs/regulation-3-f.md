# Regulation 3(f) — AML exemption for regulated businesses handling partial client property

## Obligation

**Who:** Regulated businesses (including asset managers) dealing with client property
**What:** Regulated businesses now benefit from an exemption from money laundering offences when dealing with only part of a client's property. Firms should update their legal risk assessments and AML policies to reflect this exemption.
**Where:** In AML compliance frameworks, legal opinions, and transaction monitoring procedures
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Regulation 3(f) brings into force amendments to the Proceeds of Crime Act 2002 (c. 29) which create exemptions from money laundering offences for regulated businesses dealing with only part of a client's property.
