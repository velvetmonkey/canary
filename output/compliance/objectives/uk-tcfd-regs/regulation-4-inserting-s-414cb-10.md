# Regulation 4 (inserting s.414CB(10)) — Have regard to Secretary of State guidance on climate disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors and compliance officers of in-scope companies
**What:** Companies should monitor and apply any guidance issued by the Secretary of State on climate-related financial disclosures and the requirements of ss.414CA–414CB, as such guidance will inform how the disclosure obligations should be interpreted and fulfilled in practice.
**Where:** Internal compliance and reporting processes; reflected in the strategic report
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The Secretary of State may issue guidance on the climate-related financial disclosures, which are described in subsection (2A), and otherwise in connection with the requirements of this section and section 414CA.
