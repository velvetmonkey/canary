# Regulation 4 / s.414CB(2A) — Define and report prescribed climate-related financial disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** In-scope companies (traded, banking, insurance, AIM-listed, high-turnover with >500 employees)
**What:** Companies must report the specific categories of climate-related financial disclosures as defined in s.414CB(2A), which sets out the prescribed content (governance, strategy, risk management, metrics and targets in line with [[TCFD]] framework).
**Where:** In the non-financial and sustainability information statement within the strategic report
**Deadline:** Financial years commencing on or after 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** high
