# Regulation 4 / s.414CB(2A) — Define and report prescribed climate-related financial disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors of in-scope companies
**What:** Companies must report the specific categories of climate-related financial disclosures as defined in s.414CB(2A), which sets out the prescribed content of what constitutes 'climate-related financial disclosures' (covering governance, strategy, risk management, and metrics/targets in line with [[TCFD]]).
**Where:** In the non-financial and sustainability information statement within the strategic report
**Deadline:** Financial years commencing on or after 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** high
