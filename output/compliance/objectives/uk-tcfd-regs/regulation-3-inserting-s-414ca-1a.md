# Regulation 3 (inserting s.414CA(1A)) — High-turnover companies must comply with disclosure requirement

## Obligation

**Who:** High-turnover companies (turnover >£500 million, >500 employees, not otherwise categorised)
**What:** High-turnover companies are brought within the scope of the non-financial and sustainability information statement obligation, and therefore must also produce climate-related financial disclosures in their strategic report.
**Where:** In the company's strategic report
**Deadline:** Financial years commencing on or after 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Subsection (A1) also applies to a company if it was a high turnover company in relation to that financial year.
