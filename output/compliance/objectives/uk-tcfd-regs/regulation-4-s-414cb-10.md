# Regulation 4 / s.414CB(10) — Have regard to Secretary of State guidance on climate disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** In-scope companies and their directors
**What:** The Secretary of State may issue guidance on the climate-related financial disclosures and the requirements of ss.414CB and 414CA. Companies should monitor for and apply any such guidance to ensure their disclosures meet regulatory expectations.
**Where:** Internal compliance monitoring and disclosure preparation process
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The Secretary of State may issue guidance on the climate-related financial disclosures, which are described in subsection (2A), and otherwise in connection with the requirements of this section and section 414CA.
