# Regulation 3 / s.414CA(1A) — High-turnover companies must comply with disclosure obligation

## Obligation

**Who:** High-turnover companies (turnover >£500m, >500 employees, not otherwise categorised)
**What:** The non-financial and sustainability information statement obligation is extended to 'high turnover companies', expanding the scope beyond previously listed entity types. Companies must assess whether they meet this threshold.
**Where:** Within the company's strategic report
**Deadline:** Financial years commencing on or after 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Subsection (A1) also applies to a company if it was a high turnover company in relation to that financial year.
