# Regulation 2 (amending s.414C(2)) — Expand strategic report content to cover sustainability matters

## Obligation

**Who:** All companies required to produce a strategic report under s.414C of the [[Companies Act 2006]]
**What:** The strategic report content requirements are expanded by adding 'sustainability' alongside 'non-financial' matters, broadening the scope of information that must be addressed in the strategic report.
**Where:** In the company's strategic report
**Deadline:** Financial years commencing on or after 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In section 414C(2), after "non-financial" insert "and sustainability".
