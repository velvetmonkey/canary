# Regulation 3 / s.414CA(A1) — Include non-financial and sustainability information statement

## Obligation

**Who:** In-scope companies to which s.414CA(A1) applies
**What:** The strategic report must include a non-financial and sustainability information statement. This is the overarching document-level obligation that houses the climate disclosures.
**Where:** Within the company's strategic report
**Deadline:** Financial years commencing on or after 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> A strategic report of a company to which this subsection applies must include a non-financial and sustainability information statement.
