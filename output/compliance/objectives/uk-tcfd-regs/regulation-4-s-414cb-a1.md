# Regulation 4 / s.414CB(A1) — Include climate-related financial disclosures in strategic report

## Obligation

**Who:** In-scope companies (traded, banking, insurance, AIM-listed, high-turnover with >500 employees)
**What:** The non-financial and sustainability information statement within the strategic report must contain the company's climate-related financial disclosures. This is the core obligation of the entire regulation.
**Where:** In the non-financial and sustainability information statement, which forms part of the strategic report
**Deadline:** Financial years commencing on or after 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The non-financial and sustainability information statement must contain the climate-related financial disclosures of the company.
