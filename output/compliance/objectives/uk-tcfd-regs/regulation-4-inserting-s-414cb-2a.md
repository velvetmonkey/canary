# Regulation 4 (inserting s.414CB(2A)) — Disclose defined climate-related financial information

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors of in-scope companies
**What:** Companies must disclose the specific categories of information that constitute 'climate-related financial disclosures' as defined in s.414CB(2A), covering governance, strategy, risk management, and metrics/targets (consistent with [[TCFD]] framework).
**Where:** In the non-financial and sustainability information statement within the strategic report
**Deadline:** Financial years commencing on or after 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** high
