# Regulation 3 / s.414CA(2B) — Proportionately adjust turnover figures for non-standard financial years

## Obligation

**Who:** Companies with financial years that are not exactly 12 months
**What:** Where a company's financial year is not a full calendar year, the £500m turnover threshold must be proportionately adjusted. Companies must apply this adjustment when assessing whether they qualify as a high-turnover company.
**Where:** Internal compliance and threshold assessment process
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> For a period that is a company's financial year but not in fact a year the figures for turnover given by subsection (2A) must be proportionately adjusted.
