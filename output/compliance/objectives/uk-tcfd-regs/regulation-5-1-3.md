# Regulation 5(1)–(3) — Secretary of State review of Regulations by April 2027

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State (government obligation, but triggers potential regulatory change affecting firms)
**What:** The Secretary of State must periodically review the operation and effect of these Regulations, with the first review report published before 6 April 2027 and subsequent reports at intervals not exceeding 5 years. Firms should monitor outcomes as the review may result in scope or content changes.
**Where:** Published government review report
**Deadline:** First report before 6 April 2027; subsequent reports every 5 years
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> The first report must be published before 6th April 2027.
