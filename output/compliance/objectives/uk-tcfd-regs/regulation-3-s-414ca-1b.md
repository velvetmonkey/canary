# Regulation 3 / s.414CA(1B) — Scoping exemptions apply to high-turnover and other in-scope companies

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors and compliance officers of in-scope companies
**What:** The obligation to include a non-financial and sustainability information statement is subject to the exemptions set out in s.414CA(3)–(7). Companies must assess whether any exemption applies before concluding they are in scope.
**Where:** Internal scoping and governance process
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Subsections (1) and (1A) are subject to subsections (3) to (7).
