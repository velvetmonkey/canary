# Regulation 3 (inserting s.414CA(2A)) — Determine whether company meets high-turnover threshold

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors/compliance officers of companies not otherwise categorised as traded, banking or insurance companies
**What:** Companies must assess whether they qualify as a 'high turnover company' by reference to the turnover threshold of more than £500 million, to determine whether the climate-related financial disclosure obligation applies to them.
**Where:** Internal governance and compliance assessment process
**Deadline:** Assessed per financial year commencing on or after 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** high
