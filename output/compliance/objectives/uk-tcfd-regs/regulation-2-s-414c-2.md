# Regulation 2 / s.414C(2) — Expand strategic report scope to include sustainability information

## Obligation

**Who:** All companies required to produce a strategic report under s.414C
**What:** The strategic report's content requirements are formally expanded to cover 'sustainability' information in addition to 'non-financial' information, broadening the governance and reporting framework within which climate disclosures sit.
**Where:** Within the company's strategic report
**Deadline:** 6 April 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> after "non-financial" insert "and sustainability".
