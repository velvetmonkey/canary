# Regulation 3 (inserting s.414CA(2B)) — Pro-rate turnover threshold for non-12-month financial years

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors/compliance officers of companies with financial years that are not exactly 12 months
**What:** Where a company's financial year is not exactly one year in length, the £500 million turnover threshold used to determine high-turnover company status must be proportionately adjusted. Companies must apply this adjustment when assessing their scope obligations.
**Where:** Internal compliance scoping assessment
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> For a period that is a company's financial year but not in fact a year the figures for turnover given by subsection (2A) must be proportionately adjusted.
