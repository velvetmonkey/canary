# Part 6 — Schedule 14 / Schedule 7A (Biodiversity Gain in Planning) — Biodiversity net gain (BNG) mandatory condition on planning permissions

## Obligation

**Who:** Developers, planning authorities; indirectly real-estate and infrastructure investee companies of asset managers
**What:** All new planning permissions in England must satisfy a biodiversity gain condition, requiring a measurable net gain in biodiversity value. Post-development biodiversity value must be maintained for at least 30 years. Asset managers running [[Article 8]]/9 funds with real-estate, infrastructure or land-use exposures must assess whether investee companies comply with BNG requirements and factor this into Do No Significant Harm (DNSH) and sustainable investment assessments.
**Where:** Biodiversity gain plan submitted to and approved by the planning authority; registered on the biodiversity gain site register
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> be maintained for at least 30 years after the development is completed.
