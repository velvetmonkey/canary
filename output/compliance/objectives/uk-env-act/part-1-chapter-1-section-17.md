# Part 1, Chapter 1 (Section 17) — Policy Statement on Environmental Principles

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State; Ministers of the Crown when making policy
**What:** Ministers must have due regard to a statutory Policy Statement on Environmental Principles (covering the precautionary principle, prevention, rectification at source, polluter pays, and integration) when making policy. This embeds environmental principles into government decision-making and shapes the regulatory environment for businesses.
**Where:** Applied in government policy-making processes
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> "environmental principles" has the meaning given by section 17
