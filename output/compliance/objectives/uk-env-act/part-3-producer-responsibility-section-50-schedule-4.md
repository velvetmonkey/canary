# Part 3 — Producer Responsibility (Section 50 / Schedule 4) — Producers must meet recovery and recycling obligations for products/materials

## Obligation

**Who:** Producers, importers and operators of compliance schemes for specified products and materials
**What:** Producers of specified products (including packaging) must meet statutory recovery and recycling targets, register with the relevant authority, and may be required to join or operate a compliance scheme. Asset managers with consumer goods, packaging or manufacturing investees in [[Article 8]]/9 funds must assess compliance as part of circular economy and waste PAI indicators.
**Where:** Compliance schemes registered with and reported to the relevant national authority; recovery targets evidenced annually
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> "recovery", in relation to products or materials, includes— (a) composting them, or any other transformation of them by biological processes, or (b) obtaining energy from them by any means
