# Part 5 — Storm Overflows (Section 80, inserting Chapter 4 into Water Industry Act 1991) — Water companies must reduce and report storm overflow discharges

## Obligation

**Who:** Water and sewerage undertakers in England
**What:** Water companies must progressively reduce storm overflow discharges and report on overflow activity. Regulations may require monitoring and publication of data on discharges into watercourses. Asset managers holding water utility bonds or equity must assess compliance risk and associated capital expenditure obligations.
**Where:** Regulatory reports to Ofwat and the Environment Agency; public disclosure
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> where the storm overflow or works discharge into a watercourse.
