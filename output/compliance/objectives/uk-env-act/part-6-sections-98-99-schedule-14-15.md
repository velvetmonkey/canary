# Part 6 (Sections 98–99) / Schedule 14–15 — Biodiversity Gain in Nationally Significant Infrastructure Projects

## Obligation

**Who:** Applicants for development consent orders for nationally significant infrastructure projects in England
**What:** Applicants for nationally significant infrastructure projects must include a biodiversity gain statement demonstrating how the project will achieve biodiversity net gain, using the biodiversity metric. Offsite gains and biodiversity credits may be used where onsite gains are insufficient. This extends BNG obligations beyond standard planning to major infrastructure.
**Where:** Development consent order applications submitted to the Planning Inspectorate
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> "biodiversity credits" means credits under section 101 of the [[Environment Act 2021]]
