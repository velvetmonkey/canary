# Part 1, Chapter 1 — Environmental Improvement Plans (Section 8) — Environmental improvement plans must be maintained and reviewed

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State; public authorities implementing the plan
**What:** The Secretary of State must prepare, publish and periodically review an Environmental Improvement Plan (EIP) setting out steps to improve the natural environment. The plan must be monitored and reported on annually. This creates a policy framework against which investee companies' environmental performance can be benchmarked.
**Where:** Published plan laid before Parliament; annual progress reports
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> It is the duty of the Secretary of State to ensure that—would significantly improve the natural environment in England.
