# Part 3 — Electronic Waste Tracking (Sections 57–58) — Electronic tracking of waste movements required

## Obligation

**Who:** Relevant waste controllers (producers, carriers, brokers, dealers, exporters of controlled and extractive waste)
**What:** Waste controllers must use electronic systems to track and record waste movements from production through to disposal or export. Digitally excluded persons may be exempted. Asset managers with investee companies in waste management, manufacturing or extractive industries must ensure systems are in place.
**Where:** Electronic records maintained by waste controllers; accessible to enforcement authorities
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> "relevant waste controller" means any person who—(a)is subject to the duty in section 34(1) (duty of care as respects controlled waste),(b)imports, produces, carries, keeps, treats, manages or disposes of extractive waste or, as a dealer or broker, has control of such waste, or (c)exports relevant waste
