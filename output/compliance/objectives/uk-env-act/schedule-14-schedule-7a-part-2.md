# Schedule 14 / Schedule 7A (Part 2) — Biodiversity Net Gain Condition on Planning Permission

## Obligation

**Who:** Developers seeking planning permission in England; local planning authorities
**What:** All new developments in England must achieve a mandatory biodiversity net gain of at least 10% (measured via the biodiversity metric) as a condition of planning permission. Developers must submit a biodiversity gain plan showing how onsite and/or offsite habitat enhancements will be maintained for at least 30 years post-completion.
**Where:** In planning applications and biodiversity gain plans submitted to local planning authorities
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> be maintained for at least 30 years after the development is completed.
