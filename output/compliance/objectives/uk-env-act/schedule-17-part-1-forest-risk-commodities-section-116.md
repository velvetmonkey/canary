# Schedule 17, Part 1 — Forest Risk Commodities (Section 116) — Due diligence system required for forest-risk commodities

## Obligation

**Who:** Regulated persons using forest risk commodities (e.g. cattle, cocoa, soy, palm oil) in UK commercial activities above prescribed thresholds
**What:** Businesses must establish and maintain a due diligence system to ensure that forest risk commodities used in UK commercial activities are produced in accordance with relevant local laws. They must report on their due diligence annually. Asset managers with investee companies in agriculture, food, consumer goods or retail sectors must assess compliance.
**Where:** Due diligence system maintained internally; annual reports submitted to the relevant enforcement authority
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> "due diligence system", in relation to a forest risk commodity, has the meaning given by paragraph 3
