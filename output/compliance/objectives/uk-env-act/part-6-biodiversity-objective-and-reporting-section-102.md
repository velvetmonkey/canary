# Part 6 — Biodiversity Objective and Reporting (Section 102) — Public authorities must report on biodiversity duty and local nature recovery

## Obligation

**Who:** Public authorities with a biodiversity duty; responsible authorities for Local Nature Recovery Strategies
**What:** Public authorities must report on actions taken to comply with their biodiversity duty and contribute to local nature recovery strategies. For [[SFDR]] managers, this creates a publicly available data trail on biodiversity commitments and performance of UK public-sector counterparties and land managers relevant to nature-related PAI disclosures.
**Where:** Reports published publicly; Local Nature Recovery Strategies maintained by responsible authorities
**Materiality:** medium

> In this subsection "specified" means specified in the regulations.
