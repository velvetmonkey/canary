# Part 5 — Storm Overflows (Section 80 / Chapter 4 of Water Industry Act 1991 as inserted) — Water and sewerage undertakers must reduce storm overflow discharges

## Obligation

**Who:** Water and sewerage undertakers (e.g. Thames Water, Severn Trent); indirectly their investors and lenders
**What:** Sewerage undertakers are subject to new statutory duties to monitor, report and progressively eliminate storm overflow discharges into watercourses. Asset managers holding debt or equity in UK water utilities within [[Article 8]]/9 funds must assess compliance with these obligations as part of DNSH water-related PAI indicators.
**Where:** Operational infrastructure; regulatory reporting to Ofwat and the Environment Agency; watercourse discharge points
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> where the storm overflow or works discharge into a watercourse.
