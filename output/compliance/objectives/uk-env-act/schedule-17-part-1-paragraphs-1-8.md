# Schedule 17 (Part 1, paragraphs 1–8) — Forest Risk Commodity Due Diligence System

## Obligation

**Who:** Regulated persons using forest risk commodities in UK commercial activities above prescribed thresholds
**What:** Businesses using forest risk commodities (e.g. cattle, cocoa, soy, palm oil) in UK commercial activities must establish and operate a due diligence system to ensure those commodities are produced in accordance with relevant local laws, including laws on land use and forest protection. They must also report on their due diligence activities each reporting period.
**Where:** Within internal compliance systems and periodic reports submitted to the relevant enforcement authority
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> "due diligence system", in relation to a forest risk commodity, has the meaning given by paragraph 3
