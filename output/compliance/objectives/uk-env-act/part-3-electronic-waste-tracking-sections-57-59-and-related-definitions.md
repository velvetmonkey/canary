# Part 3 — Electronic Waste Tracking (Sections 57–59 and related definitions) — Electronic tracking of relevant waste movements is mandatory

## Obligation

**Who:** Relevant waste controllers (producers, carriers, brokers, dealers, exporters of controlled and extractive waste)
**What:** Waste controllers must use electronic systems to track and record movements of relevant waste throughout its lifecycle. Exemptions exist only for digitally excluded persons. Asset managers with investee companies in waste management, manufacturing or extractive industries must verify compliance as part of PAI assessments on waste and circular economy.
**Where:** Electronic records maintained by waste controllers; data submitted to enforcement authorities (Environment Agency, NRW, SEPA)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> "digitally excluded person" means a person— (a) who is a practising member of a religious society or order whose beliefs are incompatible with using electronic communications or keeping electronic records, or (b) for whom it is not reasonably practicable to use electronic communications or to keep electronic records for any reason (including age, disability or location)
