# Part 1, Chapter 1 (Sections 5–10) — Environmental Improvement Plans: Production and Review

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State
**What:** The Secretary of State must produce, maintain, and periodically review Environmental Improvement Plans (EIPs) setting out steps to improve the natural environment. Progress must be monitored and reported, and responses to scrutiny must be laid before Parliament. This creates the policy backdrop against which corporate environmental performance is assessed.
**Where:** Laid before Parliament; published publicly
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> the Secretary of State must produce a response and lay it before Parliament.
