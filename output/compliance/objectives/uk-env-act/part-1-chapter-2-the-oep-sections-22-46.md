# Part 1, Chapter 2 — The OEP (Sections 22–46) — OEP enforcement of environmental law against public authorities

## Obligation

**Who:** Public authorities (including regulators of financial markets); OEP as enforcer
**What:** The OEP has statutory powers to scrutinise, investigate and take enforcement action (including environmental review before the High Court) against public authorities that fail to comply with environmental law. Asset managers should be aware that the OEP's oversight creates a credible enforcement backstop underpinning the environmental targets and principles that feed into [[SFDR]] sustainability indicators.
**Where:** OEP decisions, information notices, decision notices and environmental reviews before the High Court / Court of Session
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> "the principles applicable on an application for judicial review" means, in relation to an environmental review, the principles that would apply on an application for judicial review in the jurisdiction under which the environmental review arises
