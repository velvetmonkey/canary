# Part 5 (Sections 80–81, Chapter 4 of Water Industry Act 1991 as inserted) — Storm Overflow Discharge Duty and Reporting

## Obligation

**Who:** Water and sewerage undertakers in England
**What:** Sewerage undertakers must progressively reduce storm overflow discharges and report on overflow activity. Regulations require monitoring and publication of data on storm overflow events, including where overflows discharge into watercourses. This creates direct compliance obligations for water companies and shapes investor ESG assessments.
**Where:** Operational systems; regulatory reports to the Environment Agency and Secretary of State
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> where the storm overflow or works discharge into a watercourse.
