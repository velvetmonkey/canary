# Schedule 14 / Schedule 7A (inserted into Town and Country Planning Act 1990) — Biodiversity Gain Condition — Biodiversity net gain mandatory condition for planning permission

## Obligation

**Who:** Developers seeking planning permission in England; local planning authorities
**What:** All new developments in England must achieve a mandatory biodiversity net gain of at least 10% as a condition of planning permission. Developers must submit a biodiversity gain plan demonstrating how post-development biodiversity value exceeds pre-development value. Offsite and credit mechanisms are available. Asset managers holding real-estate or infrastructure assets must ensure investee companies comply.
**Where:** Biodiversity gain plan submitted to and approved by the planning authority prior to development
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> be maintained for at least 30 years after the development is completed.
