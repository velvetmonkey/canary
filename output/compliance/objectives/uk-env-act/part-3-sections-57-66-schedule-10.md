# Part 3 (Sections 57–66) / Schedule 10 — Electronic Waste Tracking Obligations for Waste Controllers

## Obligation

**Who:** Relevant waste controllers (producers, carriers, brokers, dealers, exporters of controlled and extractive waste)
**What:** Relevant waste controllers must track and record waste movements electronically, using systems specified in regulations. Digitally excluded persons may be exempted. Enforcement authorities have powers to inspect and enforce compliance. This creates a digital chain-of-custody obligation for waste across the supply chain.
**Where:** Electronic waste tracking systems specified in regulations
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> "relevant waste controller" means any person who— (a)is subject to the duty in section 34(1) (duty of care as respects controlled waste), (b)imports, produces, carries, keeps, treats, manages or disposes of extractive waste or, as a dealer or broker, has control of such waste, or (c)exports relevant waste;
