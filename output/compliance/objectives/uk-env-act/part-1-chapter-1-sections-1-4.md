# Part 1, Chapter 1 (Sections 1–4) — Legally Binding Environmental Targets Must Be Set and Met

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State (government); indirectly relevant to public authorities and regulated entities
**What:** The Secretary of State must set long-term, legally binding environmental targets (covering air quality, water, biodiversity, and resource efficiency) and has a duty to ensure policies are in place to meet them. Targets must be laid before Parliament by 31 October 2022. This creates the overarching regulatory framework within which businesses must operate.
**Where:** Laid before Parliament; implemented through policy and regulation
**Deadline:** 31 October 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> must be laid before Parliament on or before 31 October 2022.
