# Part 1, Chapter 1 — Policy Statement on Environmental Principles (Section 17) — Environmental principles must be embedded in government policy-making

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State and Ministers of the Crown; indirectly regulated entities subject to policy decisions
**What:** The Secretary of State must produce and have regard to a statutory policy statement on environmental principles (including the precautionary principle, polluter pays, etc.). Ministers must apply these principles when making policy. For [[SFDR]] [[Article 8]]/9 managers, these principles define the baseline environmental governance framework against which investee companies' regulatory exposure is assessed.
**Where:** Policy statement laid before Parliament; applied in policy-making across government departments
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> the Secretary of State must produce a response and lay it before Parliament.
