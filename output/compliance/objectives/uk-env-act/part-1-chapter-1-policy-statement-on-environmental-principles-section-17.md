# Part 1, Chapter 1 — Policy Statement on Environmental Principles (Section 17) — Environmental principles must be embedded in government policy-making

## Obligation

**Who:** Ministers of the Crown; public authorities making policy
**What:** The Secretary of State must publish a policy statement on environmental principles (such as the precautionary principle and polluter pays principle). Ministers must have due regard to the statement when making policy. This shapes the regulatory environment for all sectors and is relevant to [[SFDR]] [[Article 8]]/9 fund managers assessing policy risk.
**Where:** Policy statement published and laid before Parliament; applied in policy-making processes
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> "environmental principles" has the meaning given by section 17; "policy statement on environmental principles" has the meaning given by section 17
