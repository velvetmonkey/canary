# Part 6 — Schedule 17 (Forest Risk Commodities) — Due diligence system required for forest-risk commodities in commercial activity

## Obligation

**Who:** Regulated persons using forest-risk commodities (cattle, cocoa, coffee, palm oil, soya, etc.) in UK commercial activities above prescribed thresholds
**What:** Businesses using forest-risk commodities must establish and operate a due diligence system to ensure those commodities are produced in accordance with local laws (including land-use laws). They must report on their due diligence annually. This is directly material to [[SFDR]] PAI indicator on deforestation and to [[Article 8]]/9 fund managers assessing supply-chain sustainability of investee companies.
**Where:** Due diligence system maintained internally; annual reports submitted to the relevant enforcement authority
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> "due diligence system", in relation to a forest risk commodity, has the meaning given by paragraph 3
