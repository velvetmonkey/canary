# Regulation 3(2) — Paragraph 1 (definition of 'QTS list request') — Formal QTS list request process established

## Obligation

**Who:** Persons seeking QTS in England; Secretary of State
**What:** A formal definition of a 'QTS list request' is introduced, meaning a request made to the Secretary of State for inclusion in the list of qualified teachers in England. Persons seeking QTS must submit such a request to be included on the maintained list.
**Where:** Secretary of State's maintained list of qualified teachers in England
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> "QTS list request" means a request made to the Secretary of State for inclusion in the list of persons who are qualified teachers in England which is maintained by, or on behalf of, the Secretary of State.
