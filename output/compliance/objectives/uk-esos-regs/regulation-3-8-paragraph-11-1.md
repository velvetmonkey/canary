# Regulation 3(8) — Paragraph 11(1) — Transitional cut-off for pre-February 2023 QTS applications

## Obligation

**Who:** Persons who submitted QTS applications before 1 February 2023
**What:** The QTS route under paragraph 11(1) is restricted to persons who submitted their application before 1 February 2023. Applications submitted on or after that date must use the new routes introduced by these Regulations.
**Where:** Secretary of State's QTS list
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> In paragraph 11(1), for "has submitted" substitute "has, before 1st February 2023, submitted".
