# Regulation 3(3) — Paragraph 3 — Welsh-qualified teachers recognised for QTS in England

## Obligation

**Who:** Persons qualified as teachers in Wales under section 132 of the Education Act 2002
**What:** A person who was a qualified teacher in Wales by virtue of Welsh regulations immediately before 1 February 2023 is recognised as a qualified teacher in England. This creates a transitional recognition pathway for Welsh-qualified teachers.
**Where:** England; QTS list maintained by the Secretary of State
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> The person is, immediately before 1st February 2023, a qualified teacher by virtue of regulations made in relation to Wales under section 132 of the Education Act 2002.
