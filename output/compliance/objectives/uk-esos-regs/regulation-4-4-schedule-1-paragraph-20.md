# Regulation 4(4) — Schedule 1, Paragraph 20 — MOD school induction exemption clarified

## Obligation

**Who:** Teachers who have completed induction in Ministry of Defence schools outside the UK
**What:** A person who has successfully completed an induction programme in a school administered or maintained by or on behalf of the Secretary of State for Defence and located outside the UK is exempt from the requirement to serve a further induction period in England.
**Where:** Schools administered by the Secretary of State for Defence outside the United Kingdom
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> A person who has successfully completed an induction programme for teachers in a school which is administered or maintained by, or on behalf of, the Secretary of State for Defence and outside the United Kingdom.
