# Regulation 4(2) — Schedule 1, Paragraph 8 — Updated exemption from induction period requirement

## Obligation

**Who:** School teachers employed in maintained schools and non-maintained special schools in England
**What:** Paragraph 8 of Schedule 1 to the Induction Regulations is substituted to update the categories of persons exempt from the requirement to serve a statutory induction period before taking up employment in a maintained or non-maintained special school.
**Where:** Maintained schools and non-maintained special schools in England
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low
