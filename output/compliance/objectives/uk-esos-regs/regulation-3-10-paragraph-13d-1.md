# Regulation 3(10) — Paragraph 13D(1) — iQTS qualification route to QTS recognition

## Obligation

**Who:** Accredited institutions in England approved by the Secretary of State; prospective teachers seeking QTS via iQTS
**What:** A person may obtain QTS in England by holding an international qualified teacher status (iQTS) qualification awarded by an accredited institution in England that is approved by the Secretary of State to award iQTS qualifications. Institutions must be approved before awarding such qualifications.
**Where:** Secretary of State's QTS list; accredited institutions in England
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> The person has an international qualified teacher status ("iQTS") qualification awarded by an accredited institution in England which is approved to award iQTS qualifications by the Secretary of State.
