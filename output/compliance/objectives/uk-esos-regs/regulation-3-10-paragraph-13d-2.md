# Regulation 3(10) — Paragraph 13D(2) — Definition of iQTS as overseas ITT qualification

## Obligation

**Who:** Accredited institutions in England; Secretary of State
**What:** An iQTS qualification must be awarded following successful completion of a course of initial teacher training conducted outside England, provided by or under arrangements made by an accredited institution in England. Institutions must ensure training is delivered outside England to qualify.
**Where:** Outside England, under arrangements of an accredited English institution
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> For the purposes of this paragraph, an iQTS qualification is a qualification awarded following the successful completion of a course of initial teacher training outside England provided by, or under arrangements made by, an accredited institution in England.
