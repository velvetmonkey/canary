# Regulation 3(10) — Paragraph 13E(1)–(4) — Overseas-qualified teachers route to QTS

## Obligation

**Who:** Persons qualified to teach in a country or territory other than the UK; Secretary of State
**What:** A new route to QTS is created for persons who are qualified to teach in a specified country or territory outside the UK. The Secretary of State specifies which countries/territories are recognised. Applicants must demonstrate they meet the criteria set out in sub-paragraphs (2) and (3).
**Where:** Secretary of State's QTS list; specified countries or territories
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> In this paragraph "specified" means specified by the Secretary of State.
