# Regulation 3(4) — Paragraph 4 — Scotland and Wales added to QTS recognition routes

## Obligation

**Who:** Persons qualified as teachers in Scotland or Wales
**What:** The existing QTS recognition route that previously covered Scotland is extended to also cover Wales. Persons qualified to teach in either Wales or Scotland may now use this route to obtain QTS in England.
**Where:** England; QTS list maintained by the Secretary of State
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> In paragraph 4, for "Scotland" substitute "Wales, Scotland".
