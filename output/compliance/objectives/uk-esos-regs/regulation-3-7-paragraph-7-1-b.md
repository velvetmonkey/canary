# Regulation 3(7) — Paragraph 7(1)(b) — Clarification of eligible ITT institutions in England

## Obligation

**Who:** Persons completing initial teacher training (ITT) in England; accredited institutions
**What:** The regulation clarifies which institutions in England are eligible for a person to complete their ITT course in order to qualify for QTS. Institutions must meet the updated criteria replacing the previous reference to 'an accredited institution in England'.
**Where:** Institutions in England providing initial teacher training
**Deadline:** 1 February 2023
**Materiality:** low
