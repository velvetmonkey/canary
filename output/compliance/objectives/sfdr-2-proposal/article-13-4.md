# Article 13(4) — Prohibition on use of 'impact' term for non-impact products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Only financial products that qualify as 'sustainability-related financial products with impact' under Article 2(26) (i.e. categorised under Article 7 or 9 with a pre-defined, positive and measurable social or environmental impact objective) may use the term 'impact' in their name.
**Where:** In product names
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial products other than those referred to in Article 2, point (26), shall not use the term 'impact' in their name.
