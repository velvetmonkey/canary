# Article 7(1) (third and fourth subparagraphs) — Safe harbour: EU Climate Benchmarks and Taxonomy alignment for Transition category

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants managing Transition category products
**What:** Products that replicate or are managed in reference to an EU Climate Transition Benchmark or EU Paris-aligned Benchmark are automatically deemed to meet all conditions of Article 7(1). Additionally, products with at least 15% of investments in taxonomy-aligned economic activities are deemed to meet the 70% contribution threshold of Article 7(1)(a). These safe harbours simplify compliance for benchmark-tracking and taxonomy-aligned products.
**Where:** In the design and management of the financial product
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The conditions described in the first subparagraph shall be considered to be met for financial products that replicate or are managed in reference to an EU climate transition benchmark that complies with the requirements laid down in Section 2 of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818, or an EU Paris-aligned benchmark that complies with the requirements laid down in Section 3 of Chapter II of that Delegated Regulation.
