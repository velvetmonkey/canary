# [[Article 6]] (amended [[Article 6]] [[SFDR]]) — Pre-contractual disclosure on sustainability risk integration for all products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Must disclose in pre-contractual documents for all financial products how sustainability risks are integrated in the investment decision-making process and the likely impacts of sustainability risks on the returns of the financial product.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures (in the manner specified in [[Article 6(3)]])
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The information referred to in paragraph 1 shall be disclosed in the following manner:
