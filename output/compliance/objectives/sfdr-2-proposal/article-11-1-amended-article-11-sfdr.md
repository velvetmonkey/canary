# Article 11(1) (amended Article 11 SFDR) — Annual periodic reporting for categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Must describe annually in periodic reports for each categorised product: (a) the extent to which applicable objectives are met or sustainability factors are integrated, by reference to the chosen sustainability indicators; and (b) for products with an 'impact' objective, the impact measurement and reporting information.
**Where:** In periodic reports (annual)
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall describe on an annual basis in the periodic reports for each financial product referred to in Article 7(1), first subparagraph, Article 8(1), first subparagraph, and Article 9(1): (a) the extent to which the applicable objectives are met, or sustainability factors are integrated, in particular by way of reference to the indicator(s) referred to in Article 7(3), point (d), Article 8(3), point (d), or Article 9(3), point (d)
