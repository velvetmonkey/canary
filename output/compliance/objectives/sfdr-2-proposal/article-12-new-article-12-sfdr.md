# Article 12 (new Article 12 SFDR) — Keeping website and sustainability disclosures up to date

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Must ensure that information published on websites under Articles 3 and 10 is kept up to date. When amending such information, must explain the amendment on the same website where the information was published.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall ensure that any information published in accordance with Article 3 or 10 is kept up to date. A financial market participant that amends such information shall explain such amendment on the same website as the website on which the information has been published.
