# [[Article 9(1)(d)]] and [[Article 7(1)(d)]] (new Articles 7 and 9 [[SFDR]]) — Identification and disclosure of principal adverse impacts for Sustainable and Transition products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering Sustainable- or Transition-category products
**What:** Must identify and disclose the principal adverse impacts of their investments on sustainability factors (including climate, water, circular economy, pollution, biodiversity) and explain any actions taken to address those impacts. May comply in full or in part using appropriate sustainability-related indicators.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures and periodic reports
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> they identify and disclose the principal adverse impacts of their investments on sustainability factors, and explain any actions taken to address those impacts.
