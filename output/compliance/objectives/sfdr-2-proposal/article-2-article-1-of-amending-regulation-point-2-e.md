# Article 2 (Article 1 of amending Regulation), point (2)(e) — Deletion of 'sustainable investment' definition

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** The definition of 'sustainable investment' in Article 2(17) of SFDR is deleted. Financial market participants must re-align their product design, disclosures and marketing to the new category-based framework rather than the former sustainable investment definition. Concepts of contribution, DNSH and good governance are now embedded in the category criteria.
**Where:** In product design, pre-contractual disclosures and marketing
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high
