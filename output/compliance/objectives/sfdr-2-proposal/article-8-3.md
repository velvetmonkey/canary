# Article 8(3) — ESG Basics category: mandatory pre-contractual disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** For ESG Basics category products, disclose in pre-contractual documents: a compliance statement, description of sustainability factors integrated, investment strategy and share of qualifying investments, sustainability indicators used to measure compliance, a statement of compliance with exclusions, and data sources used.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For financial products as referred to in paragraph 1, financial market participants shall disclose the following information:
