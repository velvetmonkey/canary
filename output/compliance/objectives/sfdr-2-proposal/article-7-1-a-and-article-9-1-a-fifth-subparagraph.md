# [[Article 7(1)(a)]] and [[Article 9(1)(a)]] (fifth subparagraph) — Exclusion of general-purpose sovereign debt from the 70% numerator

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants managing Transition (Art. 7) or Sustainable (Art. 9) category products
**What:** General-purpose issuances by public sector bodies must be excluded from the numerator of the 70% threshold for Transition and Sustainable categories. Only use-of-proceeds instruments from public sector bodies that comply with EU Green Bond standards or do not fund excluded activities may count towards the 70% numerator.
**Where:** in investment strategy and portfolio construction
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The first subparagraph, point (a) shall not include investment in issuances by public sector bodies, with the exception of the use of proceeds instruments issued by public sector bodies:
