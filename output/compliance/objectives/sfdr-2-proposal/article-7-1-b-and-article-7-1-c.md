# Article 7(1)(b) and Article 7(1)(c) — Transition Category: mandatory investment exclusions

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants managing Transition category products
**What:** Transition-category products must exclude: (1) investments in companies per Article 12(1)(a)-(d) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818, with limited exceptions for qualifying use-of-proceeds instruments; and (2) companies developing new fossil fuel or coal projects, or without a plan to phase out hard coal/lignite for power generation.
**Where:** in investment strategy and portfolio construction
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis
