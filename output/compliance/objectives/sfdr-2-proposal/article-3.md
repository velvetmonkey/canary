# Article 3 — Website disclosure of sustainability risk integration policies

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Publish on their website information about their policies on the integration of sustainability risks in their investment decision-making process.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall publish on their website information about their policies on the integration of sustainability risks in their investment decision-making process.
