# [[Article 8(3)]] (new [[Article 8]] [[SFDR]]) — ESG Basics category: mandatory pre-contractual and periodic disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering ESG Basics-category products
**What:** Must disclose: (a) statement of compliance with [[Article 8(1)]] criteria; (b) description of sustainability factors integrated; (c) investment strategy, share of qualifying investments, and any phase-in period; (d) sustainability indicators used and actions for underperforming assets; (e) statement of compliance with exclusions; and (f) data sources.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures (manner laid down in [[Article 6(3)]]), on websites ([[Article 10]]) and in periodic reports ([[Article 11]])
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For financial products as referred to in paragraph 1, financial market participants shall disclose the following information: (a) a statement that the financial product meets the conditions of paragraph 1; (b) a description of the sustainability factors that the financial product integrates
