# [[Article 13(2)]] (new [[Article 13]] [[SFDR]]) — Sustainability-related claims in names and marketing restricted to categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Sustainability-related claims may only appear in the names and marketing communications of products formally categorised under Articles 7, 8 or 9. Non-categorised products are prohibited from using such claims in names or marketing. Claims used must be clear, fair, not misleading and consistent with the product's sustainability features.
**Where:** In product names and all marketing communications
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants may only include sustainability-related claims in the names and in the marketing communications of financial products referred to in [[Article 7(1)]], [[Article 8(1)]] and [[Article 9(1)]].
