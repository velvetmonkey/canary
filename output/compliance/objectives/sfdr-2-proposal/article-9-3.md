# Article 9(3) — Sustainable category: mandatory pre-contractual disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** For Sustainable category products, disclose in pre-contractual documents: a compliance statement, description of sustainability objectives, investment strategy and share of qualifying investments, whether and to what extent EU Taxonomy is used, sustainability indicators used to measure progress, a statement of compliance with exclusions, and data sources used.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For financial products as referred to in paragraph 1, financial market participants shall disclose the following information in the manner laid down in Article 6(3):
