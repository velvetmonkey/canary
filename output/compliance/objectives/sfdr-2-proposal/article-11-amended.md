# [[Article 11]] (amended) — Annual periodic disclosures for categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants manufacturing, managing or making available categorised sustainability-related financial products
**What:** Financial market participants must publish annual periodic reports for categorised products disclosing performance against sustainability objectives, compliance with the 70% investment requirement, compliance with applicable exclusions, principal adverse impacts (where applicable), chosen sustainability indicators, and relevant data sources and assumptions. Disclosures must reflect the revised categorisation framework.
**Where:** Periodic reports (annual)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Amendment of [[Article 11]] on periodic disclosures by financial market participants on an annual basis, reflecting the revised scope, the creation of categories for 'sustainability-related financial products', and consistent with the de-prioritisation list.
