# [[Article 7(1)(b)]] and (c) — Transition category: mandatory investment exclusions

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Transition category products must exclude investments in companies covered by [[Article 12(1)(a)]]-(d) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818, and additionally exclude companies developing new fossil fuel projects or without a phase-out plan for hard coal/lignite power generation. Exceptions apply for qualifying use-of-proceeds instruments.
**Where:** in investment strategy and portfolio construction
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> they exclude investments in companies as referred to in [[Article 12(1)]], points (a), (b), (c), and (d) of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818, with the exception of investments in use of proceeds instruments issued by companies:
