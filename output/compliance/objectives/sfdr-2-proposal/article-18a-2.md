# Article 18a(2) — Obtain Legal Entity Identifier (LEI) for ESAP submission

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants that are legal persons
**What:** Financial market participants that are legal persons must obtain a Legal Entity Identifier (LEI) for the purposes of ESAP submissions.
**Where:** For ESAP reporting purposes
**Deadline:** 36 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> For the purposes of paragraph 1, point (b)(ii), financial market participants that are legal persons shall obtain a legal entity identifier.
