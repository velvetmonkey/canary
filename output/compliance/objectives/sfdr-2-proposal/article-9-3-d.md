# Article 9(3)(d) — Sustainable category: disclose EU Taxonomy use for environmental objectives

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Where a Sustainable category product pursues an environmental objective, disclose whether and to what extent the 70% investment requirement is met by investing in taxonomy-aligned economic activities. This enhances comparability across products with environmental objectives.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures, websites, periodic reports
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> where the product pursues an environmental objective, a statement on whether, and the extent to which, the financial market participant meets the requirement referred to in paragraph 1 point (a) by investing in accordance with paragraph 2 point (b)
