# Article 9a (new) — Disclosure rules for products investing in categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants managing non-categorised products that invest in categorised financial products
**What:** Non-categorised products that invest in categorised products but do not themselves qualify as categorised must disclose the share of their investments in each category of sustainability-related financial products (and in portfolios managed in accordance with categorised criteria), as well as the share in non-categorised products. Products that reach the 70% threshold by investing in categorised products and meet applicable exclusions may themselves qualify as categorised.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures and periodic reports
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For financial products that do not qualify for a category but invest in categorised financial products, in order to ensure comparability, disclosures should include how much these financial products have invested in financial products that are categorised as sustainability-related financial products, as well as in portfolios managed for clients on a discretionary basis in accordance with the criteria for categorised products, and how much in non-categorised products.
