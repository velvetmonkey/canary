# [[Article 9(1)(b)]] and [[Article 9(1)(c)]] — Sustainable Category: broader mandatory investment exclusions

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants managing Sustainable category products
**What:** Sustainable-category products must apply broader exclusions than the other two categories: (1) all companies per [[Article 12(1)]] of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818 (including fossil fuel value chain activities such as oil, gas, electricity generation above 100g CO2e/kWh); and (2) companies developing new fossil fuel or coal projects, or without a plan to phase out hard coal/lignite for power generation. Limited exceptions apply for qualifying use-of-proceeds instruments.
**Where:** In the investment portfolio of the financial product
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> they exclude investments in companies referred to in [[Article 12(1)]] of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818
