# [[Article 2(25)]]-(28) (new definitions) — Apply new definitions for sustainability-related product categories

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Must apply the new definitions introduced by the Regulation: (25) 'sustainability-related financial product' (categorised under Articles 7, 8 or 9); (26) 'sustainability-related financial product with impact' (categorised under Articles 7 or 9 with pre-defined, positive and measurable social/environmental impact); (27) 'public sector bodies'; (28) 'environmental objectives' (the six Taxonomy objectives under [[Article 9]] of Regulation (EU) 2020/852). These definitions underpin all categorisation and disclosure obligations.
**Where:** Across all product design, disclosure and reporting obligations
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> '(25)'sustainability-related financial product' means a financial product that is categorised in accordance with [[Article 7]], 8 or 9; (26)'sustainability-related financial product with impact' means a financial product categorised in accordance with [[Article 7]] or 9 that has as its objective the generation of a pre-defined, positive and measurable social or environmental impact; (27)'public sector bodies' means central governments or central banks, regional governments or local authorities, multilateral development banks as referred to in [[Article 117]] of Regulation (EU) No 575/2013 of the European Parliament and of the Council, and international organisations as referred to in [[Article 118]] of that Regulation; and (28)'environmental objectives' means the objectives referred to in [[Article 9]] of Regulation (EU) 2020/852, namely climate change mitigation and climate change adaptation, the sustainable use and protection of water and marine resources, the transition to a circular economy, pollution prevention and control, and the protection and restoration of biodiversity and ecosystems.'
