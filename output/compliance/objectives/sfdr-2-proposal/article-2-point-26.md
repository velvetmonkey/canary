# Article 2, point (26) — Definition of sustainability-related financial product with impact

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** A 'sustainability-related financial product with impact' must be categorised under Article 7 (Transition) or Article 9 (Sustainable) and have as its objective the generation of a pre-defined, positive and measurable social or environmental impact. This definition gates access to the 'impact' label and associated additional disclosure requirements.
**Where:** In product design and categorisation
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> 'sustainability-related financial product with impact' means a financial product categorised in accordance with Article 7 or 9 that has as its objective the generation of a pre-defined, positive and measurable social or environmental impact
