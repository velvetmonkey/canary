# Article 8(1)(b) — ESG Basics category exclusions — ESG Basics category: mandatory exclusions for most harmful activities

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants managing ESG Basics category products
**What:** ESG Basics category products must exclude investments in companies covered by Article 12(1)(a)-(d) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818 (prohibited weapons, tobacco, UNGC/OECD violations, hard coal/lignite). Use-of-proceeds instruments (EU Green Bonds) are excepted where proceeds do not fund excluded activities. No additional fossil fuel project exclusions apply (unlike Transition and Sustainable categories).
**Where:** In product design and investment process
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> (b) they exclude investments in companies as referred to in Article 12(1), points (a), (b), (c) and (d), of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818, with the exception of investments in use of proceeds instruments issued by companies: (i) in accordance with Articles 3 of Regulation (EU) 2023/2631; or (ii) where the proceeds do not fund any underlying activities excluded under Article 12(1), points (a), (b) and (d), of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818, provided that the issuer of the use of proceeds instruments is not excluded under Article 12(1), point (c); of that Delegated Regulation.
