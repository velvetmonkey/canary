# Article 7(4) and Article 9(4) — Additional disclosures for products with impact objectives

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants managing Transition (Art. 7) or Sustainable (Art. 9) category products with impact objectives
**What:** For products qualifying as 'sustainability-related financial products with impact' (Article 2(26)), additional disclosures are required: (a) the intended impacts in terms of specified environmental or social objectives, underpinned by a pre-set impact theory; and (b) provisions to measure, manage and report on the desired impact, including in terms of investments and the contribution of investors.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> For financial products with a transition objective falling within the meaning of Article 2, point (26), the information to be disclosed shall also contain:
(a)the intended impact(s) in terms of specified environmental or social objectives, underpinned by a pre-set impact theory; and
(b)provisions to measure, manage, and report on the desired impact pursuant to point (a)
