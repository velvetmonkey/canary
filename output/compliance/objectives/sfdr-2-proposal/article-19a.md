# Article 19a — Delayed application for IBIPs, pension products and PEPPs

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering IBIPs, pension products, pension schemes and PEPPs
**What:** Financial market participants must apply the new Articles 7, 8, 9, 10 and 11 to insurance-based investment products (IBIPs), pension products, pension schemes and PEPPs only 12 months after the general date of application of the Regulation (i.e. 30 months after entry into force), giving these products additional transition time.
**Where:** In product design, pre-contractual disclosures, websites and periodic reports
**Deadline:** 30 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis
