# Article 6a(3) — Annual periodic report sustainability disclosure for non-categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Financial market participants that voluntarily include sustainability factor information in pre-contractual documents for non-categorised products must also include an annual description of the consideration of sustainability factors in their periodic report, subject to the same conditions (not central, not in KIID/KID, no reserved claims).
**Where:** In periodic reports (annual)
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants disclosing information under paragraph 1 shall include on an annual basis a description of the consideration of the sustainability factors in their periodic report.
