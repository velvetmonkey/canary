# Article 9(1) — Sustainable category: 70% threshold and exclusions

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** To use the 'Sustainable' category label, a financial product must allocate at least 70% of investments to meet a clear and measurable sustainability objective, apply mandatory exclusions (full Article 12(1) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818 plus fossil fuel project exclusions), and identify and disclose principal adverse impacts with actions taken to address them.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites, in periodic reports
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis
