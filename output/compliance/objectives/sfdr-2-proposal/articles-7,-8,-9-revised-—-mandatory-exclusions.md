# Articles 7, 8, 9 (revised) — mandatory exclusions — Mandatory sector exclusions for all categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants manufacturing, managing or making available categorised sustainability-related financial products
**What:** All categorised products must apply a common set of mandatory exclusions covering: activities related to prohibited weapons, cultivation and production of tobacco, violations of UNGC principles or OECD MNE Guidelines, and hard coal and lignite. Transition and Sustainable categories have additional exclusions (e.g. new oil/gas projects; fossil fuel value chain for Sustainable). These exclusions replace the current PAI indicator-based 'do no significant harm' approach.
**Where:** Portfolio construction and investment process; disclosed in pre-contractual and periodic disclosures
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> the current approach should be replaced by mandating financial market participants to apply a common set of clear exclusions covering practices and sectors which are commonly agreed to be most harmful and to identify and disclose the principal adverse impacts of their investments on sustainability factors.
