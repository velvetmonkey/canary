# Article 7(3) — Transition category: mandatory pre-contractual disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** For Transition category products, disclose in pre-contractual documents: a compliance statement, description of transition objectives, investment strategy and share of qualifying investments, whether [[EU Taxonomy]] is used for environmental objectives, sustainability indicators used to measure progress, a statement of compliance with exclusions, and data sources used.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For the financial products referred to in paragraph 1, first subparagraph, financial market participants shall disclose the following information:
