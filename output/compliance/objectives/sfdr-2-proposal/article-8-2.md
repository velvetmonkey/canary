# Article 8(2) — ESG Basics category: qualifying investment approaches

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** The 70% of qualifying investments for the ESG Basics category must consist of one or more of: investments with an outperforming ESG rating, investments outperforming on a specific sustainability indicator, investments favouring undertakings with positive sustainability track records, combinations with Transition/Sustainable investments, or other sustainability-integrating investments with justification.
**Where:** in product design and investment strategy
**Materiality:** high
