# Article 7(1) — Transition category: 70% threshold and exclusions

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** To use the 'Transition' category label, a financial product must allocate at least 70% of investments to meet a clear and measurable transition objective, apply mandatory exclusions (Article 12(1)(a)-(d) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818 plus new fossil fuel project exclusions), and identify and disclose principal adverse impacts with actions taken to address them.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites, in periodic reports
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis
