# Article 11(1) — Annual periodic reporting for categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** In annual periodic reports for each categorised product (Articles 7, 8, 9), describe the extent to which applicable sustainability objectives are met or sustainability factors are integrated, by reference to the chosen sustainability indicators, and for impact products, report on the desired impact outcomes.
**Where:** in periodic reports
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall describe on an annual basis in the periodic reports for each financial product referred to in [[Article 7(1)]], first subparagraph, [[Article 8(1)]], first subparagraph, and [[Article 9(1)]]:
