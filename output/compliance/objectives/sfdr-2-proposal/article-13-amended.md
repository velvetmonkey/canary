# [[Article 13]] (amended) — Sustainability-related claims in names and marketing restricted to categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Sustainability-related claims in product names and marketing communications are only permitted for categorised products. Non-categorised products investing in categorised products (under Article 9a) may make such claims in marketing communications but not in their names. Financial market participants must also include disclosures on any ESG ratings used in marketing documentation as part of website disclosures.
**Where:** Product names, marketing communications, and website disclosures
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> sustainability-related claims in names and marketing communications would only be allowed for categorised products, with the exception of non-categorised products under Article 9a that would be able to make such claims in marketing communications but not their names.
