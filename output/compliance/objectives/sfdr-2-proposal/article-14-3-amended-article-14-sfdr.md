# Article 14(3) (amended Article 14 SFDR) — Prohibition on Member States imposing additional sustainability disclosure requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Member States / national competent authorities (obligation on financial market participants not to be subject to gold-plating)
**What:** Member States must ensure that competent or other national authorities do not apply requirements additional to those set out in Articles 3, 6, 10, 11 and 13 (information requirements) and Articles 7, 8 and 9 (categorisation criteria and transparency). This protects financial market participants from national gold-plating.
**Where:** At national regulatory level
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Without prejudice to Article 17(3), Member States shall ensure that competent or other national authorities do not apply requirements additional to those set out in Articles 3, 6, 10, 11 and 13 as regards information requirements, and in Articles 7, 8 and 9 as regards the criteria and transparency regarding financial products that are categorised as sustainability-related financial products.
