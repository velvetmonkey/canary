# Article 13(3) — Prohibition on sustainability claims in names of non-categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Non-categorised products (Article 6a products) may not include sustainability-related claims in their names or marketing communications. Products under Article 9a (investing in categorised products but not qualifying themselves) may include sustainability-related claims in marketing communications but not in their names.
**Where:** In product names and marketing communications
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants may not include sustainability-related claims in the names and in the marketing communications of financial products referred to in Article 6(a).
