# [[Article 2(25)]]-(28) — New definitions: sustainability-related financial product and impact product

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Apply the new definitions: 'sustainability-related financial product' means a product categorised under Articles 7, 8 or 9; 'sustainability-related financial product with impact' means a product under Articles 7 or 9 with a pre-defined, positive and measurable social/environmental impact objective. These definitions govern which products may use sustainability-related and 'impact' terminology.
**Where:** Across all product documentation, names and marketing
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> 'sustainability-related financial product' means a financial product that is categorised in accordance with [[Article 7]], 8 or 9; (26)'sustainability-related financial product with impact' means a financial product categorised in accordance with [[Article 7]] or 9 that has as its objective the generation of a pre-defined, positive and measurable social or environmental impact
