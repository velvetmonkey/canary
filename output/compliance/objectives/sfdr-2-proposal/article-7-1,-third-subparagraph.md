# Article 7(1), third subparagraph — EU Climate Benchmark products deemed compliant with Transition category

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Financial products that replicate or are managed in reference to an EU Climate Transition Benchmark or EU Paris-aligned Benchmark (complying with Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818) are automatically deemed to meet all conditions of the Transition category, simplifying compliance for benchmark-tracking products.
**Where:** In product design and categorisation
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The conditions described in the first subparagraph shall be considered to be met for financial products that replicate or are managed in reference to an EU climate transition benchmark that complies with the requirements laid down in Section 2 of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818, or an EU Paris-aligned benchmark that complies with the requirements laid down in Section 3 of Chapter II of that Delegated Regulation.
