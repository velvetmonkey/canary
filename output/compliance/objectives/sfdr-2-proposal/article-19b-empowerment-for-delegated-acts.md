# Article 19b (empowerment for delegated acts) — Comply with future delegated acts specifying category criteria and disclosure templates

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** The Commission is empowered to adopt delegated acts supplementing Articles 7, 8, 9 and 11 to specify: conditions for qualifying investments per category; voluntary sustainability indicators; permitted deviations from exclusions (e.g. for hedging); methodologies for calculating the 70% threshold and phase-in periods; and disclosure templates (max 2 pages per category, max 1 page for impact disclosures). Financial market participants must comply with these delegated acts once adopted.
**Where:** Across all product design, disclosure and reporting obligations
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The Commission shall be empowered to adopt a delegated act in accordance with Article 19c to supplement: (a) paragraphs 1, 2, 3 and 4 of Article 7 to specify: (a) the conditions for investments to contribute to the transition-related objective ... (b) the details of the presentation of the information to be disclosed pursuant to paragraph 3, which shall not exceed two pages, and the information to be disclosed pursuant to paragraph 4, which shall not exceed one page.; (b) paragraphs 1, 2 and 3 of Article 8 to specify: ... (b) the details of the presentation of the information to be disclosed pursuant to paragraph 3, which shall not exceed two pages.; (c) paragraphs 1, 2, 3 and 4 of Article 9 to: ... (b) specify the details of the presentation of the information to be disclosed pursuant to paragraph 3, which shall not exceed two pages, and the information to be disclosed pursuant to paragraph 4, which shall not exceed one page. (d) paragraph 1 of Article 11 by specifying the details of the presentation of the information to be disclosed, which shall not exceed two pages.
