# Article 7(4) — Transition impact products: additional disclosures on intended impact

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** For Transition category products qualifying as 'sustainability-related financial products with impact' ([[Article 2(26)]]), additionally disclose: the intended environmental or social impacts underpinned by a pre-set impact theory, and provisions to measure, manage and report on the desired impact.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites, in periodic reports
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> For financial products with a transition objective falling within the meaning of [[Article 2]], point (26), the information to be disclosed shall also contain:
