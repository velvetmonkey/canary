# Article 13(2) — Sustainability-related claims restricted to categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Only financial products categorised under Articles 7, 8 or 9 may include sustainability-related claims in their names and marketing communications. All such claims must be clear, fair, not misleading, and consistent with the product's sustainability features.
**Where:** In product names and marketing communications
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants may only include sustainability-related claims in the names and in the marketing communications of financial products referred to in Article 7(1), Article 8(1) and Article 9(1).
