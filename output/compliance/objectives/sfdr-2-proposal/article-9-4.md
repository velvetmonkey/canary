# Article 9(4) — Impact products: additional disclosures on intended impact and measurement

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** For Sustainable category products qualifying as 'sustainability-related financial products with impact' (Article 2(26)), additionally disclose: the intended environmental or social impacts underpinned by a pre-set impact theory, and provisions to measure, manage and report on the desired impact including in terms of investments and investor contribution.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites, in periodic reports
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> For financial products with a sustainability objective falling within the meaning of Article 2, point (26), the information to be disclosed shall also include:
