# Article 2 (amending PRIIPs Regulation, Article 8(3)(ca)) — KID disclosure of sustainability category for PRIIP sustainability-related products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants manufacturing PRIIPs that are sustainability-related financial products
**What:** The Key Information Document (KID) for any PRIIP that is a sustainability-related financial product (as defined in Article 2(25) of SFDR) must include, under a section titled 'How sustainable is this product?', its categorisation under Article 7, 8 or 9 of SFDR, and a description of its objective including relevant indicators.
**Where:** In the Key Information Document (KID) for PRIIPs
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> for a PRIIP that is a sustainability-related financial product as defined in Article 2, point (25), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088, under a section titled 'How sustainable is this product?', its categorisation in accordance with either Article 7, 8 or 9 of that Regulation, and a description of its objective including relevant indicators.
