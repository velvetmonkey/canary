# Article 7(4) and Article 9(4) (new Articles 7 and 9 SFDR) — Additional disclosures for products with 'impact' objective

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering Transition- or Sustainable-category products with an impact objective
**What:** Products categorised as 'sustainability-related financial products with impact' must additionally disclose: (a) the intended impacts in terms of specified environmental or social objectives, underpinned by a pre-set impact theory; and (b) provisions to measure, manage and report on the desired impact, including in terms of investments and the contribution of investors.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures and on websites (max. one additional page per delegated act)
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For financial products with a transition objective falling within the meaning of Article 2, point (26), the information to be disclosed shall also contain: (a) the intended impact(s) in terms of specified environmental or social objectives, underpinned by a pre-set impact theory; and (b) provisions to measure, manage, and report on the desired impact pursuant to point (a)
