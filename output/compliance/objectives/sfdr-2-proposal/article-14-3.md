# Article 14(3) — Prohibition on additional national sustainability requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Member States / national competent authorities
**What:** Member States must ensure that national competent or other authorities do not apply requirements additional to those set out in Articles 3, 6, 10, 11 and 13 (information requirements) and Articles 7, 8 and 9 (criteria and transparency for categorised products), preventing national gold-plating.
**Where:** At national regulatory/supervisory level
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Without prejudice to Article 17(3), Member States shall ensure that competent or other national authorities do not apply requirements additional to those set out in Articles 3, 6, 10, 11 and 13 as regards information requirements, and in Articles 7, 8 and 9 as regards the criteria and transparency regarding financial products that are categorised as sustainability-related financial products.
