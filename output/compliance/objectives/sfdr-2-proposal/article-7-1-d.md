# Article 7(1)(d) — Transition category: identify and disclose principal adverse impacts

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Transition category products must identify and disclose the principal adverse impacts of their investments on sustainability factors and explain any actions taken to address those impacts. Financial market participants may use sustainability-related indicators to comply with this requirement in full or in part.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures and periodic reports
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> they identify and disclose the principal adverse impacts of their investments on sustainability factors, and explain any actions taken to address those impacts.
