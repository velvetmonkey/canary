# Article 6a (new) — Restrictions on sustainability claims for non-categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering non-categorised financial products
**What:** Non-categorised products are prohibited from making sustainability-related claims in their names or marketing communications, and from featuring sustainability information as a central/prominent element in pre-contractual or periodic disclosures. Ancillary sustainability information may be included provided it is fair, clear and not misleading, but must not constitute a prominent element and must not use claims reserved for categorised products.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures, periodic reports, product names, and marketing communications
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> non-categorised products, while not prevented from including information in their pre-contractual and periodic disclosures on whether and how they consider sustainability factors, are required to ensure that such information is not a central element of those disclosures and does not constitute sustainability-related claims reserved for categorised products.
