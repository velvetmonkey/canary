# Article 9a(1) — Category eligibility for fund-of-funds investing in categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Financial products that combine categorised products may themselves qualify as a categorised product if they meet the 70% threshold through investments in categorised products or other qualifying investments, and comply with the applicable exclusions. Only products investing solely in Sustainable products can qualify as Sustainable; mixing Sustainable and Transition qualifies as Transition; mixing any three categories qualifies as ESG Basics.
**Where:** In product design and pre-contractual disclosures
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial products that claim that they combine financial products that are categorised as sustainability-related financial products shall be deemed to be compliant with the requirements of Articles 7, 8 or 9 if they meet the 70% threshold of investments referred to in paragraph 1 point (a) of those Articles by way of investments in categorised products or other investments that meet the requirements of Articles 7, 8 or 9, and comply with the exclusions in Articles 7(1), 8(1) or 9(1).
