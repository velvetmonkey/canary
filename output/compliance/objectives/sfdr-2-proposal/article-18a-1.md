# Article 18a(1) — Submission of disclosures to European Single Access Point (ESAP)

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** From 18 months after the date of application, when making public information under Articles 3 and 10, financial market participants must simultaneously submit that information to the relevant ESAP collection body, including required identifiers (all names, LEI, size category).
**Where:** European Single Access Point (ESAP)
**Deadline:** 36 months after entry into force (18 months after application date)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> From [18 months after the date of application of this Regulation], when making public any information referred to in Articles 3 and 10 of this Regulation, financial market participants shall submit that information at the same time to the relevant collection body referred to in paragraph 3 of this Article for the purpose of making it accessible on the European single access point (ESAP)
