# Article 9(1), fifth subparagraph — Sovereign debt exclusion from sustainable category 70% numerator

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Investments in issuances by public sector bodies (sovereigns, sub-sovereigns, supranationals) do not count towards the 70% numerator for the sustainable category, except for use-of-proceeds instruments issued by public sector bodies in accordance with EU Green Bond standards or where proceeds do not fund excluded activities. This rule also applies to the transition category.
**Where:** in investment portfolio calculation
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The financial products referred to in the first subparagraph, point (a), shall not include investments in issuances by public sector bodies, with the exception of use of proceeds instruments issued by public sector bodies:
