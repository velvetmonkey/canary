# Article 7(1)(b) and (c) (new Article 7 SFDR) — Transition category: mandatory investment exclusions

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering Transition-category products
**What:** Transition-category products must exclude investments in companies covered by Article 12(1)(a)-(d) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818 (prohibited weapons, tobacco, UNGC/OECD violations, hard coal/lignite), with limited exceptions for use-of-proceeds instruments. Must also exclude companies developing new fossil-fuel exploration/extraction projects or lacking a phase-out plan for hard coal/lignite power generation.
**Where:** In the investment portfolio (binding investment strategy)
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> they exclude investments in companies as referred to in Article 12(1), points (a), (b), (c), and (d) of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818, with the exception of investments in use of proceeds instruments issued by companies: (i) in accordance with Article 3 of Regulation (EU) 2023/2631
