# Article 9(2) — Sustainable category: qualifying investment approaches

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** The 70% of qualifying investments for the Sustainable category must consist of one or more of: EU PAB-tracking portfolios, Taxonomy-aligned activities, EU Green Bonds, Union programme co-investments, comparable high-sustainability assets with justification, EuSEF investments, or other investments contributing to environmental/social objectives with justification.
**Where:** in product design and investment strategy
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Investments by financial products as referred to in paragraph 1, point (a), shall include any or a combination of the following:
