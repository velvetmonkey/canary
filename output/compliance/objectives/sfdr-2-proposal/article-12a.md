# Article 12a — Data and estimates: documentation and client disclosure obligations

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** When complying with Articles 7–11, financial market participants must: (a) formalise and document arrangements with external data providers and methodologies for in-house estimates; and (b) provide clients upon request with information on data sources, provider names and contact details, and methodologies/assumptions underlying estimates.
**Where:** internal documentation; provided to clients upon request
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In complying with Articles 7 to 11, financial market participants:
(a)shall ensure that:
(i)the use of data provided by external data providers, other than open source or research freely available to the public, is based on formalised and documented arrangements;
