# Article 10 — Website disclosure for categorised sustainability-related products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Publish and maintain on their websites, for each categorised product (Articles 7, 8, 9), all pre-contractual disclosure information and periodic report information. Information must be clear, succinct, understandable, accurate, fair, not misleading, simple, concise, and in a prominent, easily accessible area of the website.
**Where:** on websites
**Materiality:** high
