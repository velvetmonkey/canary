# Article 9(1), fourth subparagraph — Taxonomy-aligned investment safe harbour for Sustainable category (15% threshold)

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Financial products with at least 15% of investments in taxonomy-aligned economic activities are deemed to meet the 70% contribution threshold under the Sustainable category. The remaining portfolio must still comply with applicable exclusions.
**Where:** In product design and pre-contractual disclosures
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The conditions described under the first subparagraph, point (a) shall be considered met for financial products with a proportion of investments in taxonomy-aligned economic activities, as defined in Article 1, point (2), of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2021/2178, equal or higher than 15%.
