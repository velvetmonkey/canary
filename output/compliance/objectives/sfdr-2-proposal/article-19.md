# Article 19 — Commission review of Regulation application after 36 months

## Obligation

**Who:** European Commission
**What:** The Commission must review the application of the Regulation 36 months after its date of application, considering: categorisation practices, data and estimates practices, and whether to adjust the 15% Taxonomy-aligned threshold for the Transition and Sustainable category safe harbours.
**Where:** Commission-level review process
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> By [36 months after the date of application of this Regulation], the Commission shall review the application of this Regulation and shall in particular consider:

*Article 19, Proposal for a Regulation amending Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 ([[SFDR]]), Regulation (EU) No 1286/2014 (PRIIPs) and repealing Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2022/1288* [verified]
