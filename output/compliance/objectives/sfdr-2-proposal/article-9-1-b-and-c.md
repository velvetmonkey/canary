# [[Article 9(1)(b)]] and (c) — Sustainable category: extended mandatory investment exclusions

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Sustainable category products must apply the full exclusion list under [[Article 12(1)]] of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818 (broader than Transition/ESG Basics, including fossil fuel value chain activities), plus exclude companies developing new fossil fuel projects or without a phase-out plan for hard coal/lignite power generation. Exceptions apply for qualifying use-of-proceeds instruments.
**Where:** In investment portfolio construction
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> they exclude investments in companies referred to in [[Article 12(1)]] of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818;
