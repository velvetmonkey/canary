# [[Article 7(3)]] (new [[Article 7]] [[SFDR]]) — Transition category: mandatory pre-contractual and periodic disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering Transition-category products
**What:** Must disclose: (a) statement of compliance with [[Article 7(1)]] criteria; (b) description of transition objectives; (c) transition strategy, share of qualifying investments, and any phase-in period; (d) whether [[EU Taxonomy]] is used for environmental objectives; (e) sustainability indicators and actions for underperforming assets; (f) statement of compliance with exclusions; and (g) data sources.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures (manner laid down in [[Article 6(3)]]), on websites ([[Article 10]]) and in periodic reports ([[Article 11]])
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For the financial products referred to in paragraph 1, first subparagraph, financial market participants shall disclose the following information: (a) a statement that the financial product meets the conditions laid down in paragraph 1; (b) a description of the transition-related objective(s) to which the financial product contributes
