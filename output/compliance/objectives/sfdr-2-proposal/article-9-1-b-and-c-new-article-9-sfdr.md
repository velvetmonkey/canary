# Article 9(1)(b) and (c) (new Article 9 SFDR) — Sustainable category: extended mandatory investment exclusions including fossil fuels

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering Sustainable-category products
**What:** Sustainable-category products must apply the full exclusions under Article 12(1) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818 (which extends beyond the Transition category to include oil fuels, gaseous fuels, and electricity generation above 100g CO2e/kWh), plus exclusions for companies developing new fossil-fuel projects or lacking a phase-out plan for hard coal/lignite power generation.
**Where:** In the investment portfolio (binding investment strategy)
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> they exclude investments in companies referred to in Article 12(1) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818;
