# [[Article 9(3)]] (new [[Article 9]] [[SFDR]]) — Sustainable category: mandatory pre-contractual and periodic disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering Sustainable-category products
**What:** Must disclose: (a) a statement of compliance with [[Article 9(1)]] criteria; (b) description of sustainability objectives; (c) investment strategy, share of qualifying investments, and any phase-in period; (d) whether and to what extent [[EU Taxonomy]] is used for environmental objectives; (e) sustainability indicators used to measure progress; (f) statement of compliance with exclusions; and (g) data sources used.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures (manner laid down in [[Article 6(3)]]), on websites ([[Article 10]]) and in periodic reports ([[Article 11]])
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For financial products as referred to in paragraph 1, financial market participants shall disclose the following information in the manner laid down in [[Article 6(3)]]: (a) a statement that the financial product meets the conditions of paragraph 1; (b) a description of the sustainability-related objective(s) to which the financial product contributes
