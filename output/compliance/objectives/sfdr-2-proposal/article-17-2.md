# Article 17(2) — Voluntary labelling schemes with higher standards remain permitted

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and national/EU labelling scheme operators
**What:** The Regulation does not prevent voluntary sustainability-related labelling schemes for financial products that exceed the requirements of Articles 7, 8 and 9 in terms of specified objectives, investment approaches, governance or transparency requirements. Firms may participate in such schemes alongside SFDR compliance.
**Where:** At the product level
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> This Regulation is without prejudice to voluntary sustainability-related labelling schemes for financial products with features that exceed those in Articles 7, 8 and 9 in terms of specified objectives, investment approaches, governance or transparency requirements.
