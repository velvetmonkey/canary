# Article 10 (amended) — Website disclosures for categorised sustainability-related products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants manufacturing, managing or making available categorised sustainability-related financial products
**What:** Financial market participants must publish and maintain up-to-date website disclosures for categorised products, including information on the product category, sustainability objective or strategy, investment approaches, exclusions applied, PAI disclosures (where applicable), and any ESG ratings included in marketing documentation. Website disclosures must reflect the revised categorisation framework.
**Where:** Websites of financial market participants
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Amendment of Article 10 on website disclosures by financial market participants, reflecting the revised scope, the creation of categories for 'sustainability-related financial products', and consistent with the de-prioritisation list.
