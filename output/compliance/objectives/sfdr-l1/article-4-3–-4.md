# Article 4(3)–(4) — Mandatory PAI statement for large FMPs (500+ employees)

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants exceeding 500 employees on average, or parent undertakings of large groups exceeding 500 employees on a consolidated basis
**What:** Large FMPs (>500 average employees) must mandatorily publish and maintain on their websites a statement on due diligence policies with respect to the principal adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors, including all elements listed in Article 4(2). The 'comply or explain' option in Article 4(1) does not apply to these entities.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website
**Deadline:** 30 June 2021
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> From 30 June 2021, financial market participants exceeding on their balance sheet dates the criterion of the average number of 500 employees during the financial year shall publish and maintain on their websites a statement on their due diligence policies with respect to the principal adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors.
