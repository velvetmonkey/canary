# Article 9(2) — Explanation of how sustainable investment objective is attained (no benchmark)

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Where a financial product has sustainable investment as its objective but no index has been designated as a reference benchmark, include in pre-contractual disclosures an explanation of how that sustainable investment objective is to be attained.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures per Article 6(1) and (3)
**Deadline:** 10 March 2021
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Where a financial product has sustainable investment as its objective and no index has been designated as a reference benchmark, the information to be disclosed pursuant to Article 6(1) and (3) shall include an explanation on how that objective is to be attained.
