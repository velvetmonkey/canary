# Article 15(1) — IORPs: publish [[SFDR]] disclosures per IORP Directive requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Institutions for occupational retirement provision (IORPs)
**What:** Publish and maintain the information required under Articles 3–7 and [[Article 10(1)]] of [[SFDR]] in accordance with [[Article 36(2)(f)]] of Directive (EU) 2016/2341.
**Where:** In accordance with [[Article 36(2)(f)]] of Directive (EU) 2016/2341 (IORP II)
**Deadline:** 10 March 2021
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> IORPs shall publish and maintain the information referred to in Articles 3 to 7 and the first subparagraph of [[Article 10(1)]], of this Regulation in accordance with point (f) of [[Article 36(2)]] of Directive (EU) 2016/2341.
