# Article 7(1) — Pre-contractual PAI disclosure at financial product level

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants (those applying Article 4(1)(a) or Article 4(3) or (4))
**What:** For each financial product, include in pre-contractual disclosures: (a) a clear and reasoned explanation of whether and how the product considers principal adverse impacts on sustainability factors, and (b) a statement that PAI information is available in the periodic report under Article 11(2).
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures per Article 6(3)
**Deadline:** 30 December 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> By 30 December 2022, for each financial product where a financial market participant applies point (a) of Article 4(1) or Article 4(3) or (4), the disclosures referred to in Article 6(3) shall include the following: (a) a clear and reasoned explanation of whether, and, if so, how a financial product considers principal adverse impacts on sustainability factors.
