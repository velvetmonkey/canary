# Article 9(3) — Carbon-emission-reduction products: [[Paris Agreement]] alignment disclosure

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Where a financial product has a reduction in carbon emissions as its objective, include in pre-contractual disclosures the objective of low carbon emission exposure in view of achieving the [[Paris Agreement]] long-term global warming goals. Where no EU Climate Transition Benchmark or EU Paris-aligned Benchmark is available, provide a detailed explanation of how the continued effort to reduce carbon emissions is ensured.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures per [[Article 6(3)]] channels
**Deadline:** 10 March 2021
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Where a financial product has a reduction in carbon emissions as its objective, the information to be disclosed pursuant to [[Article 6(1)]] and (3) shall include the objective of low carbon emission exposure in view of achieving the long‐term global warming objectives of the [[Paris Agreement]].
