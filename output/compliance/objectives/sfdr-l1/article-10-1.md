# Article 10(1) — Website disclosure for Article 8 and Article 9 products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Publish and maintain on websites, for each Article 8 and Article 9 product: (a) description of E/S characteristics or sustainable investment objective; (b) methodologies used to assess, measure and monitor those characteristics/impacts, including data sources, screening criteria and sustainability indicators; (c) the pre-contractual information per Articles 8 and 9; and (d) the periodic report information per Article 11. Information must be clear, succinct, accurate, fair, not misleading, simple and concise, in a prominent accessible area.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website, in a prominent easily accessible area
**Deadline:** 10 March 2021
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall publish and maintain on their websites the following information for each financial product referred to in Article 8(1) and Article 9(1), (2) and (3): (a) a description of the environmental or social characteristics or the sustainable investment objective; (b) information on the methodologies used to assess, measure and monitor the environmental or social characteristics or the impact of the sustainable investments selected for the financial product.
