# Article 17(1) — Exemption for micro financial advisers (<3 employees)

## Obligation

**Who:** Insurance intermediaries providing IBIP advice and investment firms providing investment advice with fewer than 3 employees
**What:** [[SFDR]] does not apply to insurance intermediaries providing IBIP advice or investment firms providing investment advice that employ fewer than three persons. These entities are therefore not required to make [[SFDR]] disclosures (though national law transposing [[MiFID II]]/IDD still applies to them).
**Where:** N/A — exemption from all [[SFDR]] disclosure channels
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> This Regulation shall neither apply to insurance intermediaries which provide insurance advice with regard to IBIPs nor to investment firms which provide investment advice that are enterprises irrespective of their legal form, including natural persons and self‐employed persons, provided that they employ fewer than three persons.
