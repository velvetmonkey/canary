# Article 6(3) — Channel-specific delivery of pre-contractual sustainability disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and financial advisers
**What:** Deliver the pre-contractual sustainability risk disclosures required by Article 6(1) and (2) through the correct sectoral channel for each entity type: AIFMs in AIFMD investor disclosures; insurance undertakings in Solvency II/IDD pre-contractual information; IORPs in IORP member information; EuVECA/EuSEF managers in their investor information documents; pension product manufacturers in writing before the retail investor is bound; UCITS management companies in the UCITS prospectus; investment firms/credit institutions in MiFID II pre-contractual information; insurance intermediaries/undertakings in IDD pre-contractual information; ELTIF AIFMs in the ELTIF prospectus; PEPP providers in the PEPP KIID.
**Where:** In the applicable pre-contractual document per entity type (prospectus, KIID, investor disclosure, etc.)
**Deadline:** 10 March 2021
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The information referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 of this Article shall be disclosed in the following manner: (a) for AIFMs, in the disclosures to investors referred to in Article 23(1) of Directive 2011/61/EU; (b) for insurance undertakings, in the provision of information referred to in Article 185(2) of Directive 2009/138/EC or, where relevant, in accordance with Article 29(1) of Directive (EU) 2016/97; (c) for IORPs, in the provision of information referred to in Article 41 of Directive (EU) 2016/2341; (d) for managers of qualifying venture capital funds, in the provision of information referred to in Article 13(1) of Regulation (EU) No 345/2013; (e) for managers of qualifying social entrepreneurship funds, in the provision of information referred to in Article 14(1) of Regulation (EU) No 346/2013; (f) for manufacturers of pension products, in writing in good time before a retail investor is bound by a contract relating to a pension product; (g) for UCITS management companies, in the prospectus referred to in Article 69 of Directive 2009/65/EC.
