# Article 12(2) — Financial advisers must keep website disclosures up to date

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial advisers
**What:** Ensure that information published on websites under Articles 3 and 5 is kept up to date, and where information is amended, publish a clear explanation of the amendment on the same website.
**Where:** On the financial adviser's website
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> Paragraph 1 shall apply mutatis mutandis to financial advisers with regard to any information published in accordance with Articles 3 and 5.
