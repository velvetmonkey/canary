# Article 12(1) — Keep website disclosures up to date and explain amendments

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Ensure that information published on websites under Articles 3, 5 and 10 is kept up to date. Where information is amended, publish a clear explanation of the amendment on the same website.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall ensure that any information published in accordance with Article 3, 5 or 10 is kept up to date. Where a financial market participant amends such information, a clear explanation of such amendment shall be published on the same website.
