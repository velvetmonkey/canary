# [[Article 1(6)(d)]] — amending [[Article 54(10)]] of Reg. 2017/565 (third subparagraph) — Record client decision to adapt sustainability preferences

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** Where no financial instrument meets the client's sustainability preferences and the client decides to adapt those preferences, the investment firm must keep records of the client's decision and the reasons supporting that adaptation, to prevent mis-selling and greenwashing.
**Where:** Internally, within client records and suitability documentation
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Where no financial instrument meets the sustainability preferences of the client or potential client, and the client decides to adapt his or her sustainability preferences, the investment firm shall keep records of the decision of the client, including the reasons for that decision.
