# Article 1(6)(f) — amending Article 54(13) of Reg. 2017/565 — Sustainability preferences do not override core suitability conditions

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** The obligation to meet client sustainability preferences must not alter or override the core suitability conditions (financial objectives, risk tolerance, knowledge, experience, capacity for loss). Sustainability preferences are an additional layer, not a replacement for the fundamental suitability framework.
**Where:** Within the suitability assessment process and advisory procedures
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The requirements to meet the sustainability preferences of clients or potential clients, where relevant, shall not alter the conditions laid down in the first subparagraph.
