# Article 1(6)(f) — amending Article 54(13) — Sustainability preferences requirements do not override general suitability conditions

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** Investment firms must ensure that the obligation to meet client sustainability preferences does not alter or override the general suitability conditions applicable to portfolio management and investment advice. Sustainability preferences are an additional layer, not a replacement of core suitability requirements.
**Where:** Internal suitability assessment processes and procedures
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The requirements to meet the sustainability preferences of clients or potential clients, where relevant, shall not alter the conditions laid down in the first subparagraph.
