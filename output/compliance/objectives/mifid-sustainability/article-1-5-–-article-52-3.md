# Article 1(5) – Article 52(3) — Describe sustainability factors in the scope of advisory service

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice (including independent advisers)
**What:** Firms must provide clients with a description of the types of instruments considered, the range of providers analysed, and — where relevant — the sustainability factors taken into account in the selection process, as part of the service description.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures and service documentation provided to clients
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Investment firms shall provide a description of: (a) the types of financial instruments considered; (b) the range of financial instruments and providers, analysed per each type of instrument according to the scope of the service; (c) where relevant, the sustainability factors taken into consideration in the selection process of financial instruments;
