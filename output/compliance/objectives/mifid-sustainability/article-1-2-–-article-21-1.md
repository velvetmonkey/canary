# Article 1(2) – Article 21(1) — Integrate sustainability risks into organisational and internal control requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms
**What:** Firms must take sustainability risks into account when complying with organisational requirements, including processes, systems, and internal controls, ensuring technical capacity and knowledge to analyse those risks.
**Where:** Internal governance, organisational arrangements, and control frameworks
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Investment firms shall take into account sustainability risks when complying with the requirements set out in this paragraph.
