# Article 1(6)(d) – Article 54(10) subparagraph 2 — Explain and record reasons for not meeting sustainability preferences

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** When a firm cannot recommend instruments that meet the client's sustainability preferences, it must explain the reasons to the client and keep records of those reasons to prevent greenwashing and mis-selling.
**Where:** Client-facing communication and internal records
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The investment firm shall explain to the client or potential clients the reasons for not doing so and keep records of those reasons.
