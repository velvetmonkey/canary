# Article 1(5) – Article 52(3)(c) — Disclose sustainability factors in instrument selection process description

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice
**What:** Firms must include in their description of the advisory service, where relevant, the sustainability factors taken into consideration in the selection process of financial instruments recommended to clients.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures and service description documentation provided to clients
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> where relevant, the sustainability factors taken into consideration in the selection process of financial instruments;
