# Article 1(3) — amending Article 23(1)(a) of Reg. 2017/565 — Integrate sustainability risks into risk management policies and procedures

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms
**What:** Investment firms must establish, implement and maintain adequate risk management policies and procedures that identify risks relating to the firm's activities, processes and systems. These policies must explicitly take sustainability risks into account.
**Where:** Internally, within the firm's risk management framework and documented policies
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> establish, implement and maintain adequate risk management policies and procedures which identify the risks relating to the firm's activities, processes and systems, and, where appropriate, set the level of risk tolerated by the firm. In doing so, investment firms shall take into account sustainability risks;
