# Article 1(6)(b) — amending Article 54(5) of Reg. 2017/565 — Collect client sustainability preferences as part of suitability information

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** When gathering information on a client's investment objectives, firms must also collect information on the client's sustainability preferences, in addition to investment horizon, risk preferences, risk tolerance and purpose of investment.
**Where:** In pre-contractual and ongoing client suitability information-gathering processes
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The information about the investment objectives of the client or potential client shall include, where relevant, information about the length of time for which the client wishes to hold the investment, his or her preferences regarding risk taking, his or her risk tolerance, the purpose of the investment and in addition his or her sustainability preferences.
