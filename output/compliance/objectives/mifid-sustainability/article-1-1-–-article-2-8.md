# Article 1(1) – Article 2(8) — Apply SFDR definition of 'sustainability factors' consistently

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms
**What:** Firms must use the definition of 'sustainability factors' as defined in Article 2(24) of SFDR (Regulation (EU) 2019/2088) consistently across all relevant internal processes, disclosures, and client-facing documentation.
**Where:** All internal policies, disclosures, and client documentation referencing sustainability factors
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> "sustainability factors" means sustainability factors as defined in Article 2, point (24), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088;
