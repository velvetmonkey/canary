# Article 1(5) — amending Article 52(3) of Reg. 2017/565 — Disclose sustainability factors considered in financial instrument selection

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice
**What:** Investment firms must provide clients with a description of the sustainability factors taken into consideration in the selection process of financial instruments, where relevant, as part of the information provided about the advisory service.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures and descriptions of the investment advice service provided to clients
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> where relevant, the sustainability factors taken into consideration in the selection process of financial instruments;
