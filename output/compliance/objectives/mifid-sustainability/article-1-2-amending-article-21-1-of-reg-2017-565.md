# Article 1(2) — amending Article 21(1) of Reg. 2017/565 — Integrate sustainability risks into organisational requirements and internal controls

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms
**What:** Investment firms must take sustainability risks into account when complying with organisational requirements, including processes, systems and internal controls. The approach must be proportionate to the nature, scale and complexity of the firm's business and the nature and range of its investment services and activities.
**Where:** Internally, within the firm's organisational structure, processes, systems and controls
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Investment firms shall take into account sustainability risks when complying with the requirements set out in this paragraph.
