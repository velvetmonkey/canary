# Article 1(5) — amending Article 52(3)(c) — Disclose sustainability factors considered in financial instrument selection

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice
**What:** Investment firms must provide clients with a description of the sustainability factors taken into consideration in the selection process of financial instruments, where relevant.
**Where:** Pre-contractual or advisory disclosures to clients (description of service)
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> where relevant, the sustainability factors taken into consideration in the selection process of financial instruments;
