# Article 1(1) – Article 2(7)(b) — Capture client minimum proportion for SFDR sustainable investments

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** Firms must capture and act upon a client's stated minimum proportion to be invested in sustainable investments as defined in Article 2(17) of SFDR (Regulation (EU) 2019/2088).
**Where:** Client suitability questionnaires and portfolio management mandates
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> a financial instrument for which the client or potential client determines that a minimum proportion shall be invested in sustainable investments as defined in Article 2, point (17), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 of the European Parliament and of the Council;
