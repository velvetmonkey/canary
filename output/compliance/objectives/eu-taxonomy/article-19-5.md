# Article 19(5) — Commission to regularly review and update technical screening criteria

## Obligation

**Who:** European Commission
**What:** Regularly review the technical screening criteria and amend delegated acts in line with scientific and technological developments. For transitional activities under [[Article 10(2)]], review at least every three years to ensure they remain on a credible climate-neutrality pathway.
**Where:** Delegated acts (Official Journal)
**Deadline:** At least every three years for transitional activities
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The Commission shall regularly review the technical screening criteria referred to in paragraph 1 and, where appropriate, amend the delegated acts adopted in accordance with this Regulation in line with scientific and technological developments.
