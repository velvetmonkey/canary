# Article 19(1) — Technical screening criteria must meet prescribed quality requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** European Commission (when establishing/updating technical screening criteria); financial market participants must apply criteria meeting these standards
**What:** Technical screening criteria must: identify relevant contributions while respecting technological neutrality; specify minimum DNSH requirements; be quantitative with thresholds where possible; build on EU labelling/certification schemes; use sustainability indicators from SFDR; be based on scientific evidence and the precautionary principle; take into account life-cycle impacts; and be easy to use and verifiable.
**Where:** Embedded in delegated acts and applied in investment classification
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The technical screening criteria established pursuant to Articles 10(3), 11(3), 12(2), 13(2), 14(2) and 15(2) shall: (a) identify the most relevant potential contributions to the given environmental objective while respecting the principle of technological neutrality, considering both the short- and long-term impact of a given economic activity;
