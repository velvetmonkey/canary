# [[Article 5]] (second subparagraph) — Disclose proportion of enabling and transitional activities within Taxonomy-aligned investments

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] [[SFDR]] financial products
**What:** Within the taxonomy-alignment disclosure, specify the proportion of investments in Taxonomy-aligned activities as a percentage of all investments, including a breakdown between enabling activities ([[Article 16]]) and transitional activities ([[Article 10(2)]]).
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures and periodic reports
**Deadline:** From 1 January 2022 (objectives (a)-(b)); from 1 January 2023 (objectives (c)-(f))
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The description referred to in point (b) of the first subparagraph of this Article shall specify the proportion of investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities selected for the financial product, including details on the proportions of enabling and transitional activities referred to in [[Article 16]] and [[Article 10(2)]], respectively, as a percentage of all investments selected for the financial product.
