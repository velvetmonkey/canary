# Article 3 — Four cumulative criteria for Taxonomy-aligned economic activities

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and any entity seeking to classify an economic activity as environmentally sustainable
**What:** An economic activity qualifies as environmentally sustainable only if it: (a) substantially contributes to at least one of the six environmental objectives; (b) does not significantly harm any of the other objectives (DNSH); (c) is carried out in compliance with minimum social safeguards (Article 18); and (d) complies with applicable technical screening criteria established by the Commission.
**Where:** Applied in investment selection, product classification, and disclosure processes
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> an economic activity shall qualify as environmentally sustainable where that economic activity: (a) contributes substantially to one or more of the environmental objectives set out in Article 9 in accordance with Articles 10 to 16; (b) does not significantly harm any of the environmental objectives set out in Article 9 in accordance with Article 17; (c) is carried out in compliance with the minimum safeguards laid down in Article 18; and (d) complies with technical screening criteria that have been established by the Commission.
