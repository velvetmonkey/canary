# Article 25 (amending SFDR Article 9(4a)) — Article 9 SFDR products must include taxonomy disclosure in pre-contractual docs

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 9 SFDR products
**What:** Include in the pre-contractual information (Article 6(1) and (3) of SFDR) the taxonomy-related information required under Article 5 of the Taxonomy Regulation.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures under SFDR
**Deadline:** From 1 January 2022 (objectives (a)-(b)); from 1 January 2023 (objectives (c)-(f))
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall include in the information to be disclosed pursuant to Article 6(1) and (3) of this Regulation the information required under Article 5 of Regulation (EU) 2020/852.
