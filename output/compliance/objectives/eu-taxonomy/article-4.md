# Article 4 — Member States and Union must use Taxonomy criteria in sustainable finance measures

## Obligation

**Who:** Member States and the Union when adopting measures requiring financial market participants or issuers to market products as environmentally sustainable
**What:** Apply the Article 3 Taxonomy criteria as the uniform standard for determining whether an economic activity qualifies as environmentally sustainable in any national or Union measure relating to financial products or corporate bonds marketed as environmentally sustainable.
**Where:** In national and Union legislative and regulatory measures
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Member States and the Union shall apply the criteria set out in Article 3 to determine whether an economic activity qualifies as environmentally sustainable for the purposes of any measure setting out requirements for financial market participants or issuers in respect of financial products or corporate bonds that are made available as environmentally sustainable.
