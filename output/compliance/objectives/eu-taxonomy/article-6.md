# Article 6 — Taxonomy alignment disclosure for [[Article 8]] [[SFDR]] products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available financial products under [[Article 8(1)]] of [[SFDR]] that promote environmental characteristics
**What:** Apply the same taxonomy-alignment disclosure requirements as [[Article 5]] (mutatis mutandis) and accompany disclosures with a mandatory statement clarifying that the DNSH principle applies only to the portion of investments taking into account [[EU Taxonomy]] criteria, and that the remaining portion does not take those criteria into account.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures ([[Article 6(3)]] [[SFDR]]) and in periodic reports ([[Article 11(2)]] [[SFDR]])
**Deadline:** From 1 January 2022 (objectives (a)-(b) of [[Article 9]]); from 1 January 2023 (objectives (c)-(f) of [[Article 9]])
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Where a financial product as referred to in [[Article 8(1)]] of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 promotes environmental characteristics, [[Article 5]] of this Regulation shall apply mutatis mutandis.
