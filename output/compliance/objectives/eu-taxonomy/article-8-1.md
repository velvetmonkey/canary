# Article 8(1) — Non-financial undertakings: disclose Taxonomy-aligned activities in non-financial statements

## Obligation

**Who:** Undertakings subject to non-financial reporting obligations under Article 19a or Article 29a of Directive 2013/34/EU (large public-interest entities)
**What:** Include in the non-financial statement or consolidated non-financial statement information on how and to what extent the undertaking's activities are associated with Taxonomy-aligned economic activities under Articles 3 and 9 of this Regulation.
**Where:** In the non-financial statement or consolidated non-financial statement (or separate report where applicable)
**Deadline:** From 1 January 2022 (objectives (a)-(b)); from 1 January 2023 (objectives (c)-(f))
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Any undertaking which is subject to an obligation to publish non-financial information pursuant to Article 19a or Article 29a of Directive 2013/34/EU shall include in its non-financial statement or consolidated non-financial statement information on how and to what extent the undertaking's activities are associated with economic activities that qualify as environmentally sustainable under Articles 3 and 9 of this Regulation.
