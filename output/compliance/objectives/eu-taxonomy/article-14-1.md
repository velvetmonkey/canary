# Article 14(1) — Substantial contribution criteria for pollution prevention and control

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and undertakings assessing taxonomy alignment for pollution prevention objective
**What:** An economic activity contributes substantially to pollution prevention and control if it: prevents or reduces pollutant emissions into air, water or land (other than GHGs); improves air, water or soil quality; prevents or minimises adverse impacts of chemicals production/use/disposal; cleans up litter and pollution; or enables such activities.
**Where:** Applied in investment analysis and taxonomy alignment assessments
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> An economic activity shall qualify as contributing substantially to pollution prevention and control where that activity contributes substantially to environmental protection from pollution by: (a) preventing or, where that is not practicable, reducing pollutant emissions into air, water or land, other than greenhouse gasses.
