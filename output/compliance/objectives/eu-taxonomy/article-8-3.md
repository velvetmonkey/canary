# Article 8(3) — Taxonomy KPI disclosures to be published in separate non-financial report where applicable

## Obligation

**Who:** Undertakings subject to non-financial reporting under Directive 2013/34/EU that publish a separate non-financial report
**What:** Where the non-financial information is published in a separate report (as permitted under Article 19a(4) or 29a(4) of Directive 2013/34/EU), the Taxonomy-related information (Articles 8(1) and 8(2)) must also be published in that separate report.
**Where:** Separate non-financial report
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> If an undertaking publishes non-financial information pursuant to Article 19a or Article 29a of Directive 2013/34/EU in a separate report in accordance with Article 19a(4) or Article 29a(4) of that Directive, the information referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 of this Article shall be published in that separate report.
