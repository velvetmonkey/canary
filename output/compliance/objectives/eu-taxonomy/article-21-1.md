# Article 21(1) — Member States: ensure competent authority supervision of Taxonomy disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Member States
**What:** Ensure that the competent authorities designated under SFDR (Article 14(1)) monitor compliance of financial market participants with the Taxonomy disclosure obligations in Articles 5, 6 and 7, and that those authorities have all necessary supervisory and investigatory powers.
**Where:** National supervisory framework
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Member States shall ensure that the competent authorities referred to in Article 14(1) of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 monitor the compliance of financial market participants with the requirements laid down in Articles 5, 6 and 7 of this Regulation.
