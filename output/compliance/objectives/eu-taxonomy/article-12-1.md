# Article 12(1) — Substantial contribution criteria for water and marine resources

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and undertakings assessing taxonomy alignment for water/marine objective
**What:** An economic activity contributes substantially to sustainable use and protection of water and marine resources if it contributes to achieving or maintaining good status of surface/groundwater bodies or good environmental status of marine waters, through activities such as waste water treatment, drinking water protection, water efficiency improvements, sustainable marine ecosystem use, or enabling such activities.
**Where:** Applied in investment analysis and taxonomy alignment assessments
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> An economic activity shall qualify as contributing substantially to the sustainable use and protection of water and marine resources where that activity either contributes substantially to achieving the good status of bodies of water, including bodies of surface water and groundwater or to preventing the deterioration of bodies of water that already have good status, or contributes substantially to achieving the good environmental status of marine waters.
