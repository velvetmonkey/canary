# Article 26(1) — Commission review of Taxonomy Regulation application

## Obligation

**Who:** Commission
**What:** Publish a report by 13 July 2022 and every three years thereafter evaluating: progress on technical screening criteria, possible need to revise Article 3 criteria, use of the environmentally sustainable investment definition in Union and Member State law, effectiveness in channelling private investment, data access for financial market participants, and application of Articles 21 and 22.
**Where:** Published Commission report
**Deadline:** By 13 July 2022, then every 3 years
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> By 13 July 2022, and subsequently every three years thereafter, the Commission shall publish a report on the application of this Regulation.
