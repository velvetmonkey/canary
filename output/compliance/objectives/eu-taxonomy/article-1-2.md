# Article 1(2) — Scope: regulation applies to financial market participants and NFRD undertakings

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available financial products; undertakings subject to NFRD (Article 19a or 29a of Directive 2013/34/EU); Member States and the Union adopting sustainable finance measures
**What:** Understand and confirm whether the Taxonomy Regulation applies to your entity. It applies to: (a) national/EU measures setting requirements for financial market participants or issuers regarding environmentally sustainable financial products/bonds; (b) financial market participants making available financial products; (c) large undertakings subject to non-financial reporting obligations.
**Where:** Across all relevant business activities and regulatory compliance frameworks
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> This Regulation applies to: (a) measures adopted by Member States or by the Union that set out requirements for financial market participants or issuers in respect of financial products or corporate bonds that are made available as environmentally sustainable; (b) financial market participants that make available financial products; (c) undertakings which are subject to the obligation to publish a non-financial statement or a consolidated non-financial statement pursuant to Article 19a or Article 29a of Directive 2013/34/EU.
