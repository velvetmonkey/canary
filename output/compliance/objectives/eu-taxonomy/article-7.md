# Article 7 — Mandatory 'does not consider Taxonomy' statement for other financial products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available financial products not subject to [[Article 8(1)]] or [[Article 9(1)]], (2) or (3) of [[SFDR]]
**What:** Include a prescribed statement in pre-contractual and periodic disclosures explicitly stating that the investments underlying the financial product do not take into account the EU criteria for environmentally sustainable economic activities.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures and periodic reports, alongside information required under sectoral legislation referred to in Articles 6(3) and 11(2) of [[SFDR]]
**Deadline:** From 1 January 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> the information to be disclosed in accordance with the provisions of sectoral legislation referred to in Articles 6(3) and 11(2) of that Regulation shall be accompanied by the following statement: 'The investments underlying this financial product do not take into account the EU criteria for environmentally sustainable economic activities.'
