# Schedule 1, Part 1 — Revocation of EU Taxonomy Regulation framework

## Obligation

**Who:** Asset managers and financial market participants previously subject to [[EU Taxonomy]] disclosures
**What:** Regulation (EU) 2020/852 ([[EU Taxonomy]]) is revoked as retained UK law. Firms must stop making [[EU Taxonomy]]-based disclosures as a matter of UK legal obligation and await replacement UK green taxonomy rules from the [[FCA]] and Treasury.
**Where:** Fund documentation, pre-contractual disclosures, periodic reports, and websites
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Regulation (EU) 2020/852 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 18 June 2020 on the establishment of a framework to facilitate sustainable investment

*Schedule 1, Part 1, Financial Services and Markets Act 2023 (FSMA 2023)* [verified]
