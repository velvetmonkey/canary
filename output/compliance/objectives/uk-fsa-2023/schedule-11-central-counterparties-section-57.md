# Schedule 11 — Central counterparties (Section 57) — CCP special resolution regime compliance and notification obligations

## Obligation

**Who:** Recognised central counterparties (CCPs), their clearing members, and the Bank of England
**What:** CCPs and their clearing members must comply with the new special resolution regime. The Bank of England has powers to remove directors, appoint temporary managers, make stabilisation instruments, and restrict payments. CCPs must publish resolution instruments on their websites and cooperate with Bank of England resolution actions.
**Where:** In CCP rulebooks, governance documents, and published on CCP websites
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> and arrange for the publication of a copy on the website of the CCP in respect of which the instrument was made.
