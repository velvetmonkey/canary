# Schedule 10, Part 1 — New Chapter 2A of Part 18 of FSMA 2000 — Senior manager approval for FMI functions

## Obligation

**Who:** Relevant recognised bodies (exchanges, CCPs, CSDs) and their senior managers and directors
**What:** Persons performing senior management functions at relevant recognised bodies must obtain regulatory approval. Firms must certify employees performing qualifying functions and ensure rules of conduct are applied, with disciplinary action available for breaches. This extends a senior managers regime to financial market infrastructure.
**Where:** Internal governance frameworks, HR processes, and regulatory filings
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis
