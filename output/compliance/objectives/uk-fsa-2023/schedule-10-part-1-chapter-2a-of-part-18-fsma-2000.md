# Schedule 10, Part 1 — Chapter 2A of Part 18 FSMA 2000 — Senior managers approval and conduct rules for FMI functions

## Obligation

**Who:** Relevant recognised bodies (exchanges, CCPs, CSDs) and their senior managers and employees
**What:** Senior managers and employees performing qualifying functions at recognised bodies must be approved and are subject to conduct rules. Firms must certify employees, maintain rules of conduct, and ensure senior management functions are properly designated and approved by the appropriate regulator.
**Where:** In internal governance frameworks, HR processes, and regulatory submissions
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis
