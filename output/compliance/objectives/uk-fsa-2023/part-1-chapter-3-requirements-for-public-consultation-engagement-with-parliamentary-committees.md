# Part 1, Chapter 3 — Requirements for public consultation / Engagement with Parliamentary Committees — Participate in regulator public consultations and accountability processes

## Obligation

**Who:** FCA and PRA regulated firms, including asset managers, responding to regulatory consultations
**What:** The FCA and PRA are now subject to enhanced public consultation requirements and Parliamentary accountability obligations when making rules. Firms must monitor and engage with these consultation processes, particularly as the FCA develops replacement rules for revoked EU law (including SDR replacing [[SFDR]]), to ensure their compliance frameworks remain current.
**Where:** Regulatory consultation responses, governance and compliance monitoring processes
**Materiality:** medium
