# Part 1, Chapter 2 — Designated activities regime / Schedule 3 (new Schedule 6B to FSMA 2000) — Register and comply with designated activities regime

## Obligation

**Who:** Persons carrying on designated activities including use of benchmarks, short selling, securitisation, and derivatives
**What:** Firms carrying on activities designated under the new regime (including using benchmarks, short selling, and derivatives) must comply with rules made by the FCA under the designated activities regime, replacing the previous EU-derived regulatory obligations in those areas.
**Where:** Operational processes, trading systems, and compliance frameworks
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> "designated activity" has the meaning given in section 71K;
