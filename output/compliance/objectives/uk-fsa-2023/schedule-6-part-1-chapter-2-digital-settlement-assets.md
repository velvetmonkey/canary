# Schedule 6 / Part 1, Chapter 2 — Digital settlement assets — Comply with digital settlement asset regulatory framework

## Obligation

**Who:** Digital settlement asset exchange providers, payment system operators, and firms using digital settlement assets
**What:** Firms involved in digital settlement assets (including cryptographically secured digital representations of value used for payment settlement) must comply with the new regulatory framework under the Banking Act 2009 as amended, including recognition requirements and oversight by the Payment Systems Regulator and FCA.
**Where:** Operational systems, payment infrastructure, and regulatory filings
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> "digital settlement asset" means a digital representation of value or rights, whether or not cryptographically secured, that— (a)can be used for the settlement of payment obligations, (b)can be transferred, stored or traded electronically, and (c)uses technology supporting the recording or storage of data
