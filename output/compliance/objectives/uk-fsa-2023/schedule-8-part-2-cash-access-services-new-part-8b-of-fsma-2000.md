# Schedule 8 / Part 2 — Cash access services (new Part 8B of FSMA 2000) — Designated persons to comply with cash access service obligations

## Obligation

**Who:** Designated persons (relevant current account providers and cash access service providers) under Part 8B of FSMA 2000
**What:** Firms designated under the cash access regime must comply with FCA rules on the provision of cash deposit and withdrawal services, including supervision requirements. Fees may only be charged to designated persons who are relevant current account providers.
**Where:** Branch networks, ATM infrastructure, and regulatory compliance frameworks
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> Rules made under paragraph 23 of Schedule 1ZA, in connection with the carrying out of any of the FCA's functions under this Part, must not provide for the payment of fees to the FCA by any person other than a designated person who is a relevant current account provider.
