# Part 1, Chapter 3 — FCA and PRA objectives and regulatory principles — Embed competitiveness and growth objective in regulatory engagement

## Obligation

**Who:** FCA and PRA regulated firms (including asset managers) engaging with regulators on rule-making
**What:** Firms must be aware that the FCA and PRA now have a secondary competitiveness and growth objective. When engaging in consultations or rule-making processes, firms should factor in this objective, which requires regulators to facilitate competitiveness subject to alignment with relevant international standards.
**Where:** Regulatory submissions, consultation responses, and governance frameworks
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The competitiveness and growth objective is: facilitating, subject to aligning with relevant international standards—
