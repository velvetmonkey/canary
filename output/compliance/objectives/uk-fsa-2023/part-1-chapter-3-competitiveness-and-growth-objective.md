# Part 1, Chapter 3 (Competitiveness and growth objective) — Regulators must apply competitiveness and growth objective

## Obligation

**Who:** [[FCA]] and [[PRA]] (and indirectly regulated firms engaging with rule-making processes)
**What:** The [[FCA]] and [[PRA]] must apply a new secondary competitiveness and growth objective when making rules and exercising functions. Firms should factor this objective into regulatory engagement, consultation responses, and when seeking waivers or modifications, as it may affect the shape of future UK sustainability and other rules.
**Where:** Regulatory rule-making, supervisory decisions, and policy consultations
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis
