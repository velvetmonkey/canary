# Part 1, Chapter 3 — Competitiveness and growth objective — Align with FCA/PRA competitiveness and growth objective in rule compliance

## Obligation

**Who:** Regulated firms engaging with [[FCA]] and [[PRA]] rule-making and supervisory processes
**What:** The [[FCA]] and [[PRA]] now have a secondary competitiveness and growth objective. Firms participating in consultations or seeking regulatory approvals must engage with this objective, and compliance frameworks should reflect that regulators will weigh competitiveness considerations when making and applying rules.
**Where:** In regulatory engagement, governance frameworks, and responses to [[FCA]]/[[PRA]] consultations
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis
