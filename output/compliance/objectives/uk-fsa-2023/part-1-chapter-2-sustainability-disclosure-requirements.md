# Part 1, Chapter 2 — Sustainability disclosure requirements — Comply with new UK Sustainability Disclosure Requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and asset managers operating in the UK
**What:** Firms must comply with the new UK Sustainability Disclosure Requirements ([[SDR]]) framework that replaces [[SFDR]] and the [[EU Taxonomy Regulation]]. The [[FCA]] is empowered to make rules on sustainability-related disclosures; firms must monitor and implement those rules as they are published.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures, on websites, and in periodic reports as specified by [[FCA]] rules
**Materiality:** high
