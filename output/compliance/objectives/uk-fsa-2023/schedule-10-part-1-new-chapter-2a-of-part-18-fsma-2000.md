# Schedule 10, Part 1 (new Chapter 2A of Part 18 FSMA 2000) — Senior managers of recognised bodies require FCA/PRA approval

## Obligation

**Who:** Recognised bodies (investment exchanges, CCPs, CSDs) and their senior managers and directors
**What:** Persons performing senior management functions at relevant recognised bodies must obtain approval from the appropriate regulator ([[FCA]] or Bank of England) before performing those functions. Recognised bodies must submit applications for approval and ensure unapproved individuals do not perform designated senior management functions.
**Where:** Internal governance frameworks, regulatory applications, and HR/onboarding processes of recognised bodies
**Materiality:** medium
