# Schedule 8, Part 1 — Cash access services (Section 54) — Designated persons to comply with FCA cash access rules

## Obligation

**Who:** Designated persons (relevant current account providers and others designated under Part 8B [[FSMA 2000]])
**What:** Firms designated under the new cash access regime must comply with [[FCA]] rules on the provision of cash deposit and withdrawal services. The [[FCA]] may impose requirements and use disciplinary measures for non-compliance. Designated firms must pay fees to the [[FCA]] in connection with the regime.
**Where:** In branch and ATM networks, cash access policies, and [[FCA]] regulatory submissions
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> Rules made under paragraph 23 of Schedule 1ZA, in connection with the carrying out of any of the [[FCA]]'s functions under this Part, must not provide for the payment of fees to the [[FCA]] by any person other than a designated person who is a relevant current account provider.
