# Part 1, Chapter 1 — Revocation of retained EU law — Transition away from all revoked retained EU financial services law

## Obligation

**Who:** All regulated firms, including financial market participants and financial advisers
**What:** A broad sweep of retained EU financial services legislation (listed in [[Schedule 1]]) ceases to have effect as domestic law. Firms must audit their compliance frameworks, contracts, and disclosures to identify all dependencies on revoked instruments and replace them with [[FCA]]/[[PRA]] rules or new UK legislation.
**Where:** Across all internal policies, client-facing documentation, and regulatory submissions
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> cease to be so recognised and available in domestic law.
