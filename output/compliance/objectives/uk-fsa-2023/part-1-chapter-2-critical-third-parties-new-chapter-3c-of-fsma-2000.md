# Part 1, Chapter 2 — Critical third parties (new Chapter 3C of FSMA 2000) — Critical third-party oversight and written notice obligations

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and FMI entities using services of designated critical third parties
**What:** Where a relevant regulator considers a critical third party has contravened a requirement, it may publish a statement to that effect. Firms relying on critical third parties must ensure contractual and operational arrangements account for the possibility of regulatory intervention, written notices, and published statements of contravention.
**Where:** Operational risk frameworks, third-party contracts, and governance documentation
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> If a relevant regulator considers that a critical third party has contravened a requirement imposed by or under this Chapter the regulator may publish a statement to that effect.
