# Part 1, Chapter 2 — Critical third parties (Chapter 3C FSMA 2000) — Critical third party oversight and written notice obligations

## Obligation

**Who:** Relevant regulators ([[FCA]], [[PRA]], Bank of England) and designated critical third parties providing services to financial firms
**What:** Where a regulator considers a critical third party has contravened a requirement, it may publish a statement to that effect and must give the critical third party written notice. Firms that are or use critical third parties must maintain oversight arrangements and be prepared for regulatory intervention and public disclosure.
**Where:** In operational risk frameworks, third-party management policies, and regulatory correspondence
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> If a relevant regulator considers that a critical third party has contravened a requirement imposed by or under this Chapter the regulator may publish a statement to that effect.
