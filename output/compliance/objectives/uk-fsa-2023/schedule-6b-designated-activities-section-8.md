# Schedule 6B — Designated activities (Section 8) — Comply with designated activities regime for derivatives, short selling, and benchmarks

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants conducting designated activities including use of benchmarks, short selling, securitisation, and derivatives
**What:** Firms conducting activities designated under the new regime (including using benchmarks, short selling, securitisation, and derivatives) must comply with [[FCA]] rules made under the designated activities regime, even if they are not otherwise [[FCA]]-authorised. Asset managers using benchmarks in [[Article 8]]/9 fund strategies must ensure compliance.
**Where:** In trading, investment management, and fund operations
**Materiality:** medium
