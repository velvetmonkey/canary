# Article 9(1) — Describe adherence to international standards and Paris Agreement alignment in PAI statement

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants publishing a PAI statement
**What:** Describe whether and to what extent the firm adheres to responsible business conduct codes and internationally recognised due diligence standards, and (where relevant) the degree of alignment with the Paris Agreement objectives, including methodology, data, forward-looking climate scenarios used.
**Where:** In the section 'References to international standards' in Table 1 of Annex I on the website
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> financial market participants shall describe whether and to what extent they adhere to responsible business conduct codes and internationally recognised standards for due diligence and reporting and, where relevant, the degree of their alignment with the objectives of the Paris Agreement.
