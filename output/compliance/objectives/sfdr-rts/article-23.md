# Article 23 — Publish product sustainability disclosures in dedicated website section

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** For each financial product, publish [[Article 10(1)]] [[SFDR]] website information in a separate section titled 'Sustainability-related disclosures', in the same part of the website as other product information including marketing communications, clearly identifying the product and prominently displaying its E/S characteristics or sustainable investment objective.
**Where:** On the product's website page, in a separate section titled 'Sustainability-related disclosures'
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall, for each financial product, publish the information referred to in [[Article 10(1)]] of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 in a separate section titled, 'Sustainability-related disclosures', in the same part of their website as the other information relating to the financial product, including marketing communications.
