# Article 25(1)–(2) — Website Summary Section for Article 8 Funds (Max 2 A4 Pages, Multi-Language)

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 products
**What:** Publish a summary of all website sustainability disclosures for the product, maximum two sides of A4. Provide the summary in: (a) the home Member State official language (and English if different and product is cross-border); and (b) the official language of any host Member State where the product is distributed.
**Where:** On the product website, section 'Summary'
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the website section 'Summary' referred to in Article 24, point (a), financial market participants shall summarise all the information contained in the different sections referred to in that Article about the financial products that promote environmental or social characteristics. The summary section shall have a maximum length of two sides of A4-sized paper when printed.
