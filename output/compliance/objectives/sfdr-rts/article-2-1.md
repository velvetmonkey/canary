# Article 2(1) — Present all disclosures in accessible, non-discriminatory, concise and non-misleading manner

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and financial advisers
**What:** Provide all required information free of charge, in an easily accessible, non-discriminatory, prominent, simple, concise, comprehensible, fair, clear and not misleading manner, using readable characters and a style that facilitates understanding.
**Where:** All disclosure channels (websites, pre-contractual documents, periodic reports)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants and financial advisers shall provide the information required by this Regulation free of charge and in a manner that is easily accessible, non-discriminatory, prominent, simple, concise, comprehensible, fair, clear and not misleading.
