# Article 15(1) — Disclose EU Taxonomy alignment with pie charts in Article 8 pre-contractual docs

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 products that promote environmental characteristics (Article 6 first subparagraph of Taxonomy Regulation)
**What:** Include in the pre-contractual Annex II template two pie charts showing: (i) the degree of investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities (including sovereigns) and (ii) the same excluding sovereign exposures, plus a description of those investments and explanation of any non-taxonomy-aligned investments.
**Where:** In the section 'To what minimum extent are sustainable investments with an environmental objective aligned with the EU Taxonomy?' in the Annex II pre-contractual template
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> financial market participants shall provide, in the section 'To what minimum extent are sustainable investments with an environmental objective aligned with the EU Taxonomy?' in the template set out in Annex II, all of the following: (a) a graphical representation in the form of a pie chart of: (i) the degree to which the aggregated investments are investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities, as calculated in accordance with Article 17(1) to (4) of this Regulation.
