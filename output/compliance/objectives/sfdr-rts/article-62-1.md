# Article 62(1) — Disclose EU Taxonomy alignment with bar charts in Article 9 periodic reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 9 products investing in environmentally sustainable economic activities
**What:** Include in the periodic Annex V template bar charts showing actual investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities (with and without sovereign exposures), a breakdown by environmental objective, and a historical comparison.
**Where:** In the section 'What was the proportion of sustainability-related investments?' in the Annex V periodic report template
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For the financial products referred to in Article 5, first paragraph, of Regulation (EU) 2020/852, the section 'What was the proportion of sustainability-related investments?' in the template set out in Annex V shall contain all of the following information: (a) a breakdown in accordance with Article 55(1), point (a).
