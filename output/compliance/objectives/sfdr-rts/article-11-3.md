# Article 11(3) — Financial advisers: PAI statement content — product selection process

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial advisers that consider PAIs in their investment or insurance advice
**What:** The PAI statement published by financial advisers must describe the process used to select financial products, including: how they use PAI information from financial market participants; whether and how they rank/select products based on PAI indicators; and any PAI-based criteria or thresholds used to select or advise on financial products.
**Where:** On the financial adviser's website, in the dedicated PAI statement section
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The statement and information referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 shall contain details on the process used by financial advisers to select the financial products they advise on, including all of the following: (a) how the financial advisers use the information published by financial market participants pursuant to this Regulation.
