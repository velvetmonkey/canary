# Article 55(1) — Disclose EU Taxonomy alignment with bar charts in Article 8 periodic reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 products that committed to investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities
**What:** Include in the periodic Annex IV template bar charts showing actual investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities (with and without sovereign exposures), a breakdown by environmental objective, auditor/third-party assurance details, and a historical comparison.
**Where:** In the section 'What was the proportion of sustainability-related investments?' in the Annex IV periodic report template
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> the section 'What was the proportion of sustainability-related investments?' in the template set out in Annex IV shall contain all of the following information: (a) a breakdown of the proportion of the investments per each of the environmental objectives set out in Article 9 of Regulation (EU) 2020/852 to which those investments contributed.
