# Article 17(1)–(5) — Prescribed formula for calculating Taxonomy-aligned investment degree

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 or Article 9 funds with environmental characteristics
**What:** Calculate the degree of investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities using the prescribed formula (market value of taxonomy-aligned investments divided by market value of all investments). The numerator must include equities/debt of investee companies, green bonds, real estate, infrastructure, securitisation and investments in other financial products. Derivatives must be excluded from the numerator. A separate calculation excluding sovereign exposures must also be made.
**Where:** Underlying calculation methodology for pre-contractual and periodic disclosures
**Materiality:** high
