# [[Article 2(1)]] and [[Article 2(3)]] — General Presentation Standards and Website Currency

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and financial advisers
**What:** All required information must be provided free of charge, in a manner that is easily accessible, non-discriminatory, prominent, simple, concise, comprehensible, fair, clear and not misleading, using readable characters. Website information must be kept up to date, with the publication date and any update date clearly stated; downloadable files must indicate version history in the file name.
**Where:** All disclosure channels (websites, pre-contractual documents, periodic reports)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants and financial advisers shall provide the information required by this Regulation free of charge and in a manner that is easily accessible, non-discriminatory, prominent, simple, concise, comprehensible, fair, clear and not misleading... Financial market participants and financial advisers shall keep the information published on their websites in accordance with this Regulation up to date. They shall clearly mention the date of publication of the information and the date of any update.
