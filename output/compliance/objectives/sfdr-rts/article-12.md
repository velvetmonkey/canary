# Article 12 — Non-Considering FMPs Must Publish Prominent Opt-Out Statement

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants that do not consider PAIs ([[Article 4(1)(b)]] [[SFDR]])
**What:** Publish a prominent statement on the website in a dedicated section titled 'No consideration of adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors', explaining why PAIs are not considered and whether/when the FMP intends to consider them.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website (dedicated section)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants referred to in [[Article 4(1)]], point (b), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 shall publish the information referred to in [[Article 4(1)]], point (b), of that Regulation in a separate section of their website titled 'No consideration of adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors'.
