# Article 49(1) — Describe attainment of sustainable investment objective and benchmark alignment on [[Article 9]] website

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] financial products with a designated reference benchmark
**What:** Describe how the designated index is aligned with the sustainable investment objective (input data, methodologies, rebalancing, calculation). For carbon emission reduction products, confirm the benchmark qualifies as EU Climate Transition or Paris-aligned Benchmark and provide a hyperlink to the methodology.
**Where:** On the product website, in the 'Attainment of the sustainable investment objective' section
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> financial market participants shall describe all of the following: (a) for financial products that have sustainable investment as their objective and for which an index has been designated as a reference benchmark, how that index is aligned with the sustainable investment objective of the financial product, including the input data, the methodologies used to select those data, the rebalancing methodologies and how the index is calculated.
