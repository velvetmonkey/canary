# Article 26(2) — Explain DNSH and minimum safeguards for Article 8 products making sustainable investments

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products that commit to making sustainable investments
**What:** Explain on the website how sustainable investments do not significantly harm any sustainable investment objective, including how PAI indicators are taken into account and alignment with OECD Guidelines and UN Guiding Principles.
**Where:** On the product website, in the 'No sustainable investment objective' section
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Where the financial product commits to making one or more sustainable investments, financial market participants shall in the website section 'No sustainable investment objective' referred to in Article 24, point (b), explain how the sustainable investment does not significantly harm any of the sustainable investment objectives, including all of the following: (a) how the indicators for adverse impacts in Table 1 of Annex I, and any relevant indicators in Tables 2 and 3 of that Annex I, are taken into account.
