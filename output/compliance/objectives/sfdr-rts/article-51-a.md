# Article 51(a) — Report on attainment of E/S characteristics and sustainability indicators in periodic report

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] financial products
**What:** In the periodic report, disclose the extent to which E/S characteristics were met during the period, including the performance of sustainability indicators used to measure each characteristic and which derivatives (if any) were used.
**Where:** In the section 'To what extent were the environmental and/or social characteristics promoted by this financial product met?' in the Annex IV periodic report template
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> the extent to which the environmental or social characteristics promoted by the financial product were met during the period covered by the periodic report, including the performance of the sustainability indicators used to measure how each of those environmental or social characteristics are met and which derivatives, if any, have been used to meet those environmental or social characteristics.
