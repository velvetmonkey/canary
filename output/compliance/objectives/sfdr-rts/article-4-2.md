# Article 4(2) — PAI statement must use prescribed Annex I template

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** The PAI statement must be presented using the standardised template set out in Table 1 of Annex I to this Regulation.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall publish the statement referred to in paragraph 1 in the format of the template set out in Table 1 of Annex I.
