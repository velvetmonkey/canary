# [[Article 11(1)]]-(3) — Financial advisers: publish PAI statement on website with product selection process details

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial advisers applying [[Article 4(5)(a)]] of [[SFDR]]
**What:** Publish a PAI statement on the website (titled 'Statement on principal adverse impacts of insurance/investment advice on sustainability factors') describing how PAI information from financial market participants is used, whether products are ranked/selected based on PAI indicators, and any PAI-based criteria or thresholds used to select or advise on products.
**Where:** On the website, in a separate section titled 'Statement on principal adverse impacts of [insurance/investment] advice on sustainability factors'
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The statement and information referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 shall contain details on the process used by financial advisers to select the financial products they advise on, including all of the following: (a) how the financial advisers use the information published by financial market participants pursuant to this Regulation; (b) whether the financial advisers rank and select financial products based on the indicators listed in Table 1 of Annex I.
