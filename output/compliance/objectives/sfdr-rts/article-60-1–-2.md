# Article 60(1)–(2) — Top 15 investments disclosure in Article 9 periodic reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 9 financial products
**What:** In the periodic report Annex V template, list the 15 largest investments (by proportion) during the reporting period in descending order, including sector and country. If fewer than 15 investments account for 50% of the portfolio, list only those investments.
**Where:** In periodic reports (Annex V template), section 'What were the top investments of this financial product?'
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'What were the top investments of this financial product?' in the template set out in Annex V, financial market participants shall list, in descending order of size, the fifteen investments constituting the largest proportion of investments of the financial product during the period covered by the periodic report, including the sectors and countries of those investments.
