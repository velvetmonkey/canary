# Article 32 — Website: data sources and processing disclosure for [[Article 8]] products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] financial products
**What:** In the website 'Data sources and processing' section, describe: the data sources used to attain each E/S characteristic; measures taken to ensure data quality; how data are processed; and the proportion of data that are estimated.
**Where:** In the 'Data sources and processing' section of the website for [[Article 8]] products
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the website section 'Data sources and processing' referred to in [[Article 24]], point (h), financial market participants shall describe all of the following: (a) the data sources used to attain each of the environmental or social characteristics promoted by the financial product; (b) the measures taken to ensure data quality; (c) how data are processed; (d) the proportion of data that are estimated.
