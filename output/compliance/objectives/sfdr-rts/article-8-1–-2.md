# Article 8(1)–(2) — PAI statement engagement policies section

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants publishing a PAI statement
**What:** In the PAI statement 'Engagement policies' section, provide brief summaries of: (a) shareholder engagement policies under Article 3g of the Shareholder Rights Directive; and (b) any other engagement policies to reduce PAI. Describe the PAI indicators considered in those policies and how policies will be adapted if PAI are not reduced over more than one reporting period.
**Where:** PAI statement on website, 'Engagement policies' section in Table 1 of Annex I
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'Engagement policies' in Table 1 of Annex I, financial market participants shall provide all of the following information:
(a)
where applicable, brief summaries of the engagement policies referred to in Article 3g of Directive 2007/36/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council
;
(b)
brief summaries of any other engagement policies to reduce principal adverse impacts.
