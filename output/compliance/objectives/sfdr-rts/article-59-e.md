# Article 59(e) — Explain DNSH and minimum safeguards for sustainable investments in [[Article 9]] periodic report

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] financial products
**What:** In the periodic report, explain how sustainable investments contributed to a sustainable investment objective and did not significantly harm any of those objectives, including how PAI indicators were taken into account and alignment with OECD Guidelines and UN Guiding Principles.
**Where:** In the section 'To what extent was the sustainable investment objective of this financial product met?' in the Annex V periodic report template
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> an explanation of how the sustainable investments have contributed to a sustainable investment objective and have not harmed significantly any of the sustainable investment objectives during the period covered by the periodic report, including all of the following: (i) how the indicators for adverse impacts in Table 1 of Annex I, and any relevant indicators in Tables 2 and 3 of that Annex, were taken into account.
