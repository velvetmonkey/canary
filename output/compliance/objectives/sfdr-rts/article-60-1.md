# Article 60(1) — Disclose top 15 investments in Article 9 periodic report

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 9 financial products
**What:** List in descending order the 15 investments constituting the largest proportion of the financial product during the reporting period, including sector and country. If fewer than 15 investments make up 50% of the portfolio, list only those.
**Where:** In the section 'What were the top investments of this financial product?' in the Annex V periodic report template
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> financial market participants shall list, in descending order of size, the fifteen investments constituting the largest proportion of investments of the financial product during the period covered by the periodic report, including the sectors and countries of those investments.
