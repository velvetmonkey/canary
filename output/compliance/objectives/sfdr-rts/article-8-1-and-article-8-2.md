# [[Article 8(1)]] and [[Article 8(2)]] — Disclose Engagement Policies in PAI Statement

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants publishing a PAI statement
**What:** In the 'Engagement policies' section of the PAI statement, provide brief summaries of: (a) shareholder engagement policies under Article 3g of the Shareholder Rights Directive (where applicable); and (b) any other engagement policies aimed at reducing principal adverse impacts. Summaries must describe which PAI indicators are addressed and how policies will be adapted if PAIs do not reduce over more than one reporting period.
**Where:** In the 'Engagement policies' section of Table 1, Annex I
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'Engagement policies' in Table 1 of Annex I, financial market participants shall provide all of the following information: (a) where applicable, brief summaries of the engagement policies referred to in Article 3g of Directive 2007/36/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council; (b) brief summaries of any other engagement policies to reduce principal adverse impacts.
