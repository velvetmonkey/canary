# Article 17(3) — Use turnover as default KPI for non-financial undertakings in Taxonomy calculation

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 or Article 9 products
**What:** When calculating Taxonomy alignment for investments in non-financial undertakings, use turnover as the default key performance indicator for all such companies. Capital expenditure or operating expenditure may only be used where the features of the financial product justify it.
**Where:** In the Taxonomy alignment calculation underlying pre-contractual and periodic disclosures
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> the calculation referred to in paragraph 2 shall use turnover as the same type of key performance indicator for all non-financial undertakings.
