# Article 8(1)–(2) — Engagement policies in PAI statement

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants publishing a PAI statement
**What:** Include in the PAI statement brief summaries of engagement policies (including shareholder engagement under Article 3g of the Shareholder Rights Directive and any other engagement policies), describing which PAI indicators are addressed and how policies will be adapted if PAI do not reduce over time.
**Where:** In the 'Engagement policies' section of the PAI statement on the website (Table 1 of Annex I)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'Engagement policies' in Table 1 of Annex I, financial market participants shall provide all of the following information: (a) where applicable, brief summaries of the engagement policies referred to in Article 3g of Directive 2007/36/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council ; (b) brief summaries of any other engagement policies to reduce principal adverse impacts.
