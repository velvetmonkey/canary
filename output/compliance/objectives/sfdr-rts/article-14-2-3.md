# Article 14(2)-(3) — Prominent Statement in Main Body Referencing Annex II

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products
**What:** Include in the main body of the pre-contractual document a prominent statement that E/S characteristics information is available in the annex, and at the beginning of the annex state whether the product intends to make sustainable investments and confirm it promotes E/S characteristics without having sustainable investment as its objective.
**Where:** In pre-contractual documents (main body and beginning of Annex II)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall include in the main body of the documents or information referred to in Article 6(3) of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 a prominent statement that information about the environmental or social characteristics is available in the annex to those documents or that information. Financial market participants shall provide at the beginning of the annex to the documents or the information referred to in Article 6(3) of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 all of the following information: (a) whether the financial product intends to make any sustainable investments; (b) whether the financial product promotes environmental or social characteristics, without having as its objective a sustainable investment.
