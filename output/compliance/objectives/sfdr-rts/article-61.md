# Article 61 — Disclose proportion of sustainability-related investments in Article 9 periodic reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 9 financial products
**What:** In the periodic report, disclose: the proportion of investments contributing to the sustainable investment objective, the purpose of remaining investments (including minimum E/S safeguards, hedging, ancillary liquidity), and the proportion of investments in different economic sectors and sub-sectors.
**Where:** In periodic reports (Annex V template, 'proportion of sustainability-related investments' section)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'What was the proportion of sustainability-related investments?' in the template set out in Annex V, financial market participants shall provide all of the following information: (a) the proportions of the investments of the financial product that contributed to the sustainable investment objective; (b) the purpose of the remainder of the investments during the period covered by the periodic report.
