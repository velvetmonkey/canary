# Article 8(1) — Describe engagement policies in PAI statement

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants publishing a PAI statement
**What:** In the PAI statement, provide brief summaries of engagement policies (including shareholder engagement under Article 3g of Directive 2007/36/EC and other engagement policies), describing which PAI indicators are addressed and how policies will be adapted if PAIs are not reduced over more than one period.
**Where:** In the 'Engagement policies' section in Table 1 of Annex I, on websites
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'Engagement policies' in Table 1 of Annex I, financial market participants shall provide all of the following information:
(a)
where applicable, brief summaries of the engagement policies referred to in Article 3g of Directive 2007/36/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council ;
(b)
brief summaries of any other engagement policies to reduce principal adverse impacts.
