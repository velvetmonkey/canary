# Article 51(d) — Explain DNSH and minimum safeguards for sustainable investments in Article 8 periodic report

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products that committed to making sustainable investments
**What:** In the periodic report, explain how sustainable investments contributed to sustainable investment objectives and did not significantly harm any of those objectives, including how PAI indicators were taken into account and whether investments were aligned with OECD Guidelines and UN Guiding Principles.
**Where:** In the section 'To what extent were the environmental and/or social characteristics promoted by this financial product met?' in the Annex IV periodic report template
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> for financial products that included a commitment to make sustainable investments, an explanation of how those investments have contributed to the sustainable investment objectives referred to in Article 2, point (17), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 and have not harmed significantly any of those objectives during the period covered by the periodic report.
