# Article 19(1) — Disclose EU Taxonomy alignment with pie charts in Article 9 pre-contractual docs

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 9 products investing in environmentally sustainable economic activities (Article 5 first paragraph of Taxonomy Regulation)
**What:** Include in the pre-contractual Annex III template two pie charts showing the degree of investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities (with and without sovereign exposures), plus a description of those investments.
**Where:** In the section 'What is the asset allocation and the minimum share of sustainable investments?' in the Annex III pre-contractual template
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For the financial products referred to in Article 5, first paragraph, of Regulation (EU) 2020/852, financial market participants shall include in the section 'What is the asset allocation and the minimum share of sustainable investments?' in the template set out in Annex III all of the following information: (a) a graphical representation in the form of a pie chart: (i) in accordance with Article 15(1), point (a)(i), of this Regulation.
