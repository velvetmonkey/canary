# Article 4(1) and Article 4(2) — Annual PAI Statement Publication by 30 June

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants considering principal adverse impacts (Article 4(1)(a) or Article 4(3)/(4) of SFDR)
**What:** Publish annually by 30 June a 'Statement on principal adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors' on the website, covering the period 1 January to 31 December of the preceding year, using the mandatory template in Table 1 of Annex I. The statement must be placed in a dedicated section titled accordingly within the 'Sustainability-related disclosures' section.
**Where:** On the firm's website, in a separate section titled 'Statement on principal adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors'
**Deadline:** 30 June each year
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> By 30 June each year, the financial market participants referred to in Article 4(1), point (a), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088, or Article 4(3) or Article 4(4) of that Regulation, shall publish on their website, in a separate section titled: 'Statement on principal adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors', the information referred to in Article 4(1), point (a), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088, in Article 4(2), (3) and (4) of that Regulation, and in Articles 4 to 10 of this Regulation.
