# Article 17(5) — Calculate Taxonomy alignment excluding sovereign exposures as separate metric

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 or Article 9 products
**What:** In addition to the standard Taxonomy alignment calculation, calculate and disclose a separate figure excluding sovereign exposures from both the numerator and denominator of the Taxonomy alignment formula.
**Where:** Used in pre-contractual and periodic disclosures (Annexes II, III, IV, V)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> For the disclosures referred to in Article 15(1), point (a)(ii), Article 19(1), point (a)(ii), Article 55(1), point (b)(iii) and Article 62(1), point (b)(iii), paragraphs 1 to 4 of this Article shall apply, except that the sovereign exposures shall be excluded from the calculation of the numerator and of the denominator of the formula contained in paragraph 1.
