# Article 53 — Disclose asset allocation breakdown in Article 8 periodic report

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products
**What:** In the periodic report, describe the investment breakdown: the proportion attaining E/S characteristics and the purpose of remaining investments (hedging, ancillary liquidity, insufficient data), including any minimum E/S safeguards.
**Where:** In the section 'What was the asset allocation?' in the Annex IV periodic report template
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> financial market participants shall provide a description of the investments of the financial product, including all of the following: (a) the proportions of the investments of the financial product that attained the promoted environmental or social characteristics during the period covered by the periodic report; (b) the purpose of the remainder of the investments during the period covered by the periodic report.
