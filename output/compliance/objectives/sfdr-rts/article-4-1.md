# Article 4(1) — Annual PAI statement publication on website by 30 June

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants considering principal adverse impacts under Article 4(1)(a), 4(3) or 4(4) of SFDR
**What:** Publish annually by 30 June a 'Statement on principal adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors' on the website, covering the period 1 January to 31 December of the preceding year, using the mandatory template in Table 1 of Annex I.
**Where:** On the website, in a separate section titled 'Statement on principal adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors', within the 'Sustainability-related disclosures' section
**Deadline:** 30 June each year (first publication by 30 June 2023 for the period 1 January–31 December 2022)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> By 30 June each year, the financial market participants referred to in Article 4(1), point (a), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088, or Article 4(3) or Article 4(4) of that Regulation, shall publish on their website, in a separate section titled: 'Statement on principal adverse impacts of investment decisions on sustainability factors', the information referred to in Article 4(1), point (a), of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088.
