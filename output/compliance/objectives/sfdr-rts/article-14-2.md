# Article 14(2) — Include prominent statement in main body of Article 8 pre-contractual documents

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products
**What:** Include a prominent statement in the main body of the pre-contractual document directing investors to the annex where E/S characteristics information can be found.
**Where:** In the main body of pre-contractual documents (Article 6(3) SFDR documents)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall include in the main body of the documents or information referred to in Article 6(3) of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 a prominent statement that information about the environmental or social characteristics is available in the annex to those documents or that information.
