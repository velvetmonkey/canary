# Article 50(2) — Include prominent statement in main body of [[Article 8]] periodic report

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] financial products
**What:** Include a prominent statement in the main body of the periodic report document directing investors to the annex where E/S characteristics information can be found.
**Where:** In the main body of periodic report documents ([[Article 11(2)]] [[SFDR]] documents)
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall include in the main body of the document or information referred to in [[Article 11(2)]] of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 a prominent statement that information on the environmental or social characteristics is available in that annex.
