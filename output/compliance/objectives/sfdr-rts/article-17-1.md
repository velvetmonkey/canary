# Article 17(1) — Calculate Taxonomy alignment using prescribed formula and market values

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] or [[Article 9]] products with environmental characteristics
**What:** Calculate the degree of investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities using the prescribed formula: sum of market values of taxonomy-aligned investments divided by total market value of all investments, covering equities, debt securities, real estate, infrastructure, securitisations and other financial products.
**Where:** Used in pre-contractual and periodic disclosures (Annexes II, III, IV, V)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The degree to which investments are in environmentally sustainable economic activities shall be calculated in accordance with the following formula: where 'investments of the financial product in environmentally sustainable economic activities' shall be the sum of the market values of the following investments of the financial product.
