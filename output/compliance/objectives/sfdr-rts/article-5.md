# Article 5 — PAI statement summary section requirements including multilingual obligation

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** The PAI statement summary section must include: the financial market participant's name, confirmation that PAI are considered, the reference period, and a summary of principal adverse impacts. It must be provided in the home Member State official language (and international finance language if different) and in the official language of any host Member State where products are offered. Maximum length: two sides of A4.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website (PAI statement summary section)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The summary section in Table 1 of Annex I shall be drafted in all of the following languages: (a) one of the official languages of the home Member State of the financial market participant and, where different, in an additional language customary in the sphere of international finance; (b) where a financial product of the financial market participant is made available in a host Member State, one of the official languages of that host Member State.
