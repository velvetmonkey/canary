# Article 51 — Periodic report: attainment of E/S characteristics with sustainability indicator performance

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products
**What:** Report in the periodic Annex IV template the extent to which E/S characteristics were met during the period, including performance of sustainability indicators, identification of Taxonomy environmental objectives contributed to, DNSH explanation for sustainable investments, PAI information, and a historical year-by-year comparison.
**Where:** In periodic reports (Annex IV template), section 'To what extent were the environmental and/or social characteristics promoted by this financial product met?'
**Deadline:** Annually (in periodic reports from 1 January 2023)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'To what extent were the environmental and/or social characteristics promoted by this financial product met?' in the template set out in Annex IV to this Regulation, financial market participants shall provide all of the following information: (a) the extent to which the environmental or social characteristics promoted by the financial product were met during the period covered by the periodic report.
