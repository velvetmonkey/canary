# Article 58 — [[Article 9]] periodic report disclosure in mandatory Annex V template

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] financial products
**What:** Present periodic sustainability information in the format of the template set out in Annex V, as an annex to the periodic report documents, with a prominent statement in the main body.
**Where:** In periodic reports (annex to documents referred to in [[Article 11(2)]] of [[SFDR]])
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For financial products that have sustainable investment as their objective, financial market participants shall present the information referred to in [[Article 11(1)]] of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 in an annex to the document or information referred to in [[Article 11(2)]] of that Regulation in the form of the template set out in Annex V to this Regulation.
