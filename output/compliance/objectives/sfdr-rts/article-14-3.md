# Article 14(3) — State at beginning of [[Article 8]] annex whether sustainable investments are intended

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] financial products
**What:** At the beginning of the pre-contractual annex, state: (a) whether the product intends to make any sustainable investments; and (b) that the product promotes E/S characteristics without having sustainable investment as its objective.
**Where:** At the beginning of the Annex II pre-contractual disclosure
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall provide at the beginning of the annex to the documents or the information referred to in [[Article 6(3)]] of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 all of the following information: (a) whether the financial product intends to make any sustainable investments; (b) whether the financial product promotes environmental or social characteristics, without having as its objective a sustainable investment.
