# [[Article 17(1)]]–(5) — Methodology for calculating degree of Taxonomy-aligned investments

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] or [[Article 9]] products with environmental investment commitments
**What:** Calculate the degree of investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities using the prescribed formula (market value of Taxonomy-aligned investments divided by market value of all investments), applying the specified methodology for equities, debt securities, real estate, infrastructure, securitisations and other financial products; exclude derivatives from the numerator; calculate a separate figure excluding sovereign exposures.
**Where:** Underlying calculation methodology for pre-contractual and periodic disclosures
**Deadline:** From 1 January 2023
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The degree to which investments are in environmentally sustainable economic activities shall be calculated in accordance with the following formula: where 'investments of the financial product in environmentally sustainable economic activities' shall be the sum of the market values of the following investments of the financial product.
