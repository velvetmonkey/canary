# Article 59 — Periodic report: attainment of sustainable investment objective with indicator performance

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] financial products
**What:** Report in the periodic Annex V template the extent to which the sustainable investment objective was attained, including performance of sustainability indicators, Taxonomy environmental objectives contributed to, [[Paris Agreement]] alignment (for carbon reduction products), DNSH explanation, PAI information, and a historical year-by-year comparison.
**Where:** In periodic reports (Annex V template), section 'To what extent was the sustainable investment objective of this financial product met?'
**Deadline:** Annually (in periodic reports from 1 January 2023)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'To what extent was the sustainable investment objective of this financial product met?' in the template set out in Annex V, financial market participants shall provide all of the following information: (a) the extent to which the sustainable investment objective was attained during the period covered by the periodic report.
