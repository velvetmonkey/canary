# Article 22 — Disclose DNSH and minimum safeguards for non-financial-product sustainable investment options

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available financial products with underlying investment options that have sustainable investment as their objective but are not themselves financial products
**What:** For such options, provide: a description of the sustainable investment objective; a list of indicators used to measure attainment; and a description of how investments do not significantly harm any sustainable investment objective (including PAI indicators and alignment with OECD/UN standards).
**Where:** In annexes to pre-contractual documents (Article 6(3) SFDR documents)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The information on the objective of the sustainable investments referred to in Article 20(3), point (c), and Article 21(3), point (b), shall contain all of the following: (a) a description of the sustainable investment objective; (b) a list of the indicators used to measure the attainment of that sustainable investment objective; (c) a description of how the investments do not significantly harm any of the sustainable investment objectives.
