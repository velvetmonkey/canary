# Article 52(1)–(2) — Top 15 investments disclosure in Article 8 periodic reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products
**What:** In the periodic report Annex IV template, list the 15 largest investments (by proportion) during the reporting period in descending order, including sector and country. If fewer than 15 investments account for 50% of the portfolio, list only those investments. This provides investors with transparency on the most significant holdings.
**Where:** In periodic reports (Annex IV template), section 'What were the top investments of this financial product?'
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The section 'What were the top investments of this financial product?' in the template set out in Annex IV to this Regulation shall contain a list, in descending order of size, of the fifteen investments constituting the largest proportion of investments of the financial product during the period covered by the periodic report, including the sector and countries in which those investments were made.
