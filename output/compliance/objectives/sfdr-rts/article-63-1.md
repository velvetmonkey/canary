# Article 63(1) — Compare Article 9 fund performance against designated benchmark and broad market index

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 9 financial products that use a designated reference benchmark
**What:** In the periodic report, explain how the designated index differs from a broad market index (including sustainability indicator performance), compare the fund's sustainability performance against the index, and compare against a broad market index.
**Where:** In the section 'How did this financial product perform compared to the reference sustainable benchmark?' in the Annex V periodic report template
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> financial market participants shall, for financial products that have sustainable investment as their objective and for which an index has been designated as a reference benchmark, provide all of the following information: (a) an explanation of how the index designated as a reference benchmark differs from a relevant broad market index.
