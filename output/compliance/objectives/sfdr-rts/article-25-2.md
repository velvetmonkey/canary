# Article 25(2) — Website summary section language requirements for [[Article 8]] products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] financial products
**What:** The website 'Summary' section must be provided in: (a) an official language of the home Member State (and an international finance language if different and the product is available in more than one Member State); and (b) an official language of any host Member State where the product is made available. Maximum length: two sides of A4.
**Where:** In the 'Summary' section of the website for [[Article 8]] products
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The website section 'Summary' referred to in [[Article 24]], point (a), shall be provided in at least the following languages: (a) one of the official languages of the home Member State and, where different and where the financial product is made available in more than one Member State, in an additional language customary in the sphere of international finance; (b) where the financial product is made available in a host Member State, one of the official languages of that host Member State.
