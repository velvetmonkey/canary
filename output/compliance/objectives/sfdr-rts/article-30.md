# Article 30 — Describe monitoring and control mechanisms for [[Article 8]] E/S characteristics

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] financial products
**What:** Describe how the E/S characteristics and sustainability indicators are monitored throughout the lifecycle of the financial product, including the internal or external control mechanisms in place.
**Where:** On the product website, in the 'Monitoring of environmental or social characteristics' section
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> financial market participants shall describe how the environmental or social characteristics promoted by the financial product and the sustainability indicators used to measure the attainment of each of those environmental or social characteristics promoted by the financial product are monitored throughout the lifecycle of the financial product and the related internal or external control mechanisms.

*Article 30, Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2022/1288 ([[SFDR]] RTS)* [verified]
