# [[Article 38(1)]]-(2) — [[Article 9]] website summary section: max 2 A4 pages, multilingual

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] financial products
**What:** The website 'Summary' section for [[Article 9]] products must summarise all other website sections, be a maximum of two A4 pages, and be provided in the home Member State official language (and international finance language if different) and in the official language of any host Member State where the product is offered.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website ([[Article 9]] product 'Summary' section)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The website section 'Summary' referred to in [[Article 37]], point (a), shall be provided in at least the following languages: (a) one of the official languages of the home Member State and, where different and where the financial product is made available in more than one Member State, in an additional language customary in the sphere of international finance; (b) where the financial product is made available in a host Member State, one of the official languages of that host Member State.
