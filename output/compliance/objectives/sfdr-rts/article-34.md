# Article 34 — Website: due diligence disclosure including internal and external controls (Article 8)

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products
**What:** In the website 'Due diligence' section, describe the due diligence carried out on the underlying assets of the financial product, including the internal and external controls on that due diligence.
**Where:** In the 'Due diligence' section of the website for Article 8 products
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the website section 'Due diligence' referred to in Article 24, point (j), financial market participants shall describe the due diligence carried out on the underlying assets of the financial product, including the internal and external controls on that due diligence.
