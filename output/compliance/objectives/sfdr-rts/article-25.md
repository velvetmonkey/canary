# Article 25 — Website Summary Section for Article 8 Products in Multiple Languages

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products
**What:** Publish a website summary section (max 2 A4 pages) covering all website disclosure sections for the Article 8 product, in the home Member State official language (plus international finance language if different) and in the official language of any host Member State where the product is distributed.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website (Article 8 product section, 'Summary' section)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the website section 'Summary' referred to in Article 24, point (a), financial market participants shall summarise all the information contained in the different sections referred to in that Article about the financial products that promote environmental or social characteristics. The summary section shall have a maximum length of two sides of A4-sized paper when printed.
