# Article 35 — Website: engagement policies disclosure for Article 8 products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 financial products where engagement is part of the investment strategy
**What:** In the website 'Engagement policies' section, describe the engagement policies implemented where engagement is part of the E/S investment strategy, including any management procedures applicable to sustainability-related controversies in investee companies.
**Where:** In the 'Engagement policies' section of the website for Article 8 products
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the website section 'Engagement policies' referred to in Article 24, point (k), financial market participants shall describe the engagement policies implemented where engagement is part of the environmental or social investment strategy, including any management procedures applicable to sustainability-related controversies in investee companies.
