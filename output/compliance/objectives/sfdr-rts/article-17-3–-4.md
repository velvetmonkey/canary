# Article 17(3)–(4) — Turnover as default KPI for Taxonomy alignment; CapEx/OpEx by exception

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available Article 8 or Article 9 funds investing in non-financial undertakings
**What:** Use turnover as the default key performance indicator (KPI) when calculating the degree of investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities for non-financial undertakings. Capital expenditure or operating expenditure may only be used where the features of the financial product justify it, and this choice must be explained in the pre-contractual disclosure.
**Where:** Underlying calculation methodology; disclosed in pre-contractual Annex II/III
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For the disclosures referred to in Article 15(1), point (a), and Article 19(1), point (a), in the case of investee companies that are non-financial undertakings that are subject to the obligation to publish non-financial information pursuant to Delegated Regulation (EU) 2021/2178 and other non-financial undertakings that are not subject to that obligation, the calculation referred to in paragraph 2 shall use turnover as the same type of key performance indicator for all non-financial undertakings.
