# [[Article 20(1)]] to (4) — Pre-Contractual Disclosures for Multi-Option Products ([[Article 8]] Options)

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering financial products with investment options where one or more options qualify as [[Article 8]] products
**What:** Insert a prominent statement in the main pre-contractual document confirming E/S characteristics promotion, the conditionality of meeting those characteristics on option selection, and where further information is available. Accompany with a categorised list of options and their proportions. Provide full Annex II template disclosures for each [[Article 8]] option and Annex III template disclosures for each [[Article 9]] option in annexes (or by reference where the number of annexes would be unwieldy).
**Where:** In the main body and annexes of pre-contractual documents
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> By way of derogation from Articles 14 to 17, where a financial product offers investment options to the investor and one or more of those investment options qualify that financial product as a financial product that promotes environmental or social characteristics, financial market participants shall insert in the main body of the document or information referred to in [[Article 6(3)]] of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 a prominent statement confirming all of the following: (a) that the financial product promotes environmental or social characteristics; (b) that those environmental or social characteristics will only be met where the financial product invests in at least one of the investment options mentioned in the list referred to in paragraph 2, point (a), of this Article.
