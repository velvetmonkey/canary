# [[Article 9(1)]]–(2) — PAI statement: references to international standards and [[Paris Agreement]] alignment

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants publishing a PAI statement
**What:** In the PAI statement 'References to international standards' section, describe adherence to responsible business conduct codes and internationally recognised due diligence standards, and degree of alignment with the [[Paris Agreement]]. Include: indicators used, methodology and data, whether a forward-looking climate scenario is used (and if so, its name and provider), or an explanation of why forward-looking scenarios are considered irrelevant.
**Where:** In the PAI statement on the website (Table 1, Annex I), section 'References to international standards'
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'References to international standards' in Table 1 of Annex I, financial market participants shall describe whether and to what extent they adhere to responsible business conduct codes and internationally recognised standards for due diligence and reporting and, where relevant, the degree of their alignment with the objectives of the [[Paris Agreement]].
