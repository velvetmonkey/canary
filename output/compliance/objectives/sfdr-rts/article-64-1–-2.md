# Article 64(1)–(2) — Five-year historical comparison in periodic reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants that have previously published periodic reports for the same financial product
**What:** In periodic reports, compare the current period with all previous periods covered by periodic reports, up to at least the last five previous periods. Report sustainability indicator performance consistently over time, including relative measures (e.g. impact per euro invested), auditor/third-party assurance status, and proportion of sustainability-related investments.
**Where:** In periodic reports (Annex IV and Annex V templates)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall, in the historical comparisons referred to in Article 51, point (c), Article 55(1), point (b)(vii), Article 59, point (d), and Article 62(1), point (b)(vii), compare the period covered by the periodic report with periods covered by previous periodic reports and, subsequently, with every previous period covered by a periodic report up to at least the last five previous periods.
