# Article 11(1)–(3) — Financial advisers: PAI consideration statement on website

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial advisers that consider PAI in their investment or insurance advice
**What:** Publish a statement on the website (in a dedicated section titled 'Statement on principal adverse impacts of investment advice on sustainability factors' or 'insurance advice' equivalent) describing: (a) how information from financial market participants is used; (b) whether and how financial products are ranked/selected based on PAI indicators; and (c) any PAI-based criteria or thresholds used to select or advise on financial products.
**Where:** On the financial adviser's website, in a separate dedicated section
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The statement and information referred to in paragraphs 1 and 2 shall contain details on the process used by financial advisers to select the financial products they advise on, including all of the following: (a) how the financial advisers use the information published by financial market participants pursuant to this Regulation; (b) whether the financial advisers rank and select financial products based on the indicators listed in Table 1 of Annex I and any additional indicators and, where applicable, a description of the ranking and selection methodology used; (c) any criteria or thresholds based on the principal adverse impacts listed in Table 1 of Annex I that are used to select, or advise on, financial products.
