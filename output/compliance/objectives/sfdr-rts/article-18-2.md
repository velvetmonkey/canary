# Article 18(2) — Include prominent statement in main body of [[Article 9]] pre-contractual documents

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] financial products
**What:** Include a prominent statement in the main body of the pre-contractual document directing investors to the annex where sustainable investment information can be found.
**Where:** In the main body of pre-contractual documents ([[Article 6(3)]] [[SFDR]] documents)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall include in the main body of the document or the information referred to in [[Article 6(3)]] of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 a prominent statement that information about sustainable investment is available in the annex.
