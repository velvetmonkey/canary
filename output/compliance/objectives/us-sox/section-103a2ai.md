# Section 103(a)(2)(A)(i) — Auditors Must Prepare and Retain Audit Work Papers for 7 Years

## Obligation

**Who:** Registered public accounting firms
**What:** PCAOB auditing standards must require each registered public accounting firm to prepare and maintain audit work papers and other information related to any audit report in sufficient detail to support the conclusions reached, for a period of not less than 7 years.
**Where:** Retained by the registered public accounting firm
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> prepare, and maintain for a period of not less than 7 years, audit work papers, and other information related to any audit report, in sufficient detail to support the conclusions reached in such report
