# Section 301 (Section 10A(m)(4)) — Audit Committee Whistleblower Complaint Procedures

## Obligation

**Who:** Audit committees of listed issuers
**What:** Each audit committee must establish procedures for: (A) the receipt, retention, and treatment of complaints received by the issuer regarding accounting, internal accounting controls, or auditing matters; and (B) the confidential, anonymous submission by employees of concerns regarding questionable accounting or auditing matters.
**Where:** Internal audit committee governance procedures
**Deadline:** 270 days after enactment for SEC to direct exchanges to implement
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Each audit committee shall establish procedures for the receipt, retention, and treatment of complaints received by the issuer regarding accounting, internal accounting controls, or auditing matters; and the confidential, anonymous submission by employees of the issuer of concerns regarding questionable accounting or auditing matters.
