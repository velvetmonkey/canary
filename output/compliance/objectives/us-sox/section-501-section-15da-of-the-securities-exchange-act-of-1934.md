# Section 501 (Section 15D(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934) — Structural Separation of Research Analysts from Investment Banking

## Obligation

**Who:** Registered brokers or dealers; registered securities associations; national securities exchanges
**What:** Rules must be adopted to: restrict prepublication clearance of research reports by investment banking personnel; limit supervision and compensation evaluation of analysts to non-investment banking officials; prohibit retaliation against analysts for unfavorable research reports; define quiet periods for research publication after underwriting; and establish informational partitions between research and investment banking.
**Where:** Internal firm governance and structural arrangements
**Deadline:** 1 year after enactment
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> rules reasonably designed to address conflicts of interest that can arise when securities analysts recommend equity securities in research reports and public appearances, in order to improve the objectivity of research and provide investors with more useful and reliable information
