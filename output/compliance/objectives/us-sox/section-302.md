# Section 302 — CEO/CFO Certification of Financial Reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Principal executive officers and principal financial officers of issuers filing periodic reports
**What:** The CEO and CFO must personally certify in each annual and quarterly report that: they have reviewed the report; it contains no material misstatements or omissions; financial statements fairly present the issuer's financial condition; they are responsible for internal controls; and they have disclosed all significant deficiencies, material weaknesses, and fraud to auditors and the audit committee.
**Where:** in each annual or quarterly report filed or submitted under section 13(a) or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934
**Materiality:** high
