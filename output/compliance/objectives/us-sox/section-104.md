# Section 104 — PCAOB Inspection Program for Registered Accounting Firms

## Obligation

**Who:** Registered public accounting firms (subject to inspection); PCAOB (obligation to conduct)
**What:** The PCAOB must conduct a continuing program of inspections of registered public accounting firms. Firms auditing more than 100 issuers must be inspected annually; firms auditing 100 or fewer issuers must be inspected at least once every 3 years. Inspections must review selected audit engagements, evaluate quality control systems, and identify violations. Written inspection reports must be transmitted to the SEC and made publicly available.
**Where:** At the offices of registered public accounting firms; reports filed with the SEC and made public
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The Board shall conduct a continuing program of inspections to assess the degree of compliance of each registered public accounting firm and associated persons of that firm with this Act, the rules of the Board, the rules of the Commission, or professional standards, in connection with its performance of audits, issuance of audit reports, and related matters involving issuers.
