# Section 202 (amending Section 10A(i) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934) — Audit Committee Pre-Approval of All Audit and Non-Audit Services

## Obligation

**Who:** Audit committees of issuers; registered public accounting firms
**What:** All auditing services and non-audit services provided by the issuer's auditor must be pre-approved by the audit committee, subject to a de minimis exception for non-audit services not exceeding 5% of total revenues paid to the auditor. Pre-approval of non-audit services must be disclosed to investors in periodic reports.
**Where:** Audit committee governance process; disclosed in periodic reports filed with the SEC
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> All auditing services (which may entail providing comfort letters in connection with securities underwritings or statutory audits required for insurance companies for purposes of State law) and non-audit services, other than as provided in subparagraph (B), provided to an issuer by the auditor of the issuer shall be preapproved by the audit committee of the issuer.
