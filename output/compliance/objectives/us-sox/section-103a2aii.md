# Section 103(a)(2)(A)(ii) — Concurring Partner Review and Approval of Audit Reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Registered public accounting firms
**What:** PCAOB auditing standards must require each registered public accounting firm to provide a concurring or second partner review and approval of each audit report (and related information), and concurring approval in its issuance, by a qualified person other than the person in charge of the audit, or by an independent reviewer.
**Where:** Internal quality control process within the registered public accounting firm
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> provide a concurring or second partner review and approval of such audit report (and other related information), and concurring approval in its issuance, by a qualified person (as prescribed by the Board) associated with the public accounting firm, other than the person in charge of the audit, or by an independent reviewer
