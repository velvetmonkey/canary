# Section 301 (amending Section 10A(m) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934) — Audit Committee Independence and Oversight Requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Issuers listed on national securities exchanges or associations; audit committee members
**What:** Audit committees must: (1) be directly responsible for appointment, compensation, and oversight of the external auditor; (2) consist solely of independent board members who accept no consulting fees from the issuer and are not affiliated persons; (3) establish procedures for receipt of complaints about accounting/auditing matters including anonymous employee submissions; (4) have authority to engage independent advisers; and (5) receive adequate funding from the issuer.
**Where:** Corporate governance structure; listing standards enforced by national securities exchanges
**Deadline:** 270 days after enactment for SEC to direct exchanges to implement
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The audit committee of each issuer, in its capacity as a committee of the board of directors, shall be directly responsible for the appointment, compensation, and oversight of the work of any registered public accounting firm employed by that issuer
