# Section 403 (amending Section 16(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934) — Accelerated Reporting of Insider Transactions (2-Business-Day Filing)

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors, officers, and beneficial owners of more than 10% of any registered equity security class
**What:** Changes in beneficial ownership must be reported electronically before the end of the second business day following the day on which the subject transaction was executed. The SEC must make each such statement publicly available on the internet by the end of the following business day, and issuers maintaining corporate websites must post the statement by the end of the business day following filing.
**Where:** Filed electronically with the SEC; posted on SEC website and issuer's corporate website
**Deadline:** Electronic filing and website posting requirements effective 1 year after enactment
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> if there has been a change in such ownership... before the end of the second business day following the day on which the subject transaction has been executed, or at such other time as the Commission shall establish, by rule, in any case in which the Commission determines that such 2-day period is not feasible.
