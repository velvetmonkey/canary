# Section 404 — Management Assessment of Internal Controls

## Obligation

**Who:** Issuers required to file annual reports; registered public accounting firms that prepare or issue the audit report
**What:** Each annual report must contain an internal control report stating management's responsibility for establishing and maintaining adequate internal controls and providing management's assessment of the effectiveness of those controls as of fiscal year-end. The external auditor must separately attest to and report on management's assessment.
**Where:** in each annual report required by section 13(a) or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934
**Materiality:** high
