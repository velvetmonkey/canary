# Section 401(a) (amending Section 13(i) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934) — Reflect All Material Correcting Adjustments in Financial Reports

## Obligation

**Who:** Issuers required to file financial reports prepared in accordance with GAAP with the SEC
**What:** Each financial report containing financial statements that is required to be prepared in accordance with (or reconciled to) GAAP and filed with the SEC must reflect all material correcting adjustments identified by a registered public accounting firm in accordance with GAAP and SEC rules.
**Where:** In financial reports filed with the SEC
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Each financial report that contains financial statements, and that is required to be prepared in accordance with (or reconciled to) generally accepted accounting principles under this title and filed with the Commission shall reflect all material correcting adjustments that have been identified by a registered public accounting firm
