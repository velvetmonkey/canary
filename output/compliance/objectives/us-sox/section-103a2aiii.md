# Section 103(a)(2)(A)(iii) — Auditor Reporting on Internal Control Testing in Audit Report

## Obligation

**Who:** Registered public accounting firms
**What:** PCAOB auditing standards must require each registered public accounting firm to describe in each audit report the scope of the auditor's testing of the issuer's internal control structure and procedures, and present findings from such testing, an evaluation of whether internal controls include adequate record-keeping and transaction authorization, and a description of any material weaknesses and material noncompliance found.
**Where:** In each audit report (or a separate report)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis
