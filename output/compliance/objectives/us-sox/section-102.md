# Section 102 — Mandatory Registration of Public Accounting Firms with PCAOB

## Obligation

**Who:** Any public accounting firm that prepares or issues audit reports for issuers
**What:** It is unlawful for any person that is not a registered public accounting firm to prepare, issue, or participate in the preparation or issuance of any audit report with respect to any issuer. Firms must register with the PCAOB and submit detailed applications including client lists, fees, quality control policies, personnel information, and disciplinary history.
**Where:** Registration with the PCAOB; applications made publicly available
**Deadline:** 180 days after the Commission's determination under [[Section 101(d)]]
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> it shall be unlawful for any person that is not a registered public accounting firm to prepare or issue, or to participate in the preparation or issuance of, any audit report with respect to any issuer.
