# Section 407 — Disclosure of Audit Committee Financial Expert

## Obligation

**Who:** Issuers required to file periodic reports under Sections 13(a) and 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934
**What:** Issuers must disclose in periodic reports whether their audit committee includes at least one member who is a 'financial expert' as defined by the SEC, and if not, the reasons why. The SEC must define 'financial expert' considering accounting/auditing education and experience.
**Where:** In periodic reports filed with the SEC
**Deadline:** Final rules within 180 days of enactment
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> require each issuer, together with periodic reports required pursuant to sections 13(a) and 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, to disclose whether or not, and if not, the reasons therefor, the audit committee of that issuer is comprised of at least 1 member who is a financial expert
