# Section 802 (18 U.S.C. § 1520) — Mandatory Retention of Audit Workpapers for 5 Years

## Obligation

**Who:** Accountants conducting audits of issuers subject to Section 10A(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934
**What:** Any accountant conducting an audit of a covered issuer must maintain all audit or review workpapers for a period of 5 years from the end of the fiscal period in which the audit or review was concluded. Knowing and willful violation is a criminal offense punishable by up to 10 years imprisonment.
**Where:** Retained by the accounting firm
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Any accountant who conducts an audit of an issuer of securities to which section 10A(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78j-1(a)) applies, shall maintain all audit or review workpapers for a period of 5 years from the end of the fiscal period in which the audit or review was concluded.
