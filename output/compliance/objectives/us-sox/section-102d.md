# Section 102(d) — Registered Accounting Firms Must File Annual Reports with PCAOB

## Obligation

**Who:** Registered public accounting firms
**What:** Each registered public accounting firm must submit an annual report to the PCAOB, and may be required to report more frequently, to update the information contained in its registration application and to provide such additional information as the PCAOB or SEC may specify.
**Where:** Filed with the PCAOB
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Each registered public accounting firm shall submit an annual report to the Board, and may be required to report more frequently, as necessary to update the information contained in its application for registration under this section
