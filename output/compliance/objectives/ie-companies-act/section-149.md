# Section 149 — Register of directors and secretaries

## Obligation

**Who:** All companies registered under the Act
**What:** Every company must maintain a register of its directors and secretaries containing prescribed particulars (name, address, nationality, other directorships, etc.) and must notify the Registrar of Companies of any changes within the required timeframe. The register must be available for inspection.
**Where:** At the company's registered office; changes filed with the Registrar of Companies
**Materiality:** medium
