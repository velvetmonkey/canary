# Section 167 — Audit committee requirement

## Obligation

**Who:** Companies that are public-interest entities or that otherwise fall within the audit committee requirement under the Act and related regulations
**What:** Qualifying companies must establish an audit committee with responsibility for monitoring the financial reporting process, the effectiveness of internal controls, and the statutory audit. The committee must include at least one member with competence in accounting or auditing.
**Where:** At board/governance level; terms of reference and composition to be documented
**Materiality:** medium
