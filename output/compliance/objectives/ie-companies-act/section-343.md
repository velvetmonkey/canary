# Section 343 — Obligation to make annual return to Registrar

## Obligation

**Who:** All companies registered under the Act
**What:** Every company must deliver an annual return to the Registrar of Companies by the annual return date, with all required documents annexed (including audited financial statements where applicable). Failure to file on time results in loss of audit exemption and potential strike-off.
**Where:** Filed with the Registrar of Companies (Companies Registration Office)
**Deadline:** Annual return date as determined under [[Section 345]]
**Materiality:** high
