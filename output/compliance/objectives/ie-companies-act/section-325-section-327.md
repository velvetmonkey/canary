# Section 325 / Section 327 — Obligation to prepare directors' report including business review

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors of all companies required to prepare statutory financial statements
**What:** Directors must prepare a directors' report for every financial year, including a business review containing a fair review of the company's business and a description of the principal risks and uncertainties it faces. This is the primary narrative reporting obligation under Irish company law.
**Where:** In the annual directors' report accompanying the statutory financial statements
**Materiality:** high
