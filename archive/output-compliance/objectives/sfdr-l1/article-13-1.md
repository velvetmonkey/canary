# Article 13(1) — Marketing communications must not contradict [[SFDR]] disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and financial advisers
**What:** Ensure that marketing communications do not contradict the sustainability-related information disclosed pursuant to [[SFDR]]. This is a prohibition on inconsistency between marketing materials and regulatory disclosures.
**Where:** In all marketing communications
**Deadline:** 10 March 2021
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Without prejudice to stricter sectoral legislation, in particular Directives 2009/65/EC, 2014/65/EU and (EU) 2016/97 and Regulation (EU) No 1286/2014, financial market participants and financial advisers shall ensure that their marketing communications do not contradict the information disclosed pursuant to this Regulation.
