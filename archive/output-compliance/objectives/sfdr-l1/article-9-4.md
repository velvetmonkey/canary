# Article 9(4) — Disclose index/benchmark methodology location for [[Article 9]] products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Include in pre-contractual disclosures an indication of where the methodology used for the calculation of the designated index and/or Paris-aligned benchmark (for [[Article 9]] products) can be found.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures per [[Article 6(1)]] and (3)
**Deadline:** 10 March 2021
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall include in the information to be disclosed pursuant to [[Article 6(1)]] and (3) an indication of where the methodology used for the calculation of the indices referred to in paragraph 1 of this Article and the benchmarks referred to in the second subparagraph of paragraph 3 of this Article are to be found.
