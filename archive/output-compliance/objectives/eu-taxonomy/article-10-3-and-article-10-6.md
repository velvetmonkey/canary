# [[Article 10(3)]] and [[Article 10(6)]] — Commission to adopt climate mitigation technical screening criteria by 31 December 2020

## Obligation

**Who:** Commission (obligation to adopt); financial market participants and undertakings (obligation to apply from 1 January 2022)
**What:** The Commission must adopt delegated acts establishing technical screening criteria for: (a) what constitutes substantial contribution to climate change mitigation; and (b) what constitutes significant harm to other environmental objectives for activities covered by (a). These criteria must be adopted by 31 December 2020 for application from 1 January 2022.
**Where:** Delegated acts; applied in investment analysis and disclosures
**Deadline:** 31 December 2020 (adoption); 1 January 2022 (application)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The Commission shall adopt the delegated act referred to in paragraph 3 by 31 December 2020, with a view to ensuring its application from 1 January 2022.
