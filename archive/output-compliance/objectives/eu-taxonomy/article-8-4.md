# Article 8(4) — Commission to adopt delegated act on Taxonomy KPI disclosure methodology

## Obligation

**Who:** European Commission
**What:** Adopt a delegated act specifying the content, presentation and methodology for the Taxonomy-related disclosures required from undertakings under Articles 8(1) and 8(2), taking into account the specificities of financial and non-financial undertakings and the applicable technical screening criteria.
**Where:** Delegated act (Official Journal)
**Deadline:** By 1 June 2021
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The Commission shall adopt a delegated act in accordance with [[Article 23]] to supplement paragraphs 1 and 2 of this Article to specify the content and presentation of the information to be disclosed pursuant to those paragraphs, including the methodology to be used in order to comply with them.
