# Article 20(6) — Financial market participants may flag non-covered activities to the Platform

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Where financial market participants consider that an economic activity not meeting (or not yet covered by) technical screening criteria should qualify as environmentally sustainable, they may inform the Platform on Sustainable Finance to assist the Commission in evaluating whether to update the criteria.
**Where:** Communication to the Platform on Sustainable Finance
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> Where financial market participants consider that an economic activity which does not comply with the technical screening criteria established pursuant to this Regulation, or for which such technical screening criteria have not yet been established, should qualify as environmentally sustainable, they may inform the Platform thereof.
