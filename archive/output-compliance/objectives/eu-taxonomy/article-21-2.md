# Article 21(2) — Competent authorities must cooperate and share information

## Obligation

**Who:** Competent authorities designated under [[SFDR]] [[Article 14(1)]]
**What:** Cooperate with each other and provide relevant information without undue delay for the purposes of carrying out their supervisory duties under this Regulation.
**Where:** Cross-border supervisory cooperation
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> For the purposes of this Regulation, the competent authorities shall cooperate with each other and shall provide each other, without undue delay, with such information as is relevant for the purposes of carrying out their duties under this Regulation.
