# Article 15(1) — Substantial contribution criteria for biodiversity and ecosystems

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and undertakings assessing taxonomy alignment for biodiversity objective
**What:** An economic activity contributes substantially to biodiversity and ecosystem protection/restoration if it contributes to: nature and biodiversity conservation (including favourable conservation status of habitats/species); sustainable land use and management; sustainable agricultural practices; sustainable forest management; or enabling such activities.
**Where:** Applied in investment analysis and taxonomy alignment assessments
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> An economic activity shall qualify as contributing substantially to the protection and restoration of biodiversity and ecosystems where that activity contributes substantially to protecting, conserving or restoring biodiversity or to achieving the good condition of ecosystems, or to protecting ecosystems that are already in good condition.
