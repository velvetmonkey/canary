# Article 19(3) — Prohibition: solid fossil fuel power generation cannot be Taxonomy-aligned

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and undertakings assessing Taxonomy alignment
**What:** Power generation activities that use solid fossil fuels must not be classified as environmentally sustainable economic activities under the Taxonomy, regardless of any other criteria being met.
**Where:** Applied in investment selection and Taxonomy alignment assessment processes
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The technical screening criteria referred to in paragraph 1 shall ensure that power generation activities that use solid fossil fuels do not qualify as environmentally sustainable economic activities.
