# [[Article 25]] (amending [[SFDR]] [[Article 9]], new paragraph 4a) — [[Article 9]] [[SFDR]] products: include Taxonomy info in pre-contractual disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] [[SFDR]] financial products
**What:** Include in the pre-contractual information disclosed under [[Article 6(1)]] and (3) of [[SFDR]] the Taxonomy-related information required under [[Article 5]] of the [[Taxonomy Regulation]] (environmental objectives contributed to and proportion of Taxonomy-aligned investments).
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures under [[SFDR]]
**Deadline:** From 1 January 2022 (objectives (a)-(b)); from 1 January 2023 (objectives (c)-(f))
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall include in the information to be disclosed pursuant to [[Article 6(1)]] and (3) of this Regulation the information required under [[Article 5]] of Regulation (EU) 2020/852.
