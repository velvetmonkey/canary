# Section 333 — Statutory financial statements must be audited

## Obligation

**Who:** All companies not availing of a statutory audit exemption
**What:** Companies must have their statutory financial statements audited by a qualified statutory auditor each financial year, unless they qualify for and validly claim an audit exemption. Asset managers running regulated funds will typically be ineligible for audit exemption.
**Where:** Annual statutory financial statements; auditor's report to be included in circulated financial statements
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Statutory financial statements must be audited (unless audit exemption availed of)
