# Section 225 — Directors' compliance statement obligation

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors of companies that meet the relevant size thresholds under the Act
**What:** Directors must prepare a compliance statement confirming that the company has appropriate policies and procedures in place to ensure material compliance with relevant obligations under company law and tax law, and that those policies and procedures have been reviewed during the financial year.
**Where:** In the directors' report accompanying the annual statutory financial statements
**Materiality:** high
