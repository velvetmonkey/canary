# Section 228 — Statement of principal fiduciary duties of directors

## Obligation

**Who:** Directors of all companies registered under the Act
**What:** Directors must act in good faith in what they consider to be the interests of the company, act honestly and responsibly in relation to the conduct of the company's affairs, act in accordance with the company's constitution, and avoid conflicts of interest. These are codified statutory fiduciary duties.
**Where:** In the conduct of all company affairs and board decision-making
**Materiality:** high
