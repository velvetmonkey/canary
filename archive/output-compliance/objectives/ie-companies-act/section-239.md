# Section 239 — Prohibition of loans to directors and connected persons

## Obligation

**Who:** All companies registered under the Act and their directors
**What:** Companies are prohibited from making loans, quasi-loans, or entering into credit transactions in favour of directors or persons connected with directors, except where specific statutory exceptions apply (e.g., Summary Approval Procedure, intra-group transactions, or minor value thresholds). Breach renders the transaction voidable and may result in personal liability.
**Where:** Applies to all company transactions and financial arrangements with directors
**Materiality:** medium
