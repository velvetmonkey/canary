# Regulation 3(i) — Widened SFO pre-investigation powers beyond bribery/corruption

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and regulated firms potentially subject to SFO investigation
**What:** The Serious Fraud Office's pre-investigation powers are now widened beyond bribery and corruption offences. Firms should review their legal exposure and ensure internal investigation and cooperation protocols are updated to reflect the broader SFO remit.
**Where:** In legal risk frameworks, internal investigation policies, and regulatory engagement procedures
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Regulation 3(i) brings into force amendments to the Criminal Justice Act 1987 (c. 38) which widen the pre-investigation powers of the Serious Fraud Office so they are not limited to certain bribery and corruption offences.
