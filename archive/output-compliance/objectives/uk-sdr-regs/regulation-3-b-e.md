# Regulation 3(b)-(e) — Identity verification requirements with national security exemptions

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and companies subject to [[Companies Act 2006]] identity verification requirements
**What:** New identity verification requirements under the [[Companies Act 2006]] come into force, alongside national security-related defences and exemptions. Firms must implement identity verification processes while accounting for applicable national security exceptions.
**Where:** In client onboarding, KYC procedures, and Companies House filings
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> [[Regulation 3(a)]] to (e) bring into force various provisions inserted into the [[Companies Act 2006]] (c. 46) by the Act which establish national security-related defences, exemptions and exceptions to new requirements concerning company names, identity verification requirements and false statement offences.
