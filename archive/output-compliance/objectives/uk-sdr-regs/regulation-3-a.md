# Regulation 3(a) — National security defences for company name requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Companies and financial market participants subject to [[Companies Act 2006]] company name rules
**What:** National security-related defences and exceptions to new company name requirements are now in force. Firms should review their corporate governance and naming policies to understand when these defences apply.
**Where:** In corporate governance documentation and Companies House filings
**Deadline:** 15 January 2024
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Regulation 3(a) to (e) bring into force various provisions inserted into the [[Companies Act 2006]] (c. 46) by the Act which establish national security-related defences, exemptions and exceptions to new requirements concerning company names, identity verification requirements and false statement offences.
