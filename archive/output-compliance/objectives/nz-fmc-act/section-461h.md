# Section 461H — Lodge financial statements and auditor's report with Registrar

## Obligation

**Who:** Every FMC reporting entity; every manager of a registered scheme
**What:** FMC reporting entities must deliver copies of their financial statements (or group financial statements) together with the auditor's report to the Registrar for lodgement within 4 months after balance date. Managers of registered schemes have the same obligation for scheme financial statements. Failure is an infringement offence carrying a fine up to NZD 50,000.
**Where:** Filed with the Registrar of Financial Service Providers
**Deadline:** Within 4 months after balance date
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Every FMC reporting entity must ensure that, within 4 months after the balance date of the entity, copies of the financial statements or group financial statements that are required to be prepared under any of sections 460, 461, and 461B, together with a copy of the auditor's report on those statements, are delivered to the Registrar for lodgement.
