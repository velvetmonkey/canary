# Section 446G — Financial Institutions Must Establish Effective Fair Conduct Programme

## Obligation

**Who:** Every financial institution (registered banks, licensed insurers, licensed NBDTs providing relevant services)
**What:** Every financial institution must establish, implement, and maintain an effective fair conduct programme — comprising policies, processes, systems, and controls designed to ensure compliance with the fair conduct principle (treating consumers fairly). The programme must comply with the minimum requirements in section 446J.
**Where:** Internally (governance and operational framework); summary must also be published on the institution's website (section 446H)
**Deadline:** 31 March 2025 (commencement date of subpart 6A)
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Every financial institution must establish, implement, and maintain an effective fair conduct programme.
