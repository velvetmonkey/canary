# [[Article 1(6)(a)]] – [[Article 54(2)(a)]] — Suitability assessment must include sustainability preferences

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** When assessing suitability, firms must ensure the recommended financial instrument meets the client's investment objectives including their risk tolerance AND any sustainability preferences expressed.
**Where:** In the suitability assessment process, pre-contractual and ongoing
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> it meets the investment objectives of the client in question, including the client's risk tolerance and any sustainability preferences;
