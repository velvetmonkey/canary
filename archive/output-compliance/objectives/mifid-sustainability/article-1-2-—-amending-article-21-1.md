# [[Article 1(2)]] — amending [[Article 21(1)]] — Integrate sustainability risks into organisational and operational requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms
**What:** Investment firms must take sustainability risks into account when complying with organisational requirements, including processes, systems and internal controls, proportionate to the nature, scale and complexity of the firm's business.
**Where:** Internal organisational arrangements, processes and systems
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Investment firms shall take into account sustainability risks when complying with the requirements set out in this paragraph.
