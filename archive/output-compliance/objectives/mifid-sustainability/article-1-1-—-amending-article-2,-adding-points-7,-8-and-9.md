# [[Article 1(1)]] — amending [[Article 2]], adding points (7), (8) and (9) — Apply defined terms for sustainability preferences, factors and risks

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms
**What:** Investment firms must apply the newly defined terms 'sustainability preferences', 'sustainability factors' and 'sustainability risks' consistently across their operations, as these definitions underpin all sustainability-related obligations in the amended Regulation.
**Where:** Across all relevant internal processes, disclosures and client-facing activities
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> 'sustainability preferences' means a client's or potential client's choice as to whether and, if so, to what extent, one or more of the following financial instruments shall be integrated into his or her investment
