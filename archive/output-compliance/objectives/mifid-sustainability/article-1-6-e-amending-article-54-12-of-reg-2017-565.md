# [[Article 1(6)(e)]] — amending [[Article 54(12)]] of Reg. 2017/565 — Suitability report must explain how recommendation meets sustainability preferences

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice to retail clients
**What:** The suitability report provided to retail clients must include an explanation of how the recommendation meets the client's sustainability preferences, in addition to other suitability criteria such as investment objectives, personal circumstances, knowledge, risk attitude and capacity for loss.
**Where:** In the written suitability report provided to retail clients
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> When providing investment advice, investment firms shall provide a report to the retail client that includes an outline of the advice given and that explains how the recommendation provided is suitable for the retail client, including how the recommendation meets the client's investment objectives, his or her personal circumstances with reference to the investment term required, the client's knowledge and experience, the client's attitude to risk his or her capacity to sustain losses and his or her sustainability preferences.
