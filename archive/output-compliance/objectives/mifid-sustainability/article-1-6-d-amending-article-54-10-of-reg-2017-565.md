# [[Article 1(6)(d)]] — amending [[Article 54(10)]] of Reg. 2017/565 — Prohibition on recommending instruments not meeting sustainability preferences

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** Investment firms must not recommend or decide to trade financial instruments as meeting a client's sustainability preferences when those instruments do not actually meet those preferences. They must explain the reasons to the client and keep records of those reasons. If the client decides to adapt their sustainability preferences, the firm must record that decision and the reasons for it.
**Where:** In investment advice and portfolio management processes; records kept internally
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> An investment firm shall not recommend financial instruments or decide to trade such instruments as meeting a client's or potential client's sustainability preferences when those financial instruments do not do meet those preferences. The investment firm shall explain to the client or potential clients the reasons for not doing so and keep records of those reasons.
