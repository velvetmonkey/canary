# [[Article 1(6)(a)]] — amending [[Article 54(2)(a)]] of Reg. 2017/565 — Suitability assessment must include client sustainability preferences

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** When assessing suitability, investment firms must ensure that a recommended financial instrument meets the client's investment objectives, including both their risk tolerance and any sustainability preferences expressed by the client.
**Where:** In the suitability assessment process, prior to making investment recommendations or portfolio management decisions
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> it meets the investment objectives of the client in question, including the client's risk tolerance and any sustainability preferences;
