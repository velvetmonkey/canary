# [[Article 1(1)]] – [[Article 2(7)]] — Apply the defined concept of 'sustainability preferences' consistently

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms
**What:** Firms must operationalise the regulatory definition of 'sustainability preferences', covering three categories: (a) Taxonomy-aligned investments, (b) [[SFDR]] sustainable investments, and (c) instruments considering principal adverse impacts — and apply this definition consistently across client interactions.
**Where:** Client onboarding, suitability processes, product selection, and disclosures
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high
