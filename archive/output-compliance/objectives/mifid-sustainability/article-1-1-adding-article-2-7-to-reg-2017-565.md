# [[Article 1(1)]] — adding [[Article 2(7)]] to Reg. 2017/565 — Apply defined concept of 'sustainability preferences' across all obligations

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms
**What:** Investment firms must apply the defined concept of 'sustainability preferences' — covering minimum proportions in Taxonomy-aligned investments, [[SFDR]] sustainable investments, and instruments considering principal adverse impacts — when implementing all sustainability-related obligations under the amended Regulation.
**Where:** Across all client-facing and internal processes where sustainability preferences are relevant
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> 'sustainability preferences' means a client's or potential client's choice as to whether and, if so, to what extent, one or more of the following financial instruments shall be integrated into his or her investment
