# [[Article 1(6)(d)]] – [[Article 54(10)]] — No recommendation of instruments mismatching sustainability preferences

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** Firms must not recommend or decide to trade financial instruments as meeting a client's sustainability preferences when those instruments do not actually meet those preferences. They must explain the reasons to the client and keep records of those reasons.
**Where:** In investment advice and portfolio management processes; records kept internally
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> An investment firm shall not recommend financial instruments or decide to trade such instruments as meeting a client's or potential client's sustainability preferences when those financial instruments do not do meet those preferences.
