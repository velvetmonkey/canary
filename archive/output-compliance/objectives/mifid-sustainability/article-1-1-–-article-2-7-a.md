# [[Article 1(1)]] – [[Article 2(7)(a)]] — Capture client minimum proportion for Taxonomy-aligned investments

## Obligation

**Who:** Investment firms providing investment advice or portfolio management
**What:** Firms must be able to capture and act upon a client's stated minimum proportion to be invested in environmentally sustainable investments as defined under the [[EU Taxonomy Regulation]] (EU) 2020/852.
**Where:** Client suitability questionnaires and portfolio management mandates
**Deadline:** 2 August 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> a financial instrument for which the client or potential client determines that a minimum proportion shall be invested in environmentally sustainable investments as defined in [[Article 2]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) 2020/852 of the European Parliament and of the Council;
