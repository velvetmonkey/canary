# Part 1, Chapter 2 (Sustainability disclosure requirements section) — Comply with new UK Sustainability Disclosure Requirements

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and other regulated firms subject to [[FCA]] rules under the new [[SDR]] framework
**What:** Firms must comply with sustainability disclosure requirements as introduced under the new domestic framework enabled by [[FSMA 2023]]. The [[FCA]] is empowered to make rules on sustainability disclosures to replace the revoked EU framework, and firms must implement those rules in their product disclosures and reporting.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures, periodic reports, websites, and marketing materials as specified by [[FCA]] rules
**Materiality:** high
