# Schedule 1, Part 1 — Benchmarks Regulation revoked; transition to UK BMR replacement

## Obligation

**Who:** Benchmark administrators, users of benchmarks, and investment managers referencing benchmarks in fund documentation
**What:** Regulation (EU) 2016/1011 ([[Benchmarks Regulation]]) is revoked as retained UK law. Asset managers referencing benchmarks (including ESG/climate benchmarks) in [[Article 8]]/9-equivalent fund documentation must transition to the [[FCA]]'s replacement UK benchmarks framework and update fund prospectuses and disclosures accordingly.
**Where:** Fund prospectuses, KIDs/KIIDs, investment management agreements, and index licensing arrangements
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Regulation (EU) 2016/1011 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 8 June 2016 on indices used as benchmarks in financial instruments and financial contracts or to measure the performance of investment funds
