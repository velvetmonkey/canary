# [[Article 6(1)]] and 6(3) — Pre-contractual disclosure of sustainability risk integration for all products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Must disclose in pre-contractual documents for all financial products how sustainability risks are integrated in the investment decision-making process and the likely impacts of sustainability risks on returns, in the manner prescribed by [[Article 6(3)]] (i.e. in the relevant sectoral pre-contractual document).
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures (prospectus, KIID, KID, or equivalent sectoral document)
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high
