# Article 13(1) — Marketing communications must not contradict regulatory disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Notwithstanding stricter sectoral legislation ([[UCITS]], [[MiFID II]], IDD, PRIIPs), financial market participants must ensure that their marketing communications do not contradict the information disclosed pursuant to the Regulation.
**Where:** Marketing communications
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> financial market participants shall ensure that their marketing communications do not contradict the information to be disclosed pursuant to this Regulation.
