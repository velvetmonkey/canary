# Article 18 — ESA biennial reporting on categorised product market

## Obligation

**Who:** European Supervisory Authorities (ESAs)
**What:** The ESAs must take stock of the extent of financial products categorised under Articles 7, 8 and 9, and submit a report on best practices to the Commission every two years (first report due 24 months after application date), which is also transmitted to the European Parliament and Council and made public.
**Where:** Report submitted to the Commission, European Parliament and Council
**Deadline:** 24 months after date of application
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> By [24 months after the date of application of this Regulation] and every two years thereafter, the ESAs shall submit a report to the Commission on best practices.
