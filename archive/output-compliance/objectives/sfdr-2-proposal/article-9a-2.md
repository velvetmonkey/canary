# Article 9a(2) — Disclosure requirements for products investing in categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Non-categorised financial products that invest in or are exposed to categorised products (Articles 7, 8, 9) must disclose: the relative share of underlying categorised products, the share not covered by categorised products, and the objective, strategy and exclusions applicable to the non-categorised share.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> For non-categorised financial products that claim that they invest in, are exposed to or are constituted of two or more underlying financial products as referred to in Articles 7, 8 and 9, the information to be disclosed pursuant to [[Article 6(3)]] shall include:
