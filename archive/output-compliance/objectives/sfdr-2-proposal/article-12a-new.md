# Article 12a (new) — Data use principles and investor information on data sources

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Financial market participants must follow defined principles for the use of data and estimates when complying with the Regulation. Upon investor request, they must provide information on the relevant data sources and assumptions underpinning estimates used in sustainability-related disclosures and product categorisation.
**Where:** Upon investor request; underlying all sustainability-related disclosures
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Addition of a new Article 12a setting out principles for financial market participants on the use of data and estimates for the purposes of this Regulation as well as rules on the information to be provided to investors upon request on relevant data sources and assumptions underpinning estimates.
