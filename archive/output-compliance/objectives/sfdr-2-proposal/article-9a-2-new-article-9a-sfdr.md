# Article 9a(2) (new Article 9a [[SFDR]]) — Disclosure requirements for non-categorised products investing in categorised products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants offering non-categorised products that invest in categorised products
**What:** Non-categorised products that invest in two or more categorised products must disclose: (a) the relative share of underlying categorised products (Articles 7, 8, 9); (b) the share of investments not in categorised products; and (c) the objective, strategy and exclusions applicable to the non-categorised portion. These products may include sustainability-related claims in marketing communications (but not names) if claims are clear, fair and not misleading.
**Where:** In pre-contractual disclosures (manner laid down in [[Article 6(3)]]) and marketing communications
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> For non-categorised financial products that claim that they invest in, are exposed to or are constituted of two or more underlying financial products as referred to in Articles 7, 8 and 9, the information to be disclosed pursuant to [[Article 6(3)]] shall include: (a) the composition of the financial product in terms of the relative share of the underlying financial products referred to in Articles 7, 8 and 9
