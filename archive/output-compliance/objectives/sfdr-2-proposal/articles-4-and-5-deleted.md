# Articles 4 and 5 (deleted) — Deletion of entity-level PAI and remuneration disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** The existing obligations to publish entity-level principal adverse impact statements ([[Article 4]] [[SFDR]]) and remuneration policy disclosures ([[Article 5]] [[SFDR]]) are deleted. Financial market participants must update their compliance frameworks to remove these entity-level disclosures and ensure no duplication with [[CSRD]] obligations. This represents a significant reduction in recurring compliance costs (estimated EUR 56 million annually).
**Where:** Websites and entity-level disclosures (obligation removed)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Deletion of entity-level disclosure requirements in Articles 4 and 5 regarding, respectively, principal adverse impacts and remuneration policies, also consistent with the de-prioritisation list.
