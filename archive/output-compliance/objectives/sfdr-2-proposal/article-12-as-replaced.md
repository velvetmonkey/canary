# [[Article 12]] (as replaced) — Keep website disclosures up to date and explain amendments

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Financial market participants must ensure that information published on their websites under Articles 3 and 10 is kept up to date. When amending such information, they must explain the amendment on the same website where the information was published.
**Where:** On websites
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall ensure that any information published in accordance with [[Article 3]] or 10 is kept up to date. A financial market participant that amends such information shall explain such amendment on the same website as the website on which the information has been published.
