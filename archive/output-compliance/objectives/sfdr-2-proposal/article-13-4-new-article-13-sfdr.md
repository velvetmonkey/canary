# [[Article 13(4)]] (new [[Article 13]] [[SFDR]]) — Prohibition on use of 'impact' in product names for non-impact products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** The term 'impact' may not be used in the name of any financial product unless it is a sustainability-related financial product with impact as defined in [[Article 2(26)]] (i.e. a product categorised under [[Article 7]] or 9 that has as its objective the generation of a pre-defined, positive and measurable social or environmental impact).
**Where:** In product names
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial products other than those referred to in [[Article 2]], point (26), shall not use the term 'impact' in their name.
