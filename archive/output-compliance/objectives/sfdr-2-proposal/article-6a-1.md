# Article 6a(1) — Restrictions on sustainability information in non-categorised product disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Non-categorised products may include sustainability factor information in pre-contractual documents only if: it is not a central element of the disclosure (less than 10% of investment strategy presentation volume, secondary in breadth and positioning), not included in the KIID/KID, and does not constitute sustainability-related claims reserved for categorised products.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures
**Materiality:** high
