# [[Article 13(5)]] (new [[Article 13]] [[SFDR]]) — ESG rating disclosure in marketing communications and on websites

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants that include ESG ratings in marketing communications
**What:** Where a financial market participant issues and discloses an ESG rating (as defined in Regulation (EU) 2024/3005) in marketing communications, it must publish on its website the information required by Annex III, point 1, of that Regulation and include a link to those website disclosures in the marketing communications.
**Where:** On the financial market participant's website and in marketing communications
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Where a financial market participant issues and discloses to third parties an ESG rating, as defined in [[Article 3]], point (1), of Regulation (EU) 2024/3005 of the European Parliament and of the Council, as part of its marketing communications, it shall include on its website the same information as that required by point 1 of Annex III to that Regulation and it shall disclose in those marketing communications a link to those website disclosures.
