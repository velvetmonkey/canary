# [[Article 18]] (as replaced) — ESA biennial reporting on categorised product market and best practices

## Obligation

**Who:** European Supervisory Authorities (ESAs)
**What:** The ESAs must take stock of the extent of financial products categorised under Articles 7, 8 and 9 made available or managed by financial market participants. By 24 months after the date of application and every two years thereafter, the ESAs must submit a public report to the Commission on best practices, transmitted also to the European Parliament and Council.
**Where:** Report to the Commission, European Parliament and Council
**Deadline:** 24 months after date of application, then every 2 years
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> The ESAs shall take stock of the extent of financial products referred to in Articles 7, 8 and 9 made available or managed by financial market participants. By [24 months after the date of application of this Regulation] and every two years thereafter, the ESAs shall submit a report to the Commission on best practices. That report shall be made public and be transmitted to the European Parliament and to the Council.
