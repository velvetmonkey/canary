# Article 7(3)(d) — Transition category: disclose [[EU Taxonomy]] use for environmental objectives

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Where a Transition category product pursues an environmental objective, disclose whether and to what extent the 70% investment requirement is met by investing in taxonomy-aligned economic activities.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures, websites, periodic reports
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> where the product pursues an environmental objective, a statement on whether, and the extent to which, the financial market participant meets the requirement referred to in paragraph 1, first subparagraph, point (a), by investing in accordance with paragraph 2, first subparagraph, point (b).
