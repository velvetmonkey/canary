# Article 8(1) — ESG Basics category: 70% threshold and exclusions

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** To use the 'ESG Basics' category label, a financial product must allocate at least 70% of investments integrating sustainability factors beyond sustainability risk consideration, and apply mandatory exclusions ([[Article 12(1)(a)]]-(d) of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2020/1818).
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures, on websites, in periodic reports
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall not claim that their financial products, other than those referred to in Articles 7 and 9, integrate sustainability factors in their investment strategy beyond the consideration of sustainability risks, unless those financial products meet the following conditions:
