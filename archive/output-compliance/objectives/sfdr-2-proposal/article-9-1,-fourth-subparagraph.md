# [[Article 9(1)]], fourth subparagraph — 15% Taxonomy-aligned investment threshold satisfies Sustainable category contribution criterion

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** The 70% contribution criterion of the Sustainable category is deemed met if the financial product has at least 15% of investments in taxonomy-aligned economic activities. The remaining portfolio must still comply with applicable exclusions.
**Where:** In product design and categorisation
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The conditions described under the first subparagraph, point (a) shall be considered met for financial products with a proportion of investments in taxonomy-aligned economic activities, as defined in [[Article 1]], point (2), of Delegated Regulation (EU) 2021/2178, equal or higher than 15%.
