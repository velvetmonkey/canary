# [[Article 6(1)]] (as amended) — Pre-contractual disclosure of sustainability risk integration for all products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** For all financial products (categorised and non-categorised), disclose in pre-contractual documents how sustainability risks are integrated in the investment decision-making process and the results of the assessment of the likely impacts of sustainability risks on the returns of the financial product.
**Where:** in pre-contractual disclosures
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The information referred to in paragraph 1 shall be disclosed in the following manner:
