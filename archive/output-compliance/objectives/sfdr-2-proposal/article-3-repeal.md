# [[Article 3]] (Repeal) — Repeal of Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2022/1288

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants
**What:** Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2022/1288 (the current [[SFDR]] RTS on disclosures) is repealed as of the date of application of the revised Regulation. Financial market participants must cease using the existing disclosure templates and transition to the new framework.
**Where:** Across all regulated disclosures
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Commission Delegated Regulation (EU) 2022/1288 of 6 April 2022 is repealed.
