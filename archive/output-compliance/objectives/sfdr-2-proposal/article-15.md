# Article 15 — IORPs: publish sustainability risk and product information per IORP Directive

## Obligation

**Who:** Institutions for Occupational Retirement Provision (IORPs)
**What:** IORPs must publish and maintain the information required under Articles 3 (sustainability risk policies), 6 (pre-contractual sustainability risk disclosures) and 10 (website disclosures for categorised products) in accordance with [[Article 36(2)(f)]] of Directive (EU) 2016/2341.
**Where:** On websites and in pre-contractual documents, per IORP Directive requirements
**Deadline:** 18 months after entry into force
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> IORPs shall publish and maintain the information referred to in Articles 3, 6 and 10 of this Regulation in accordance with [[Article 36(2)]], point (f), of Directive (EU) 2016/2341.
