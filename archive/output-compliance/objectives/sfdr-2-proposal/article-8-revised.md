# [[Article 8]] (revised) — ESG Basics category: criteria and disclosure obligations

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants manufacturing, managing or making available ESG Basics financial products
**What:** Financial products claiming to integrate sustainability factors beyond sustainability risk management must qualify under the ESG Basics category by meeting defined criteria (e.g. ESG rating outperformance, combination of sustainable/transition standards, positive track record on sustainability factors), apply mandatory exclusions (prohibited weapons, tobacco, UNGC/OECD violations, hard coal and lignite), and disclose the strategy, investment approach, indicators and data sources to investors in pre-contractual and periodic documents.
**Where:** Pre-contractual disclosures, periodic reports, and websites
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The ESG basics category should consist of financial products the strategy or design of which is based on selected sustainability factors. [...] The criteria should cater for investments which do not specifically pursue a sustainability or transition related objective but integrate sustainability factors in their investment strategies through credible sustainability-related approaches.
