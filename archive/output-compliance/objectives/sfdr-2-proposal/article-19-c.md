# Article 19(c) — Review of 15% Taxonomy alignment threshold after 36 months

## Obligation

**Who:** European Commission
**What:** The Commission must review the application of the Regulation within 36 months of its date of application, specifically considering whether to adjust the 15% proportion of taxonomy-aligned investments needed to comply with the safe harbour option under Articles 7(1) and 9(1), to reflect developments in the [[EU Taxonomy]] and the broader economy.
**Where:** Commission review report
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> By [36 months after the date of application of this Regulation], the Commission shall review the application of this Regulation and shall in particular consider:
(a)practices regarding the categorisation of products in accordance with Articles 7, 8 and 9;
(b)practices regarding the use of data and estimates pursuant to Article 12a and whether these practices and the functioning of this Regulation are inhibited by data availability or quality issues;
(c)adjusting the proportion of investments in taxonomy-aligned economic activities needed to comply with the option under [[Article 7(1)]], third subparagraph, and [[Article 9(1)]], third subparagraph.
