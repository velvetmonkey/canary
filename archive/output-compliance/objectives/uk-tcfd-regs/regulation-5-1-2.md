# Regulation 5(1)–(2) — Secretary of State to review and report on Regulations by April 2027

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State (government obligation; firms should be aware for anticipating regulatory change)
**What:** The Secretary of State must periodically review the Regulations and publish a report, with the first report due before 6 April 2027 and subsequent reports at intervals not exceeding five years. Asset managers should monitor these reviews as they may trigger amendments to disclosure requirements.
**Where:** Published review report (public document)
**Deadline:** 6 April 2027 (first review)
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> The first report must be published before 6th April 2027.
