# Part 2 – Committee on Climate Change (Directions) — Secretary of State must consult devolved authorities before issuing Committee directions

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State
**What:** Before giving directions to the Committee on Climate Change regarding the exercise of its functions, the Secretary of State must consult the other national authorities. Where the request comes from two or more national authorities, directions must be issued jointly.
**Where:** In inter-governmental consultation processes prior to issuing directions
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> Before giving directions under any of paragraphs (a) to (f), the Secretary of State must consult the other national authorities.
