# Schedule 3, Part 1 – Trading Schemes Regulations: Parliamentary Procedure — Single-authority trading scheme regulations require affirmative parliamentary resolution

## Obligation

**Who:** National authorities (Secretary of State, devolved administrations) making trading scheme regulations
**What:** Trading scheme regulations made by a single national authority must not be made unless a draft of the statutory instrument has been laid before and approved by a resolution of each House of Parliament (or the relevant devolved legislature). This affirmative procedure must be followed before any scheme takes effect.
**Where:** In the parliamentary/legislative procedure for making trading scheme regulations
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> the regulations must not be made unless a draft of the statutory instrument containing them has been laid before and approved by a resolution of each House of Parliament.
