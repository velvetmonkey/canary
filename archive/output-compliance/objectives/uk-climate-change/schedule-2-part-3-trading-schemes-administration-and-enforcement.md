# Schedule 2, Part 3 – Trading Schemes: Administration and Enforcement — Trading scheme regulations must cap pre-commencement imprisonment terms at six months

## Obligation

**Who:** National authorities making trading scheme regulations
**What:** Where trading scheme regulations create criminal offences, they must provide that offences committed before a specified date are punishable with imprisonment for a term not exceeding six months. Regulators must ensure enforcement provisions comply with this statutory cap.
**Where:** In trading scheme regulations and enforcement provisions
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> must provide that, where the offence is committed before that date, it is punishable with imprisonment for a term not exceeding six months.
