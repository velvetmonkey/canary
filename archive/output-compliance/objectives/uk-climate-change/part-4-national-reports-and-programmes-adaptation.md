# Part 4 – National Reports and Programmes (Adaptation) — Adaptation programmes must address risks from most recent climate change report

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State and relevant national authorities
**What:** National adaptation programmes must specifically address the risks identified in the most recent statutory climate change risk assessment report. This ensures adaptation policy remains current and evidence-based.
**Where:** In national adaptation reports and programmes published by government
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> addressing the risks identified in the most recent report under section 56.
