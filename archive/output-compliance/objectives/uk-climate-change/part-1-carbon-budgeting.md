# Part 1 – Carbon Budgeting — Secretary of State must prioritise UK domestic climate action

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State
**What:** When setting proposals and policies for meeting carbon budgets, the Secretary of State must have regard to the need for UK domestic action on climate change, ensuring domestic measures are central to compliance strategies rather than relying solely on international offsets.
**Where:** In proposals and policies for meeting carbon budgets submitted to Parliament
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> the Secretary of State must have regard to the need for UK domestic action on climate change.
