# Schedule 4 — Producer Responsibility Obligations (Section 50) — Producers must meet recycling and recovery obligations

## Obligation

**Who:** Producers, importers and compliance scheme operators for specified products and packaging
**What:** Producers of specified products (including packaging) must join a compliance scheme or individually meet statutory recycling and recovery targets. They must register, report data and pay fees. Asset managers with investee companies in consumer goods, packaging or retail must assess exposure to extended producer responsibility costs and compliance obligations.
**Where:** Registration with and reporting to the relevant national authority or compliance scheme; records maintained on-site
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> "operator", in relation to a compliance scheme, includes any person responsible for establishing, maintaining or managing the scheme
