# Part 6 — Biodiversity Objective and Reporting (Section 102) — Biodiversity reporting obligations on public and regulated bodies

## Obligation

**Who:** Public authorities and regulated bodies in England with biodiversity duties
**What:** Public authorities must report on actions taken to conserve and enhance biodiversity and to comply with the biodiversity duty. Reports must be published at intervals specified in regulations. Asset managers should monitor investee companies' biodiversity reporting as part of nature-related due diligence aligned with [[TNFD]]/[[SFDR]] [[Article 8]]/9 principal adverse impact indicators.
**Where:** Published reports; submitted to the Secretary of State as specified in regulations
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> In this subsection "specified" means specified in the regulations.
