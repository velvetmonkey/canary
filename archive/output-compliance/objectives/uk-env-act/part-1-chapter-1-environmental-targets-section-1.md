# Part 1, Chapter 1 — Environmental Targets (Section 1) — Legally binding environmental targets must be set

## Obligation

**Who:** Secretary of State; indirectly, public authorities and regulated entities subject to those targets
**What:** The Secretary of State must set long-term, legally binding environmental targets covering areas such as air quality, water, biodiversity and resource efficiency. Targets must be laid before Parliament by 31 October 2022. Compliance with these targets drives obligations on public bodies and regulated industries that asset managers must monitor in investee companies.
**Where:** Statutory instrument laid before Parliament; targets apply across England
**Deadline:** 31 October 2022
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> must be laid before Parliament on or before 31 October 2022.
