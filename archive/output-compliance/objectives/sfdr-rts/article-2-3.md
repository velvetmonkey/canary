# Article 2(3) — Keep website disclosures up to date with publication and update dates

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and financial advisers
**What:** Keep website information up to date, clearly mention the date of publication and any update, and indicate the version history in the file name for downloadable files.
**Where:** On websites
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants and financial advisers shall keep the information published on their websites in accordance with this Regulation up to date. They shall clearly mention the date of publication of the information and the date of any update.
