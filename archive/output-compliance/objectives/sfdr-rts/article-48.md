# Article 48 — Website: engagement policies disclosure for [[Article 9]] products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] financial products where engagement is part of the sustainable investment objective
**What:** In the website 'Engagement policies' section, describe the engagement policies implemented where engagement is part of the sustainable investment objective, including any management procedures applicable to sustainability-related controversies in investee companies.
**Where:** In the 'Engagement policies' section of the website for [[Article 9]] products
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the website section 'Engagement policies' referred to in [[Article 37]], point (k), financial market participants shall describe the engagement policies implemented where engagement is part of the sustainable investment objective, including any management procedures applicable to sustainability-related controversies in investee companies.
