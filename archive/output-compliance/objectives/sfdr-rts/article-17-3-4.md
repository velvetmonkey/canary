# [[Article 17(3)]]-(4) — Turnover as Default KPI for Taxonomy Alignment; CapEx/OpEx Exception

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] or [[Article 9]] products with environmental characteristics/objectives
**What:** Use turnover as the default key performance indicator when calculating the degree of investments in environmentally sustainable economic activities for non-financial undertakings. Capital expenditure or operating expenditure may only be used where the features of the financial product justify it and this is explained. For financial undertakings, use the KPIs specified in Delegated Regulation (EU) 2021/2178.
**Where:** Underlying methodology for pre-contractual and periodic disclosures
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> In the case of investee companies that are non-financial undertakings that are subject to the obligation to publish non-financial information pursuant to Delegated Regulation (EU) 2021/2178 and other non-financial undertakings that are not subject to that obligation, the calculation referred to in paragraph 2 shall use turnover as the same type of key performance indicator for all non-financial undertakings. By way of derogation from paragraph 3, where, due to the features of the financial product, capital expenditure or operating expenditure gives a more representative calculation of the degree to which an investment is into environmentally sustainable economic activities, the calculation may use the most appropriate of those two key performance indicators.
