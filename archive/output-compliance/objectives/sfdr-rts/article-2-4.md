# Article 2(4) — Include LEIs and ISINs when referring to entities or products

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and financial advisers
**What:** Where available, provide legal entity identifiers (LEIs) and international securities identification numbers (ISINs) when referring to entities or financial products in any required disclosure.
**Where:** All disclosure channels
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants and financial advisers shall provide, where available, legal entity identifiers (LEIs) and international securities identification numbers (ISINs) when referring to entities or financial products in the information provided in accordance with this Regulation.
