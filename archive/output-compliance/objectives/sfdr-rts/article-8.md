# Article 8 — Disclose Engagement Policies in PAI Statement

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants publishing a PAI statement
**What:** Include in the PAI statement brief summaries of engagement policies (including shareholder engagement under Directive 2007/36/EC and any other engagement policies), describing which PAI indicators are addressed and how policies will be adapted if PAIs are not reduced over more than one period.
**Where:** In the PAI statement on the website (Table 1 of Annex I, Engagement policies section)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> In the section 'Engagement policies' in Table 1 of Annex I, financial market participants shall provide all of the following information: (a) where applicable, brief summaries of the engagement policies referred to in Article 3g of Directive 2007/36/EC of the European Parliament and of the Council; (b) brief summaries of any other engagement policies to reduce principal adverse impacts.
