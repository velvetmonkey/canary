# Article 28 — Disclose investment strategy and good governance policy on [[Article 8]] website

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 8]] financial products
**What:** In the website 'Investment strategy' section, describe the investment strategy used to meet the E/S characteristics AND the policy to assess good governance practices of investee companies (covering sound management structures, employee relations, remuneration and tax compliance).
**Where:** On the product website, in the 'Investment strategy' section of the 'Sustainability-related disclosures'
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> In the website section 'Investment strategy' referred to in [[Article 24]], point (d), financial market participants shall describe all of the following: (a) the investment strategy used to meet the environmental or social characteristics promoted by the financial product; (b) the policy to assess good governance practices of the investee companies, including with respect to sound management structures, employee relations, remuneration of staff and tax compliance.
