# Article 2(2) — Provide all disclosures in searchable electronic format

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants and financial advisers
**What:** Provide all required information in a searchable electronic format, unless sectoral legislation requires otherwise.
**Where:** All disclosure channels
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants and financial advisers shall provide the information required by this Regulation in a searchable electronic format, unless otherwise required by the sectoral legislation referred to in [[Article 6(3)]] and [[Article 11(2)]] of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088.
