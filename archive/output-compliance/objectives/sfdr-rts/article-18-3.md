# Article 18(3) — State at beginning of [[Article 9]] annex that product has sustainable investment objective

## Obligation

**Who:** Financial market participants making available [[Article 9]] financial products
**What:** At the beginning of the pre-contractual annex, include a statement that the financial product has sustainable investment as its objective.
**Where:** At the beginning of the Annex III pre-contractual disclosure
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Financial market participants shall include at the beginning of the annex to the document or the information referred to in [[Article 6(3)]] of Regulation (EU) 2019/2088 a statement that the financial product has sustainable investment as its objective.
