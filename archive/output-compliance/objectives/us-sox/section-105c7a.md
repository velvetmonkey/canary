# Section 105(c)(7)(A) — Prohibition on Association with Suspended/Barred Persons

## Obligation

**Who:** Registered public accounting firms; persons suspended or barred from association with a registered public accounting firm
**What:** It is unlawful for any person suspended or barred from being associated with a registered public accounting firm to willfully become or remain associated with any registered public accounting firm. It is also unlawful for any registered public accounting firm that knew or should have known of the suspension or bar to permit such an association, without consent of the PCAOB or SEC.
**Where:** Applies to all employment and association arrangements
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> It shall be unlawful for any person that is suspended or barred from being associated with a registered public accounting firm under this subsection willfully to become or remain associated with any registered public accounting firm, or for any registered public accounting firm that knew, or, in the exercise of reasonable care should have known, of the suspension or bar, to permit such an association
