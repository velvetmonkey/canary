# Section 404(a) — Management Assessment of Internal Controls Annual Report

## Obligation

**Who:** Issuers required to file annual reports under [[Section 13(a)]] or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934
**What:** Each annual report must contain an internal control report that: (1) states management's responsibility for establishing and maintaining adequate internal control structure and procedures for financial reporting; and (2) contains management's assessment of the effectiveness of those internal controls as of the end of the most recent fiscal year.
**Where:** In annual reports filed with the SEC
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> The Commission shall prescribe rules requiring each annual report required by section 13(a) or 15(d) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78m or 78o(d)) to contain an internal control report, which shall--
            (1) state the responsibility of management for establishing and maintaining an adequate internal control structure and procedures for financial reporting
