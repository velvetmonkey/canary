# Section 108 (amending Section 19(b) of the Securities Act of 1933) — Accounting Standard-Setting Body Annual Report

## Obligation

**Who:** The designated accounting standard-setting body (FASB)
**What:** The designated accounting standard-setting body must submit an annual report to the SEC and the public, containing audited financial statements of that standard-setting body.
**Where:** Submitted to the SEC and made public
**Deadline:** Annual
**Materiality:** low

## Legal Basis

> A standard setting body described in paragraph (1) shall submit an annual report to the Commission and the public, containing audited financial statements of that standard setting body.
