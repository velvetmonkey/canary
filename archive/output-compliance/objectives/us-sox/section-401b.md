# Section 401(b) — Pro Forma Financial Information Must Not Be Misleading

## Obligation

**Who:** Issuers filing periodic or other reports with the SEC or making public disclosures or press releases containing pro forma financial information
**What:** Pro forma financial information included in any periodic or other report filed with the SEC, or in any public disclosure or press release, must not contain any untrue statement of a material fact or omit a material fact necessary to prevent it from being misleading, and must be reconciled with the issuer's financial condition and results of operations under GAAP.
**Where:** In periodic or other reports filed with the SEC, or in any public disclosure or press or other release
**Materiality:** medium
