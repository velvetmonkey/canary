# Section 106 — Foreign Public Accounting Firms Subject to PCAOB Oversight

## Obligation

**Who:** Foreign public accounting firms that prepare or furnish audit reports with respect to any issuer
**What:** Any foreign public accounting firm that prepares or furnishes an audit report for an issuer is subject to SOX and PCAOB/SEC rules to the same extent as a US public accounting firm. Such firms are deemed to have consented to produce audit workpapers for the PCAOB or SEC in connection with any investigation. Domestic registered firms relying on foreign firm opinions are deemed to have consented to supply those foreign firms' workpapers.
**Where:** Applies to the foreign firm's engagement and workpaper retention
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> Any foreign public accounting firm that prepares or furnishes an audit report with respect to any issuer, shall be subject to this Act and the rules of the Board and the Commission issued under this Act, in the same manner and to the same extent as a public accounting firm that is organized and operates under the laws of the United States or any State
