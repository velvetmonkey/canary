# Section 306(a)(6) — Issuer Notice of Blackout Period to Directors, Officers, and SEC

## Obligation

**Who:** Issuers of equity securities subject to pension fund blackout periods
**What:** When a director or executive officer is subject to the blackout period trading prohibition, the issuer must timely notify such director or officer and the SEC of the blackout period.
**Where:** Notice to affected directors/officers and filed with the SEC
**Deadline:** 180 days after enactment
**Materiality:** high

## Legal Basis

> the issuer of such equity securities shall timely notify such director or officer and the Securities and Exchange Commission of such blackout period.
