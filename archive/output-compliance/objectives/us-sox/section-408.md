# Section 408 — Regular and Systematic SEC Review of Issuer Periodic Disclosures

## Obligation

**Who:** The SEC (obligation on the regulator); issuers subject to review
**What:** The SEC must review disclosures made by listed issuers reporting under [[Section 13(a)]] on a regular and systematic basis, including a review of financial statements. No issuer required to file under [[Section 13(a)]] or 15(d) shall be reviewed less frequently than once every 3 years. Review criteria include material restatements, stock price volatility, market capitalization, and economic significance.
**Where:** SEC review of periodic reports (Form 10-K and others)
**Materiality:** medium

## Legal Basis

> The Commission shall review disclosures made by issuers reporting under section 13(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (including reports filed on Form 10-K)... on a regular and systematic basis for the protection of investors.
